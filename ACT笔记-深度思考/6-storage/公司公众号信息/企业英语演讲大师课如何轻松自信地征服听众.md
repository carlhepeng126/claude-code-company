**How to Deliver Effective Presentations?**

Delivering presentations is an everyday art form that anyone can master. To
capture your audience's attention, present your information with ease and
confidence. Act as if you are in a conversation with your audience, and they
will pay attention to you. To get this level of fluency, write an engaging
narrative, use more visuals than text in your slides, and practice, practice,
practice.  
译文：进行演讲是一种日常艺术，任何人都可以掌握。要吸引观众的注意力，您需要轻松自如地展示信息，充满自信。表现得像是在与观众对话一样，他们自然会专注于您。要达到这种流畅度，首先要写一个引人入胜的叙述，幻灯片中使用比文字更多的视觉元素，并不断练习、练习、再练习。

**1、Fake confidence**

Fake confidence. It's totally fine to feel nervous, but you don't have to act
that way. The more confident you act, the more confident you will feel. This
doesn't mean that you need to act arrogant. Try to calm yourself and remind
yourself that what you're doing isn't unusual. Rather, it's necessary,
interesting, and ordinary.  
·Remind yourself that your audience likely can't see your nerves.  ·Take a
deep breath and exhale before you go on stage.

假装自信-
感到紧张是完全正常的，但你不需要表现得那么紧张。表现得越自信，你就会感觉越自信。这并不意味着你需要表现得自大。试着让自己冷静下来，并提醒自己，你正在做的事情并不罕见。相反，它是必要的、有趣的，而且很普通。

  

·提醒自己，观众可能看不出你的紧张。

·上台前深吸一口气，然后呼气。

**2、Show your emotion**

Show your emotion. Smile, raise your eyebrows, and otherwise tell your story
through feelings as well as facts. Show your audience your own passion for the
topic. Say everything as if you find it interesting and expect them to be
interested by it as well. Your enthusiasm will be infectious.

展示你的情感。微笑、扬眉，通过情感和事实来讲述你的故事。向观众展示你对主题的热情。讲话时，要表现出你对内容的兴趣，并期望他们也会对其感兴趣。你的热情将具有感染力。

**3、Speak to your audience**

Speak to your audience. The more directly you address your audience, the more
engaged they will feel. Be relaxed and conversational. Rather than rattling
off your points, deliver them to the audience as if you are certain they will
be interesting. Look directly into the faces of the people you are
addressing.If there are too many people to really see faces, just look boldly
into the crowd.

与观众对话。你越直接地与观众交流，他们就会感到越投入。放松并保持对话的语气。不要只是机械地列出要点，而是像确信它们会引起兴趣一样传达给观众。直视你讲话对象的面孔。如果人数太多，无法看清每个人的脸，就大胆地望向人群。

**4、Mind your body language.**

Mind your body language. Stand up straight and face the audience completely.
Keep your head up and your shoulders back and relaxed. Don't twist any part of
your body (shoulders, hips) away from the audience.  
·Move your hands as you speak. Don't wave them, as this will make you look
nervous. Instead, try calmly gesturing with your palm out when you make a
point. If you describe a shape, draw it in the air with your hands.

注意你的肢体语言。站直身体，完全面向观众。抬起头，肩膀向后放松。不要将身体的任何部分（肩膀、臀部）扭向远离观众的方向。

  

说话时移动你的手，但不要挥舞手臂，这会让你看起来紧张。相反，在表达观点时，试着平静地用手掌朝外的手势。如果描述一个形状，用双手在空中比划出来。
