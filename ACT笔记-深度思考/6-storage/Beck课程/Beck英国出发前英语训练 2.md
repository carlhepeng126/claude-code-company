# Beck英国出发前英语训练

---

## 一、海关 & 入境

### 可能被问的问题及回答

| 问题                               | 回答                                            |
| ---------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| What is the purpose of your visit? | **Business. I’m attending a trade show.**       |
| How long will you stay?            | **About 5 days. I’ll leave on Saturday night.** |
| Where will you be staying?         | **At a hotel near the exhibition centre.**      |
| Do you have anything to declare?   | **No**                                          |
| Have you been to the UK before?    | **Yes, a few times.**                           |

### 主动说

- **I’m here for a trade show at [ExCel / NEC / Olympia].**  
- **I have my hotel confirmation if you need to see it.**  
- **I’ll be staying in London for 5 nights.**

> 提示：保持平静，看着对方眼睛，回答干脆。不需要主动说多余的话。

## 二、打招呼 & 自我介绍

### 不同时间段的问候

- **Good morning** (到中午12点)  
- **Good afternoon** (12点-傍晚)  
- **Good evening** (晚上6点后)  
- 非正式场合：**Morning / Afternoon / Evening**（更轻松）

### 介绍自己（不同说法）

- **I’m Beck, General manager from [Company].**  
- **Let me introduce myself. I’m Beck.**  

### 回应对方介绍

- **Nice to meet you too.**  
- **I’ve heard a lot about you.** (久仰)  
- **It’s a pleasure.** (很荣幸)

### 简单寒暄

- **How was your flight?** → **Long but comfortable, thanks.**  
- **Is this your first time in London?** → **Yes, first time. 
- **The weather is a bit cold, isn’t it?** → **Yes, but I like it.**

## 三、展会 & 商务社交

### 讨论产品 / 合作

- **We’re looking for partners in Europe.**  
- **Do you have a distributor in the UK?**  
- **I’d like to introduce our key features.**  
- **Please feel free to ask any questions.**

### 交换联系方式

- **May I have your business card?**  
- **Let me give you my card.**  
- **Would you prefer email or WhatsApp?**  
- **I’ll scan your QR code if that’s OK.**

### 预约进一步沟通

- **Are you free for a 15-minute chat later today?**  
- **How about tomorrow morning? Around 10?**  
- **Let’s set up a call after the show. I’ll email you.**

### 委婉结束对话

- **It’s been great talking to you. I don’t want to take too much of your time.**  
- **I should go catch my next meeting. Let’s stay connected on LinkedIn.**  
- **Thanks for your time. Enjoy the rest of the show.**

## 五、酒店入住 & 服务

### 前台常用

- **I have a reservation under the name Beck.**  
- **Could I have a quiet room, please?**  
- **What time is breakfast?** → **From 7 to 10 am.**  
- **Is Wi-Fi free?**  
- **Could I have an extra key / more towels?**  
- **What’s the check-out time?** → **Usually 11 am.**

### 遇到问题

- **The air conditioning doesn’t work.**  
- **There’s no hot water in my room.**  
- **Could you send someone to fix it?**

## 六、餐厅 & 咖啡厅（简单点餐）

### 基本用语

- **A table for one / two, please.**  
- **Can I see the menu, please?**  
- **What do you recommend?**  
- **I’ll have the fish and chips.** (经典英国菜)  
- **Just a coffee / tea, please.**  
- **Still water, please.** (无气泡水)  
- **Sparkling water.** (气泡水)  

### 结账

- **Can I have the bill, please?** (英式说法，不用check)  
- **Is service charge included?**  
- **Keep the change.** (小费不用找)

### 常见食物词汇

- **Full English breakfast** (英式全餐)  
- **Sandwich / Toast / Porridge**  
- **Beer / Wine / Soft drink**

## 七、应急 & 常用礼貌用语

### 求助

- **Excuse me.** (不好意思 / 打扰了 – 万能开头)  
- **I’m sorry, I don’t understand. Could you write it down?**  
- **Could you help me? I need to make a phone call.**  
- **Where is the nearest toilet / restroom?** (英国说toilet)

### 礼貌短句

- **Please.** / **Thank you very much.** / **Thanks a lot.**  
- **Sorry.** (不小心碰到人)  
- **No problem.** / **That’s fine.**  
- **Have a nice day!** / **Have a good evening!**
