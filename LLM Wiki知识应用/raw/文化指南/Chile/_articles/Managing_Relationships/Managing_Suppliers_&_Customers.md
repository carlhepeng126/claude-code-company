# Managing Suppliers & Customers

## Managing Relationships

Relationships between suppliers and customers in Chile form through in-person contact and multiple meetings before commitments are made. Business happens between individuals who trust one another, so early phone and email exchanges rarely move deals forward without face-to-face follow-up. Suppliers visiting Chilean customers and customers meeting their suppliers for lunch or dinner establishes the personal connection that underpins reliability. If you manage a supplier relationship remotely without regular visits, expect slower decision-making and less transparent communication during problems.

Chile's extreme geography makes logistics central to how suppliers are judged. The country stretches more than 4,000 kilometres north to south, with mountain passes to the east and coastal routes vulnerable to weather and strikes. Customers evaluate suppliers not on perfect delivery timelines but on how openly they communicate during disruptions and how credibly they explain fixes. A supplier who owns a delay caused by a port strike and proposes a workaround will be remembered more favorably than one who stays silent until pressed.

Large state entities and private economic groups dominate many sectors as anchor clients, and winning their contracts can define a supplier's growth. These relationships come with stringent compliance expectations and tight payment terms that can squeeze margins. Suppliers who overpromise capacity or pricing to secure entry often find themselves unable to sustain the relationship. Building in realistic lead times and pricing from the start protects the partnership and your reputation.

Chilean consumers increasingly compare local service to global standards encountered through e-commerce and travel. Being unresponsive, opaque, or slow now risks lasting damage, especially with middle-class customers. Offering clear communication across multiple channels, including WhatsApp, phone, and email, meets expectations shaped by international platforms and strengthens customer loyalty.
