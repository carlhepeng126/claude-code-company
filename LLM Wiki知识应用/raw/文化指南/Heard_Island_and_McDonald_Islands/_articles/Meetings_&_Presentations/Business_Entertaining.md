# Business Entertaining

## Meetings & Presentations

Heard Island and McDonald Islands is an uninhabited Australian external territory in the southern Indian Ocean, consisting of barren volcanic islands with no permanent human presence. There are no business activities, meetings, or professional interactions of any kind on the islands. Scientific expeditions are rare and logistically complex, typically conducted under strict protocols that do not include social or business entertaining.

Because the territory has no resident population, economy, or commercial infrastructure, the concept of business entertaining does not apply. Any professional collaboration related to the islands takes place in Australian offices on the mainland, where Australian business norms and practices apply. Researchers or officials working on matters related to Heard Island and McDonald Islands would follow the entertaining conventions of their home institutions or the cities where they are based, not practices specific to the territory itself.
