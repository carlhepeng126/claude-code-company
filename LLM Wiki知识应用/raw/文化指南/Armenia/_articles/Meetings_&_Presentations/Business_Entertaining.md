# Business Entertaining

## Meetings & Presentations

Business entertaining in Armenia plays a real role in deepening relationships, though it tends to be informal rather than lavish. Meals offer a chance to talk beyond the agenda and assess how someone thinks and relates. Once trust begins to form, Armenian colleagues may suggest lunch or dinner, often at a restaurant rather than a home. This invitation is usually extended by more senior people, and it reflects growing interest in the relationship, not just courtesy.

The meal itself is rarely about negotiation. Conversations range widely, and business topics may surface only briefly, or not at all. Armenians often use these settings to understand character and compatibility, which they consider more telling than formal exchanges. Raising business too early can come across as transactional. The host will steer the discussion when they feel the moment is right, and that timing matters.

Toasting is common and can be quite heartfelt, especially when cognac is involved. Armenian brandy holds national pride, and offering it is a gesture of warmth. Declining alcohol is generally respected, but be ready for light encouragement if the table is convivial. Women may find themselves less frequently invited to these dinners, particularly in more traditional sectors or outside Yerevan, where business entertaining remains a largely male setting.

Reciprocating the invitation on a later occasion is appreciated and reinforces mutual regard. It need not be formal, but it should be genuine.
