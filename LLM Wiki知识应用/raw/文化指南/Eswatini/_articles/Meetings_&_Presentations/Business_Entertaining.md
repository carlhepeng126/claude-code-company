# Business Entertaining

## Meetings & Presentations

Business entertaining in Eswatini plays a meaningful role in building relationships, though the setting and tone differ depending on the stage of the connection and the seniority of the people involved. Meals often take place once initial rapport has been established, offering an opportunity to deepen trust outside the constraints of the office. The gathering itself serves a relational purpose rather than a transactional one. Conversation is usually relaxed and wide-ranging, and business topics may not be raised at all unless the host brings them up. Introducing work matters too early can feel rushed or overly instrumental, particularly when the aim is to understand how people think and relate rather than simply closing a point.

Urban settings tend to favor restaurants, where the choice of cuisine may reflect a mix of local preference and consideration for international guests. Alcohol is rarely part of business entertaining in Eswatini. Most professional gatherings are alcohol-free, shaped by cultural and religious norms. Even in settings where alcohol might be available, it's uncommon to see it ordered during work-related meals. Assuming its presence or offering it without clear indication from the host can create discomfort. Following the host's lead is the safest approach.

Reciprocating an invitation is valued and should be done thoughtfully, either during the same visit or on a return trip. The gesture demonstrates respect for the relationship and acknowledges the time and effort the host has invested.
