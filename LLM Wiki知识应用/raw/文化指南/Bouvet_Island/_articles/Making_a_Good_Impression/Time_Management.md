# Time Management

## Making a Good Impression

Bouvet Island is uninhabited and has no professional culture or workplace norms. The island is a Norwegian dependency located in the sub-Antarctic, covered almost entirely by glaciers, and visited only occasionally by research expeditions. There are no permanent residents, businesses, or cultural practices related to time management.

Any discussion of time management in a professional context would require a population and an economy, neither of which exist on Bouvet Island. The island has no offices, no meetings, and no schedules beyond the logistical planning of the rare scientific teams that land there under extreme conditions.

For guidance on working with Norwegian professionals, who administer the territory, refer to the Norway country guide.
