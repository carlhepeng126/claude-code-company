# Business Lunch & Dinner

## Meetings & Presentations

Food in Trinidad and Tobago is more than fuel or hospitality. It holds memory and lineage, reflecting African, Indian, Chinese, European, and Indigenous histories layered into everyday meals. What appears on the table often quietly marks cultural identity and family pride. A colleague might serve dishes tied to Divali, Eid, or Creole traditions during a meal, and how you respond to the food can be read as respect for that heritage. Showing genuine interest in what you are eating and its origins tends to deepen connection faster than treating the meal as background to business talk.

Lunch is the main meal of the day, often eaten at home or as a full cooked meal rather than something quick at a desk. Roti, pelau, doubles, and bake and shark are everyday foods that cross cultural lines and provide natural common ground. Choosing to eat at informal street food spots can soften formality and show willingness to engage on local terms. Knowing what to order or how to talk about the food can become a visible marker of familiarity, which often builds trust in early relationships. A colleague from a culture where business meals stay formal throughout might misread this informality as unprofessional, while a Trinidadian colleague might read overly transactional dining as cold or distant.

Religious and cultural pluralism means dietary norms vary widely. Hindu, Muslim, Christian, and Afro-syncretic communities each hold different expectations around alcohol, meat, fasting periods, and table conduct. Many people accommodate this through quiet flexibility rather than stating rules upfront. Asking about preferences ahead of time rather than assuming everyone drinks or eats the same way avoids awkwardness. Respectability norms around tone, modesty, and alcohol can influence how you are perceived, particularly in settings where family reputation and upbringing are judged through behavior. Beer and rum are common, but whisky and wine are seen as more prestigious, and not everyone will drink at all.
