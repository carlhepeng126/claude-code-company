# Business Entertaining

## Meetings & Presentations

Business entertaining in Curaçao tends to be informal and relationship-focused, though invitations may come later in the working relationship rather than early on. Meals offer a chance to build rapport outside the office, and hospitality, when extended, is genuine. The setting is usually a restaurant, often one with local seafood or dishes reflecting the island's Dutch, Caribbean, and Latin American influences. Conversation is typically relaxed and personal, rarely dominated by work talk unless the host opens that door.

Alcohol is part of social life in Curaçao, and it's common for drinks to be served during business meals. That said, not everyone drinks, and preferences can vary by individual or setting. Following the host's lead avoids missteps. Pushing business topics too early or treating the meal purely as a forum for negotiation can feel transactional. The evening itself is the point: a space to observe tone, humor, and how people relate beyond formalities.

Reciprocation is appreciated, though it need not be immediate or elaborate. Hosting a return meal during your visit or on a future trip signals respect for the relationship. The gesture acknowledges the time and thought your counterpart invested, and reinforces that the connection extends beyond a single transaction or project phase.
