# Business Entertaining

## Meetings & Presentations

Business entertaining in Cambodia often means gathering at open-air beer gardens, Khmer barbecue spots, or riverside restaurants, where sharing grilled meats, soups, and beer served over ice builds the kind of relaxed familiarity that formal meetings rarely allow. These settings offer a chance to observe who the group defers to, who initiates toasts, and who pours drinks for whom. These details say more about hierarchy and working relationships than org charts usually reveal.

Toasting is common among those who drink, and there can be real pressure to keep pace, especially among men. Some colleagues will use humor to encourage others to drink more, and declining repeatedly without joining the group in some way can feel isolating. If you drink, it helps to pace yourself and never push others to match you. If you don't, find a comfortable way to decline while still raising a glass with a soft drink. Either approach works if it's done with warmth and consistency.

Karaoke venues are popular for entertaining, particularly in male-dominated sectors, but they can blur into adult entertainment spaces that exclude or discomfort women colleagues and raise compliance concerns. If you're organizing, suggest venues that work for the whole team, not just a subset. A beer garden or restaurant with an easy atmosphere offers connection without putting anyone in an awkward position. Business entertaining is as much about who feels included as it is about building rapport.
