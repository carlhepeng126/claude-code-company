#  硬关井(Hard Shut-in)

一、定义

硬关井是指钻井过程中，节流管汇阀(液控阀)处于关闭位置。关井后，打开液控阀。硬关井是最快的关井方法。因此，硬关井能将进入井筒的溢流最小化(It means
that while drilling choke line valves (HCR) are in the closed position; it
will be opened after the well is shut in. The hard shut-in is the fastest
method to shut in the well; therefore, it will minimize volume of kick allowed
into wellbore)

二、关井程序

1.在钻井时观察到任何表明井可能正在发生井涌的迹象时，停止旋转钻柱，并且在开泵状态下提升钻柱，直到钻杆接头高出钻台。When any indication
is observed while drilling that the well may be flowing, stop rotating the
drill string and raise the drill string with pumps on until the tool joint is
above the drill floor.  
2.停泵，检查是否有溢流。如果有，关闭环形和半封(Stop pumps and check for flow; if positive, Close
annular BOP or pipe preventer rams)  
3.打开节流管汇液控阀(Open choke line HCR valve)  
4.叫钻井监督，开始绘制关井钻杆压力图(Call the rig drilling supervisor and commence plotting a
graph of shut-in drill pipe pressure)

5.检查泥浆池体积增量(Check pit volume gain)

三、优点和缺点

1.与软关井相比，硬关井程序关井时间短，因此侵入井筒的溢流量较少(This procedure allows the well to be closed
in the shortest possible time, thereby minimizing the amount of additional
influx of kicking fluid to enter the wellbore)

2.与软关井相比，硬关井程序相对没那么复杂，可以由一人执行，不会产生无意的延误(The hard close-in procedure is
somewhat less complicated, can be performed by one man working on the rig
floor, and is more likely to be performed without inadvertent delays in
closure than the soft close-in the procedure)

3.关井套管压力相对较低，有利于井控。(Shut-in casing pressure is relatively low,which aids in
well control)

‌4.硬关井时，液流通道突然关闭，导致液流速度急剧变化，产生强烈的“水击效应”，可能对井口装置、套管和地层造成损害(With hard shut-in,
flow path is suddenly closed resulting in dramatic change of flow speed and
creating strong water hammer effect.It can cause damage to wellhead
device,casing and formation)
