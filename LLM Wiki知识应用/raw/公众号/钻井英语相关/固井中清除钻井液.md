For cementing operations to be successful, all annular spaces must be filled
with cement, and the cement properly bonded to the previous casing and
formation. In order for this to occur, all the drilling fluid must be
displaced by the cement slurry. This is not always an easy matter, because
there are

several factors which affect the removal of the drilling fluid:  

要想固井作业成功，所有的环空必须充满水泥。水泥能恰当地将之前下入的套管与地层粘合在一起。为了达到这个目的，所有的钻井液应该由水泥浆顶替。这通常不是一件容易的事，因为有好几个因素影响着钻井液的清除。

•washouts in the open hole, making it difficult to remove drilling fluid and
filter cake

裸眼井中冲洗液使得清除钻井液和滤饼变得困难。

•crooked holes, making casing centralization difficult and drilling fluid not
being removed from the low side  

弯井眼使得套管居中困难，钻井液不能从低侧清除

•poorly treated drilling fluids having high fluid losses  

钻井液处理的不好，漏失量大

Good drilling practices will not assure a good cement job, but they may help
prevent a failure. The ideal drilling fluid for cementing operations should
have:

好的钻井做法并不一定确保好的固井质量

，但是可以预防固井失败。对于固井作业来说，理想的钻井液应具有以下特点：

•a low gel strength, with low PV and low YP  

低胶凝强度，低塑性粘度和屈服点

•a low density

低密度

•a low fluid loss

低漏失量

•a chemical make-up similar to the cement  

化学结构与水泥类似

Since these conditions are very seldom met, fluid washes and spacers are

usually pumped ahead of the cement to remove as much drilling fluid as
possible.

因为很少能满足这些条件，所以通常是在固井前，泵入冲洗液和隔离液来尽可能多的清除钻井液。

重点词汇：

annular space-环空

cement slurry-水泥浆

bond-粘合

filter cake-滤饼

displace-顶替

washouts-清洗液

crooked hole-弯曲井眼

cementing operation-固井作业

gel strength-胶凝强度

PV-塑性粘度

YP-屈服点

fluid loss-滤失量

spacer-隔离液

chemical make-up-化学结构
