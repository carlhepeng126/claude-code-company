准备测试防喷器组

1.Before the test,the rig manager and the tester will ensure that all
personnel are fully aware of their required assistance,including any needed
emergency action.

测试前，平台经理和测试人员应确保，所有人员完全了解他们所需要的帮助和必要的应急措施。

2.Ensure the testing area is secure with caution tape.

测试区域布置警戒带，确保安全

3.all blowout prevention equipment,except annular BOP's will be pressure
tested to the minimum following criteria:

所有防喷器设备，除了环形防喷器外，都按照以下最低标准进行压力测试

-maximum anticipated well head pressure(if known) 

预计最大的井口压力（如果已知）

-80% of casing burst pressure 

百分之八十套管破裂压力

-working pressure rating of the wellhead 

井口工作压力等级

-working pressure rating of the BOP stack 

防喷器组工作压力等级

4.annular BOP's should be tested to 70% of rated working pressure

环形防喷器试压，应试到额定工作压力的百分之七十

5.all BOP equipment tests must include a low-pressure test of 250psi before
proceeding to the full pressure test

在进行全压测试前，所有防喷器设备测试，必须包括250psi低压测试

6.test pressure held (and recorded )for a minimum of five minutes after full
pressure has been established represents a satisfactory pressure test

全压测试后，结果满意的话，稳压（并记录）至少5分钟。

7.all high pressure connections associated with well control
equipment(i.e.choke&kill line valves,choke manifold valves,TIW valves,floor
safety valves,inside BOP valves) are to be isolated and pressure tested during
each BOP test.

每次防喷器测试期间，所有与井控设备有关的高压连接处（例如节流&压井管线阀、节流管汇阀、德克萨斯钢厂生产的阀、平台安全阀、内防喷器阀）要隔离并进行压力测试。

8.when the integrity of the BOP stack has been comprised(I.e.changing rams or
lifting the stack),the comprised component must be re-tested. This includes
testing the ram,associated bonnet seals,or body testing a flange connection

当防喷器组整体性受到破坏（例如换闸板或上提防喷器组）时，被破坏的部件必须重新进行测试。重新测试包括测试闸板、有关的密封阀盖或是法兰连接处本体测试。

9.the possibility of test pressure leaking past a packoff or test plug and
being applied to a weaker element(I.e.casing collapse,lower rated ring
gasket,etc.)must always be considered. All reasonable steps should be taken to
monitor or eliminate this event.

必须经常考虑到这种可能性：测试中的漏压，通过密封装置或测试塞，作用在更加脆弱的部件上（例如导致套管挤压，漏压作用下额定垫圈等）。必须采取所有可能措施来监测或消除这种事件的发生。
