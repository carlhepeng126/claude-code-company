The introduction of advanced drill bit design s has not always had the effect
of Obsoleting existing designs. Instead,the availability of new designs has
increased the number of types of bit available from the various bit
manufacturers.

引入先进的钻头设计，通常并不会淘汰已有的钻头设计。相反，新的钻头设计使用，增加了不同钻头生产商生产钻头类型的数量。

Selection of a bit best suited for a specific use is further complicated by
the variable performances and prices of many types of bits. Thus the question
which needs to be answered is :How can the correct bit be selected for a given
application?

由于不同类型钻头不同的性能及价格，这让选择钻头适合特定的用途变得更加复杂化。因此，需要回答的问题就是：如何针对指定的应用，选择正确的钻头。

The decision concerning which bit to use often base on some performance
criteria,such as total rotating hours,total footage or maximum penetration
rate. Other times,the least expensive bit is chosen. This approach may be
satisafactory in areas where practices and cost are constant but it may not be
satisafactory where drilling cost are changing,and drilling practices and bit
selection vary.

选用什么样钻头决定，通常基于一些性能指标，例如总旋转时间、总进尺、或最佳机械钻速。其它时间，选择便宜不太贵的钻头。这种方法在钻井方法和成本恒定的地区，可能会令人满意。但是，在钻井成本变化、钻井方法和钻头选择变动地区，这种方法可能不太令人满意。

A realistic approach to bit selection base the final decision on the minimum
cost-per-foot.

In this way,it is possible to achieve an optimum relationship between
penetration rate,bit footage,rig cost,trip time and bit cost.

现实中钻头选择的方法最终决定，基于最低每英尺成本。使用这种方法，有可能实现机械钻速、钻头进尺、钻机成本、起下钻时间和钻头成本之间的最佳关系。
