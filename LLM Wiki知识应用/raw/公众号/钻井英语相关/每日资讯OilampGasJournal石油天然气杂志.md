Sinopec discovers shale oil in Shandong province(中石化在山东省发现页岩油)

1.shale oil-页岩油，属于非常规能源的一种。非常规能源（Unconventional resource）包括页岩油(shale
oil)、页岩气(shale gas)、致密油(tight oil)、致密气(tight gas)、煤层气(coal bed
methane)、可燃冰(combustible ice)。

  

China Petroleum & Chemical Corp. (Sinopec) discovered hydrocarbons at its
Shengli Oilfield's Jiyang shale oil national demonstration zone in East
China's Shandong province(中国石油化工集团有限公司，简称中石化，在中国东部山东省其所属的胜利油田济阳页岩油国家示范区发现了油气)

2.hydrocarbon-可指油气或碳氢化合物

  

Over 140 million tonnes of proven geological reserves of shale oil were
discovered with technically recoverable reserves of 11.3599 million tonnes.
This is the first shale oil field with proven geological reserves of over 100
million tonnes that has been certificated by China's Ministry of Natural
Resources.(发现的已探明页岩油地质储量超过1.4亿吨，技术上可开采储量为11,359,900吨。这是首个经中国国家自然资源部认定的、已探明地质储量超过1亿吨的页岩油油田)

3.proven geological reserves-已探明地质储量。是指经过详细勘探，在预期的当地经济条件下，可用现有技术开采的储量。

  

Shengli Oilfield has developed advanced automated drilling equipment for fast
drilling and explored horizontal well optimization. The average drilling cycle
has been reduced to 29.5 days from 133 days, and the drilling cycle of 6,000-m
wells has reached 17.7 days, according to the
company.(根据公司报导，胜利油田已经研发出高级的自动化钻井设备实现快速钻井，并对水平井优化技术进行了探索。平均钻井周期已经从133天减少到29.5天。6000米井的钻井周期已经达到17.7天)

4.horizontal well-水平井。是指井斜角(inclination)达到或接近90°，并在目的层中维持一定长度水平井段的特殊井

5.drilling cycle-钻井周期。是从第一次开钻时间起到钻井完成时间为止的全部时间。

  

By using advancing reserve-fracture-pressure theories, developing full-cycle
3D technologies, and building an integrated geological engineering platform,
Sinopec expanded shale oil development from three to seven layers to increase
extraction efficiency and maximize resource recovery, it
said.(据报道，中石化通过使用先进的储层-裂缝-
压力理论，开发全周期3D技术，建立地质工程一体化平台，将开发的页岩油层从3层扩展到7层，提高了开采效率，并最大限度实现资源采收 )

6.integrated geological engineering platform-一体化地质工程平台。

  

In January 2021, Sinopec tested a high-yield shale oil and gas well at Fuling
shale gas field in Chongqing, with shale oil production surpassing 30,000
tonnes for the first time in
2024.(2021年1月，中石化在重庆涪陵页岩气田，对一口高产页岩油气井进行测试，2024年，该井页岩油产量第一次超过30,000吨)

  

By December 2021, Sinopec identified a favorable area of 420 sq km in the
Qintong Depression of Northern Jiangsu basin, with shale oil resources
totaling 350 million tonnes. In July 2022, a Northern Jiangsu basin well
yielded more than 30 tonnes of oil and 1,500 cu m of natural gas daily,
activating 1.1 billion tonnes of shale oil
resources.(到2021年12月，中石化在苏北盆地溱潼凹陷确定了一处面积为420平方公里的有利区域，该区域页岩油产量总计为3.5亿吨。2022年7月，一口苏北盆地的井，日产油量超过30吨，日产天然气量为1500立方米，盘活了11亿吨的页岩油资源。)

7.depression-凹陷。是地壳表面或地下岩石层向内或向下凹进的部分。
