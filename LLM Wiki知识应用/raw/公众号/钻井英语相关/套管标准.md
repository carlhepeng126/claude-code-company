The American Petroleum Institute (API) has developed certain standards and
specifications for oil-field related casing and tubing.One of the more common
standards is weight per unit length. There are three “weights” used:

美国石油协会为油田有关的套管和油管制定了某些标准和规范。其中一个更为普遍使用的标准是每单位长度重量。使用的重量有三种。

•Nominal Weight: Based on the theoretical calculated weight per foot for a 20
ft length of threaded and coupled casing joint.

套管公称重量：基于理论，计算20英尺长包括螺纹和接箍的套管重量。

•Plain End Weight: The weight of the joint of casing without the threads and
couplings.

平端重量：不带螺纹和接箍的一根套管重量。

•Threaded and Coupled Weight: The weight of a casing joint with threads on
both ends and a coupling at one end.

螺纹和接箍重量：两端带螺纹一端带接箍的套管重量。

The Plain End Weight, and the Threaded and Coupled Weight are calculated using
API formulas. These can be found in API Bulletin 5C3.

平端重量和带螺纹接箍重量使用美国石油协会方程式进行计算。这些方程式可以在美国石油协会公告5C3上发现。

API standards include three length ranges, which are:  

美国石油协会标准包括三种长度范围，分别是：

•R-1: Joint length must be within the range of 16 to 25 feet, and 95% must
have lengths greater than 18 feet.

R-1:单根套管长度必须在16到25英尺范围内。%95的单根长度必须大于18英尺。

•R-2: Joint length must be within the range of 25 to 34 feet, and 95% must
have lengths greater than 28 feet.

R-2:单根套管长度必须在25到34英尺范围内。%95的单根长度必须大于28英尺。

•R-3: Joint length must be over 34 feet, and 95% must have lengths greater
than 36 feet.

The API grade of casing denotes the steel properties of the casing. The grade
has a letter, which designates the grade, and a number, which designates the
minimum yield strength in thousands of psi.

R-3:单根套管长度必须超过34英尺。%95的单根套管长度必须大于36英尺。美国石油协会套管分级指示出套管钢性。分级上字母代表着等级，分级上数字代表几千psi下最小屈服强度。

Casing properties are defined as:

套管性质定义如下：

•Yield Strength: The tensile stress required to produce a total elongation of
0.5% per unit length

套管屈服强度：每单位长度产生0.5%总拉伸所需的抗拉应力。

•Burst Strength: The maximum internal pressure required to cause a casing
joint to yield.

套管破裂强度：导致套管接头屈服所需的最大内压。

•Collapse Strength: The maximum external pressure or force required to
collapse the casing joint.

套管挤压强度：挤压套管接头所需的最大内压或作用力。

Casing dimensions are specified by its outside diameter (OD) and nominal wall
thickness. Normal wellsite conventions specify casing by its OD and

weight per foot. As stated earlier, one should specify which weight one is
referring to, though most often it is the nominal weight.

套管尺寸由套管的外径及公称壁厚所确定。正常的井场惯例是由套管外径和每英尺

重量来确定套管。如之前所述，尽管通常是指公称重量，但是必须确定指的是哪个重量。
