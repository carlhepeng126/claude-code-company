#  与上周同期相比美国钻机数量减少7部，加拿大钻机数量减少15部

_2025年04月13日 06:00_ __ _ _ _ _ _ 黑龙江  _

The US drilling rig count decreased by 7 units during the week ended Apr. 11
to reach a total of 583 rotary rigs working, Baker Hughes Inc. reported. That
compares with 617 rigs working in the comparable week last year.  
据贝克休斯报导，截止到4月11日本周内，美国工作的旋转钻机数量减少7部，总数量达到583部。与之对比，去年同期钻机数量是617部。  
Land-based drilling decreased by 6 units from a week ago to 567 rigs working.
Offshore drilling decreased by 1 unit to 13 rigs working. Inland water
drilling, at 3 rigs, was unchanged from a week ago. Of the rigs drilling
offshore, 11 were in the Gulf of Mexico, a decrease of 1 unit from a week ago.

与一周前相比，陆上工作钻机数量减少了6部，达到567部。海上工作钻机数量减少了1部，达到13部。内陆水域钻机数量为3部，这一数字与一周前相比保持不变。所有的海上钻机中，11部钻机分布在墨西哥湾，与一周前相比减少了1部。

Rigs targeting natural gas increased by 1 unit to 97. Those rigs targeting
oil, meanwhile, decreased by 9 to 480. There were 6 rigs considered
unclassified, up 1 unit from a week ago.  
用于天然气井钻机数量增加1部，达到97部。同时，用于油井的钻机数量减少了9部，达到480部。有6部钻机未分类，与一周前相比，增加了1部。  
Rigs drilling directionally were reported at 46, down 2 units from a week ago
and 5 fewer than the comparable week last year. Rigs drilling horizontally
fell 6 units to 523. This compared with 554 rigs working horizontally in the
comparable week a year ago.

定向井钻机数量为46部，与一周之前相比，减少了2部，与去年同期相比减少了5部。水平井钻机数量减少了2部，达到523部。与之形成对比，去年同期，水平井钻机的数量是554部。

Of the major oil and gas producing states, Texas was down 3 rigs to 274. West
Virginia and California were each down 2 units to reach respective counts of 9
and 6. New Mexico was off 1 unit to 100  
在主要油气生产州，德克萨斯洲钻机数量减少3部，达到274部。西弗吉尼亚州和加利福利亚州钻机数量各减少2部，分别达到9部和6部。新墨西哥州减少1部，达到100部。  
Canada’s rig count slumped this week, down 15 units to 138. This count
includes 91 rigs drilling for oil (down 8 units from a week ago) and 47 units
drilling for gas (down 7 from a week ago). The total was down 3 units from the
comparable week last year.

本周加拿大钻机数量减少了15部，达到138部。这138部钻机中，91部钻机用来钻油井（与一周之前相比减少了8部），47部钻机用来钻气井(与一周之前相比减少了7部)。钻机总数与去年同期相比，减少了3部。
