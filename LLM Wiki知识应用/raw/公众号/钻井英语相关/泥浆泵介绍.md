Mud pumps serve as the heart of the mud circulating system. Reciprocating
piston pumps (also called slush pumps or power pumps) are widely used for
drilling oil and gas wells.

泥浆泵是泥浆循环系统的核心。往复式活塞泵（也叫做污泥泵或动力泵）广泛地使用在油气钻井中。

难点解析：

1.reciprocating piston pump-往复式活塞泵

2.slush pump-污泥泵

The advantages of the reciprocating positive-displacement pump include the
ability to move high-solids-content fluids laden with abrasives, the ability
to pump large particles, ease of operation and maintenance, reliability, and
the ability to operate over a wide range of pressures and flow rates by
changing the diameters of the compression cylinders (pump liners) and pistons.

往复式正排量泵的优点：能够移动含有磨料的高固相含量液体；能够泵入大颗粒；容易操作和维护；可靠；通过改变压缩气缸（缸套）和活塞的直径，能够在不同的压力和流量范围进行操作。

难点解析：

3.positive-displacement pump-正排量泵

4.high solid content-高固相含量

5.compression cylinder-压缩气缸

6.pump liner-泥浆泵缸套

7.piston-活塞

The two types of piston strokes are the single-action piston stroke and the
double-action piston stroke. A pump that has double-action strokes in two
cylinders is called a duplex pump (Figure 1.2). A pump that has single-action
strokes in three cylinders is called a triplex pump (Figure 1.3). Triplex
pumps are lighter and more compact than duplex pumps, their output pressure
pulsations are not as great, and they are cheaper to operate.

活塞冲程有两种类型，即单作用活塞冲程和双作用活塞冲程。使用双气缸双作用冲程的泵叫做双缸泵。使用三个气缸单作用冲程的泵叫做三缸泵。三缸泵比两缸泵更轻、更紧凑。三缸泵输出压力脉冲不那么大，而且更容易操作。

难点解析：

8.single-action-单作用

9.stroke-冲程

10.double-action-双作用

11.duplex pump-双缸泵

12.triplex pump-三缸泵

13.pulsation-脉冲
