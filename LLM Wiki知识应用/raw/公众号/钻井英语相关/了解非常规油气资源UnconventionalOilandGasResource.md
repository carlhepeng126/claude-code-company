一.定义

非常资源指的是使用传统钻井方法不能开采的油气藏。传统的油气藏中，油气能自由流入井中。而非常规资源是被困在岩石地层中，需要先进的技术来开采(Unconventional
resources refer to hydrocarbon deposits that cannot be extracted using
traditional drilling methods.Unlikeconventional reservoirs, where oil and gas
flow freely into a well, unconventional resources are trapped in rock
formations, requiring advanced techniques for extraction)

二.非常规资源构成

页岩油、页岩气  :发现在低渗透性的页岩层(Shale oil and gas: Found in low-permeability shale
formations)

致密油、致密气  :封锁在砂岩或碳酸盐储层中(Tight oil and gas: Locked in sandstone or carbonate
reservoirs)

煤层气:从煤层缝中开采出来的天然气(

Coalbed methane: Natural gas extracted from coal seams  )

油砂  :沥青的沉积物，需要露天开采或热开采(  Oil sands: Bitumen deposits that require surface
mining or thermal extraction  )

三.开采工艺

水平钻井(horizontal drilling)和水利压裂(hydrauluc fracturing)工艺是开采非常规资源的常用工艺。

四、优缺点

1.开采灵活(Flexibility in oil extraction)

2.油可以一直存储在井筒内，直到油价上涨(Oil can be stored in wells until oil price rise)

3.负面影响环境(negatively impacts the environment)

4.需要相当多的水(requires a tremendous amount of water)

5.污染当地社区饮用水(pollutes drinking water in communities )

6.造成地震(causes earthquake)
