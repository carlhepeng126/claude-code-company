#  顶驱的优点

The top drive has many advantages that help better its functions during the
drilling involving safety and efficient way of drilling and are enlisted
below:  
顶驱的许多优点，能在钻井过程中，确保钻井安全、高效，更好发挥其功能。主要优点如下:  
1.In the top drive system, it is capable to drill with three drill pipes at a
time instead of a single drill pipe in a conventional Kelly system.

顶驱设备能够一次钻三根钻杆，这与传统方钻杆设备一次钻一根钻杆形成了鲜明对比  
2.Easy installation on any mast or derrick.

可以很容易安装在任何井架上  
3.There is also an economic perspective of using the top drive as a drilling
method to make it feasible. As it involves a low risk of differential stuck of
drill pipe in the formation saving cost and time.

使用顶驱设备钻井，也有经济角度的考量。因为顶驱方法钻井，降低了在地层中压差卡钻的风险，这节省了钻井成本和时间  
4.The top drive system gives drillers enough time to activate or deactivate
mud pumps or the rotary while connecting or disconnecting the pipes.

在上扣、卸扣同时，顶驱设备可以留给司钻足够的时间去启动和关停泥浆泵或转盘  
5.It also proves feasible for directional drilling wells and extended reach.

在钻定向井和大位移井，使用顶驱钻井也证明是可行的。  
6.The torque required to make the connections tight can easily be provided by
the top drives.

上扣所需的扭矩可以很容易地由顶驱提供
