一、定义

The red zone refers to the highest-risk areas where heavy machinery is moving
up and down and only highly skilled crew members are permitted access. It has
a static geofence and should be clearly marked at the rig with appropriate
physical and digital safety signage and tools (e.g. traffic light system).

红区是指重型设备上下移动、仅有具备高水平技术能力的井队人员才允许进入的高风险区域。红区应设置静态地理围栏，并用合适的实体和数字安全标识和工具做出明显的标记。（如交通信号系统）

二、  钻井现场红区范围

1\. 钻台及其周边区域(drill floor and its perimeter)

Drill floor:the moving scope of heavy equipment such as traveling block, TDS,
hook existsts the risks of collision, squeezing, dropping  
钻台：游车、顶驱、大钩等重型设备的移动范围，存在碰撞、挤压或坠落风险。  
Wellhead:the area where drilling string moves when tripping and making a
connection, is likely to encounter the dropping of tool and jetting of high
pressure flow.

井口：起下钻、接单根时钻柱活动区域，易发生工具掉落或高压流体喷射。  
The area surrounding mouse hole and rat hole:the risk of string swaying exists
when operating the DP stands

鼠洞与大鼠洞（Rat Hole）附近：钻杆立柱操作时存在管柱摆动风险。

2\. 防喷器组周围(The surrounding of BOP stack)

BOP body and control line area:be under extremely high pressure(up to
15,000psi  ) during well control operation and be likely to cause the risk of
high pressure leakage or blow out

防喷器本体及控制管线区域：井控作业时承受极高压力（可达15,000 psi以上），存在高压泄漏或井喷风险。  
Choke manifold and kill manifold:in the key area controlled by high pressure
flow, operation failure may cause uncontrol  
节流管汇与压井管汇：高压流体控制的关键区域，操作失误可能导致失控。

3\. 高压流体喷射路径(Jetting path for high pressure flow)

Connection between drill string and wellhead:high pressure flow may spew from
the wellhead or pipe joint when circulating the drilling fluid or encountering
the well kick.  
钻柱与井口连接处：循环钻井液或发生井涌时，高压流体可能从井口或管线接头喷出。

High pressure pipe from mud pump outlet to standpipe: the pressure surge or
pipe rupture may cause flow jetting injuries.  
泥浆泵出口至立管的高压管线：压力波动或管线破裂可能造成流体喷射伤害。

4\. 危险物质暴露区(Exposure area of dangerous materials)  
Potential leak area for H2S:if the formation contains H2S,the area like
wellhead, mud circulation tank needs to be marked as red zone to prevent the
toxic gas poisoning.  
硫化氢（H₂S）潜在泄漏区：若地层含硫化氢，井口、泥浆循环罐等区域需划为红区，防止有毒气体中毒。  
Radioactive source or storage area of chemical additives:area involved in
radioactive logging or dangerous chemicals usage  
放射性源或化学添加剂存放区：涉及放射性测井或危险化学品使用的区域

三、红区的安全管理措施  
1\. 物理隔离(Physical demarcation)：  
Clearly identity the boundary with red warning tape,fence or signage

使用红色警戒线、围栏或标识牌明确边界。  
Install infrared senser alarm and monitor the personnel from entering
mistakenly  
安装红外感应报警装置，监测人员误入。  
  
2\. 进入许可(Entry permit)  
Entry permit that is approved by supervisor is required  
必须持有进入许可证，并由监督人员批准。

Necessary personnel (driller, Derrick man) is only allowed during work  
仅允许必要人员（如司钻、井架工）在作业期间进入。  
3\. 个人防护装备（PPE)

Wear fire clothing, safety helmet, googles, hard toe shoe, etc.  
穿戴防火服、安全帽、护目镜、防砸鞋等。  
be equipped with SCBS in the red zone of H2S

在硫化氢红区需配备正压式空气呼吸器  
  
4\. 实时监控(Real-time Monitoring)  
CCTV monitoring system covers the red zone  
视频监控系统覆盖红区  
set up audible and visual alarm to indicate equipment startup or abnormal
conditions

设置声光报警装置，提示设备启动或异常情况。
