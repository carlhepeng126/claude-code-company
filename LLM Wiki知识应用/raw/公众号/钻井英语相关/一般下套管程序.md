#  一般下套管程序

1\. Visually check each joint of casing to ensure all joints are clear of
foreign matter, measured and drifted（目视检测每根套管，确保所有套管内部不含异物，所有套管都进行了测量、通径）

2\. Reduce the annular preventer closing pressure to less than the collapse
rating of the casing, if necessary（如果必要的话，将关闭环形的压力减少到小于套管的抗挤强度）

3\. Make up a full opening safety valve on the casing circulating swage and
position it in a readily accessible drill floor
location（在套管循环头上安装全开安全阀，并将安全阀放置在易进入钻台位置）

4\. Make up the shoe floats and shoe track as per the running
list（按照入井列表，连接浮鞋和浮箍）.

5\. Check float equipment after the shoetrack is run in to ensure the float is
holding and that circulation is possible（下入套管柱后，检查浮鞋、浮箍，确保浮鞋、浮箍有效，可以进行循环）  
6\. Install centralisers as per the centraliser programme（按照扶正器设计安装扶正器）  
7\. Partially fill each joint and completely fill every five
joints（逐根部分灌浆，并且每5根完全地灌浆一次）  
8\. Make up the connections in accordance with API Spec 5CT（按照APISpec
5CT套管上扣要求进行套管上扣作业）.  
9\. Run the casing smoothly, avoiding high acceleration and deceleration which
could cause unnecessary surge/swab
pressures（平稳下入套管，避免速度过快、过慢，否则能产生不必要的激动/抽汲压力）.  
10\. Limit the casing lowering speed to 45 sec/joint or to the optimum speed
from surge/swab calculations（将下入套管的速度限制在45秒/根，或限制在最佳速度，避免产生激动/抽汲压力）  
11\. Monitor returns constantly by using the trip tank and inform the Driller
of any potential loss circulation zones（通过使用补给罐，持续监测返出泥浆量，通知司钻任何可能发生井漏的层位）

12\. Monitor drags（监测阻力）

13\. Count the joints of casing remaining on deck before landing the casing at
the settling depth and compare this number against the amount in the hole and
amount received at the well site. This should confirm that the casing is set
at the proper
depth（在将套管坐到预设深度前，查钻台上剩余套管的根数。将这个数量与井底数量和现场收到数量进行对比。这样能确定套管是否下在合适的深度。）  
14\. Minimize the time between landing the casing and breaking circulation to
avoid plugging the float equipment（尽量减少座套管和循环中断之间的时间，从而避免堵塞浮箍、浮鞋等设备。）

15\. Land the casing such that it is at a safe height for installing the
cementing head, i.e., 4 feet - 5 feet above the rig floor if
possible（将套管坐放在安全高度，以便于安装水泥头。尽可能使套管顶端高出转台面4-5英尺。）  
16\. Circulate a minimum volume of 150% of the annulus/casing contents, once
circulation has been established（一旦循环建立，至少按照环空/套管容量的150%进行循环）.
