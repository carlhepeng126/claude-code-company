#  泥浆中气体(Mud Gas)分类

1.背景气:  指泥浆在裸眼井段循环时，单纯因循环作用所携带的气体总体含量  。

Background Gas: The general level of gas carried by the mud purely as a
function of circulating in an open hole.

2.钻井气  :由于实际钻进地层所进入泥浆的气体，即已钻岩石基质中所含的气体。  
Drilled Gas: Gas that has entered the mud due to the actual drilling of the
formation, i.e. the gas contained in the matrix of the rocks which have been
drilled.

3.单根气  :接单根时，由于当量循环密度丢失，和起钻时抽吸，导致静液压力减少，从而使气体进入泥浆，这样的气体叫接单根气。  
Connection Gas: The gas which enters the mud when a connection is made due to
a reduction in hydrostatic due to loss of ECD and due to swabbing while
pulling back.

4.抽吸气:  由于抽吸而进入井筒的气体。这可能由于起下钻所导致。  
Swabbed Gas: The gas which enters the well due to swabbing. This may be caused
by tripping

5.起下钻气  :在起下钻过程中所测量的进入泥浆的气体。  
Trip Gas: The gas which enters the mud during a trip is measured after a trip
has taken place.

6.停泵气  :由于停泥浆泵和消除井底静液压力中当量循环密度，而进入泥浆的气体。  
Pump Off Gas: The gas which enters the mud due to turning off the mud pumps
and removing ECD from the hydrostatic pressure on the bottom of the well.
