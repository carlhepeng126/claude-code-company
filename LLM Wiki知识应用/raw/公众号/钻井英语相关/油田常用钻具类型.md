1.Slick BHA(Bottom Hole Assembly)光屁股钻具

This type of bottom hole assembly (slick BHA) consists of a Drill Bit and
Drill Collar without Stabilizer. Slick BHA is suitable for formations which
have mild crooked hole tendencies. Slick bottom hole assemblies are seldom. As
there are no expensive components on this type of BHA, It is more easier to
fish such BHA.

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_jpg/7L78Sia7BSkb4JOAlbaiaovvttPO5tZyBRT2ajrOPbPXPepgJWw9mkth6YZXvibtibTic8rVunvh2gSy8t6oXPoQslA/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

这种钻具由钻头和钻挺构成，不包含稳定器。光屁股钻具适用于有中度弯曲井眼倾向的地层。光屁股钻具很少使用。因为这种钻具构件不贵，所以打捞这样钻具更容易。

2.Pendulum BHA(钟摆钻具)

The Pendulum BHA Assembly is one of the types of bottom hole assembly which we
can use primarily to reduce or maintain hole deviation. The pendulum technique
relies on the force of gravity to deflect the hole to vertical.  

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_jpg/7L78Sia7BSkb4JOAlbaiaovvttPO5tZyBRkIlX6Tj0F0Vibd54VlnxIVstXFRDK0QmkX90XSDKNo3pyNuXd4XKHqw/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

钟摆钻具是我们主要使用，用来降低井斜或维持井斜的一种类型工具之一。钟摆原理就是依靠重力力量，向垂直方向偏斜井眼。

3.Packed BHA(满眼钻具)

Relies on the principle that two points will contact and follow a sharp curve,
while three points will follow a straight line as shown . We can obtain three-
point stabilization by placing three or more Stabilizers in the portion of the
hole immediately above the bit.  

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_jpg/7L78Sia7BSkb4JOAlbaiaovvttPO5tZyBRibCmICmo6VHaic7X3LczA2icibFNoB6xzzCGicIAJAfqVIqbkbjzaOwNQ5g/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

该钻具依赖的就是这一原理：两点接触，会形成尖锐弧形轨迹，然而三点会形成图中所示直线轨迹。在钻头上部放置三个或多个稳定器，就能立即获得三点稳定性。
