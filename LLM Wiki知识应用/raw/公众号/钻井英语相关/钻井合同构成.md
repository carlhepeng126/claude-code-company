1.Form of Contract（合同格式）

包含bid sheet(招标文件)和drilling order(钻井指令)，构成master contract(主合同)。

2.Description of Parties(合同双方描述)

包括甲方（operator）和乙方（drilling contractor）定义以及respective obligations of
parties(合同双方各自义务)

3.Well Location(井位)

4.Well-hole Specification(井眼规格)

5.Time for Performance(履约时间)

6.Permits Required for Drilling(钻井所需许可)

7.Modification of Contract Term During the Course of Performance(履约期间合同条款变更)

8.Description of and Responsibility for Furnishing Equipment(合同双方提供设备责任描述)

9.Consideration to Be Paid(支付对价)

10.Drilling Methods and Practices(钻井方法和惯例)

11.Drilling Reports(各种钻井报告)

12.Insurance Requirement(保险要求)
