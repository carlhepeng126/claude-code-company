一、检测重要性

钻具在井底要承受极端的环境(extreme conditions),如高压(high pressure)、高温(high
temperature)、持续的机械应力(continuous mechanical
stress)。如果不早期识别的话，随着时间推移，这些因素会导致钻具磨损(wear)，疲劳(fatigue)甚至造成灾难性的故障(catastrophic
failures)。钻具检测能够预防意想不到的故障(preventing unexpected
failures)，确保现场人员和环境的安全(ensuring safety for on-site personnel and
environment)，将作业效率最大化(maximizing operational efficiency)，延长了设备的生命周期(extending
the lifespan of the equipment 。

二、检测频率及级别

初始检测:钻具是新的或是螺纹重新加工(Initial Inspection: Conducted when the pipe is new or after
re-threading.)

一级检测:每次使用前后，在现场进行目测检查(Category I Inspections: Visual inspections performed on-
site before and after each use.)

二级检测:每使用1000-2000小时候，或者按照使用要求进行更多的全面检查(Category II Inspections: More thorough
inspections conducted after 1,000 to 2,000 operating hours or as dictated by
usage.)

三级和四级检查:每年度或每使用5000小时候，进行综合性检测，例如无损检测(Category III and IV Inspections:
Comprehensive inspections, including non-destructive testing (NDT), performed
annually or after every 5,000 operating hours.)

三、检测方法

  * 磁粉探伤(Magnetic particle inspection) 
  * 超声波扫描(Ultrasonic scanning) 
  * 电磁检测(Electromagnetic inspection (EMI)) 
  * (液体渗透检测)Liquid penetrant inspection 
  * (湿磁粉检测)Wet magnetic particle inspection 
  * (目视检测)Visual Inspection 

  

四、检测内容

1.检查 钻杆上是否有任何磨损或损坏的迹象(Check for any signs of wear or damage on the drill pipe)  
2.检查接头处螺纹是否有任何损坏或腐蚀迹象(Inspect the connections for any signs of thread damage
or corrosion.)  
3.确定钻铤和稳定器完整性(Verify the integrity of the drill collars and stabilizers)  
4.检查钻头是否有任何磨损迹象(Examine the drill bit for any signs of wear or dullness)  
5.检查动力钻具 是否存在任何泄露或损坏(Inspect the mud motor for any leaks or damage)  
6.检查钻具附件，如接头和转换接头等的状况(Check the condition of the drill string accessories,
such as subs and crossovers)  
7.检验钻具是否正常工作，如震击器，减震器等。(Verify the proper functioning of the drilling tools,
including jars and shock absorbers)  
8.检查钻柱是否有任何弯曲或变形迹象(Inspect the drill string for any signs of bending or
deformation)  
9.确保所有螺纹连接处上扣正确、牢固可靠(Ensure that all connections are properly tightened and
secured)  
10.对整个钻具进行目视检测，查看是否存在任何异常或缺陷(Perform a visual inspection of the entire drill
string for any abnormalities or defects)
