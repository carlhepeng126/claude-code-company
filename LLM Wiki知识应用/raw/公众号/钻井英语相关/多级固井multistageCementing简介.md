#  多级固井(multi-stage Cementing)简介

一、定义

初次固井(Primary Cementing)，水泥浆泥浆液注压力过高，可能导致泥浆漏失(mud
losses)，地层破裂(fracturing)，水泥的返高(top of
cement)不会达到目的高度，这时就需要使用特殊的工具进行双级或多级固井作业。(two-stage or more stages)。

二、固井工艺(Cementing Procedures)

1.一级固井作业完成后，依据浮箍和浮鞋立即投入开启塞。记录开启时间。(  Drop the bomb (opening plug) immediately
after the first stage cement job according with floating equipment. Record the
opening time  )

2.打开双级/多级固井接箍。遵按照厂家的程序，确定分级工具打开压力和确切的开启塞坐封时间。(Open the two / multi stage
cementing collar. Follow the manufacturer’s procedure for the stage tool
opening pressure and approximate bomb landing time)

3.打开分级箍，用低泵速循环，控制泥浆液位。只有在确定不会因岩屑或水泥污染而导致形成桥堵，并且不会因分级箍以下地层破裂而影响循环的情况下，才可以提高泵速。With
the stage collar opened, start circulation with a low pump rate, keeping the
mud level under control. Increase the pump rate only when it is certain that
no cuttings or cement contamination will cause bridges and compromise the
circulation due to fracturing below the stage collar.

4.在循环期间和循环1周后，记录不同流量下的压力。During circulation and after bottom’s up, record the
pressures at each different flow rates.

5.按照固井设计准备第二阶段固井。Prepare for the second stage cement job as per the Cementing
Programme.

6.第一阶段固井水泥硬化时间一结束就开始进行第二阶段固井作业。Perform the second stage operation as soon as
the cement setting time of the first stage is complete.

7.在钻台布置带有双管线（泵送和反循环排出管线）的旁通管汇。Arrange the by-pass manifold at the rig floor
with double lines (pumping and reversing out).

8.按照套管悬挂计算的要求，用卡瓦吊卡使套管保持张力状态。Keep the casing in tension with the slip elevator
as required by casing hanging calculations.

9.第二阶段固井结束后，准备井口和防喷器吊装设备快速悬挂套管串。Prepare the wellhead (with partially made up
bolts) and Blowout Preventer lifting system to quickly hang the casing string
after the second stage cement job.

10.井内泥浆平衡良好，将关闭塞插入水泥头内。检查挡销、指示标志和循环管汇。With the mud well balanced, insert the
closing plug into the cementing head. Check the stop pin, indicating flag and
the circulation manifold.

11.泵入第一隔离液，泵入水泥浆。Pump the first cushion and pump the cement slurry.

12.投放关闭塞，并核实是否已释放。Launch the closing plug and verify its release.

13.顶替水泥浆。Displace the slurry.

14.配置地面泥浆系统，回收多余的水泥浆、隔离液和污染的泥浆。Configure the surface mud system to recover the
excess cement slurry, spacer, contaminated mud.

15.使用厂家说明书推荐的压力关闭分级箍(Stage collar)。试压后，逐渐泄压(bleed off pressure)。

16.用水冲洗井口和防喷器组所有入口Wash through all the inlets in the wellhead and BOP stack
with water.
