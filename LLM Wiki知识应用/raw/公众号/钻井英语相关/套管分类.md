#  套管分类

Casing has several important functions during the drilling and completing of a
well.

It is used to prevent the borehole from caving in during the drilling of a
well,to provide a means of controlling fluid encountered while drilling,to
prevent the contamination of fluid to be produced,and to

protect or isolate certain formations during the course of a well. Casing is
also one of the most expensive parts of a well,around 20% of the cost of a
completed well.

Casing is usually divided into five basic types.

在钻井和完井期间，套管具有重要的作用。在钻井期间，使用套管来预防井壁塌陷，钻井同时，提供钻井液控制方式，预防产液污染，在整个建井期间保护或封隔某些地层。套管也是一口井最昂贵的部分之一，套管费用大约占完井费用的百分之二十。套管通常分为5种基本类型。

Conductor Casing（导管套管）

Conductor pipe or drive pipe if it is hammer-driven to depth, is the first
string of casing to be used. The setting depth can vary from 10 ft to around
300 ft. The normal size range for conductor pipe is from 16 to 36 inches

(outside diameter).The conductor pipe must be large enough to allow the other
casing strings to be run through it.Purposes of conductor pipe are to:

导管是将使用的第一层套管串。导管坐深从10英尺到300英尺不等。导管正常尺寸范围是16到36英寸（外径）。导管必须大到其它套管串可以穿过。下导管的目的：

1)raise the level of circulating fluid so that fluid returns are possible.

2)prevent washouts in the near surface, normally unconsolidated formations.

1)提高钻井液循环水平，目的是钻井液可能返出。

2）预防近表面冲蚀，通常是未加固的地层

Surface Casing（表层套管）

The amount of surface casing used will depend on the depth of the
unconsolidated formations. Surface casing is usually set in the first
competent formation.

表层套管所用数量将取决于未加固地层深度。表套通常放在第一坚硬地层。

Normal size for surface casing is between 20 inch and 13-3/8 inch (outside
diameter). Since temperature, pressure and corrosive fluids tend to increase
with depth, different grades of casing will

be required to handle the different well conditions.

表套正常尺寸介于20英寸和13-3/8英寸之间（外径）。因为温度、压力和腐蚀液倾向于随着深度增加，所以需要不同等级套管来处理不同井情况。

Purposes of surface

casing are to:

•protect fresh water formations

•seal off unconsolidated formations and lost circulation zones

•protect “build” sections on deviated wells

•provide a place to install the B.O.P.'s

•provide for a sufficient “leak-off” test to be conducted

表套目的是:保护淡水层，封隔未加固的地层井漏段，保护斜井增斜段，提供安装防喷器的地方，提供充足地层漏失测试。

Intermediate Casing(技套)

Intermediate casing is set after surface casing, normally to seal off a

problem formation. The size of intermediate casing, will depend on the size

of the surface casing and the grade required to withstand the subsurface
conditions. Normal sizes are between 9 5/8 and 13 3/8 inch (outside

diameter).

技套下在表套之后，通常技套用来封隔问题地层。技套尺寸取决于表套尺寸和需要经受地表条件程度。正常技套尺寸是9 5/8到13 3/8英寸之间（外径）。

Production Casing(油套)

Production casing is usually the last full string of pipe set in a well. These

strings are run to isolate producing formations and provide for selective

production in multi-zone production areas.

The size of production casing

will depend on the expected production rate, the higher the barrel per day

production rate, the larger the inside diameter of the pipe. Common sizes

are between 3 and 7 inch (outside diameter).

油套通常是放置在井中最后完整管柱。下入这些管柱是为了封隔产层、在多层开采区域提供选择性开采。油套尺寸将取决于所期待产量。每日产量越高，管柱内径就越大。常规尺寸是3到7英寸之间（外径）。

Liner(尾管)

A liner is a string of casing that does not reach the surface. They are
usually “hung”

(attached to the intermediate casing using an arrangement of packers and
slips) from the base of the intermediate casing and reach to the bottom of the
hole. The major advantage of a liner is the cost of the string is reduced, as
are running and cementing times.During the course of the well,

if the liner has to be extended to the surface (making it another string of
casing)

the string attaching the liner to the surface is known as a “tie-back” string.

尾管是一串不需要接触地面的管柱。他们通常从技套底部悬挂（使用排列的封隔器和卡瓦连接到技套上），一直到达井底。尾管主要优势是降低管柱费用，同时也能减少下管柱和固井时间。在钻井期间，如果尾管必须延伸到地面（成为又一个套管串），将尾管连接到地面的管柱叫做回接管柱。
