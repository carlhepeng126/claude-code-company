Redrill Footage

Occasionally, a hole is lost or junked and a second hole may be drilled from
the surface in close proximity to the first. Footage drilled for the second
hole is defined as "redrill footage." Under these circumstances, the first
hole is reported as a dry hole (explanatory or developmental) and the total
footage is reported as dry hole footage. The second hole is reported as an oil
well, gas well, or dry hole according to the result. The redrill footage is
included in the appropriate classification of total footage, but is not
reported as a separate classification.
