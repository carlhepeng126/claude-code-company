一、定义

在油气井背景中，井筒干预是指在没有使用重大干预或大修的条件下，对井进行诊断、维护或提高井的生产性能而进行的一系列活动。这些干预，对于优化井的生产、解决特定的问题、确保井正常生产，起到了关键作用。大修通常涉及重大干预，与大修不同，井筒干预从本质上来说对井的干预是很小，侧重于提高井的产量和效率(Well
intervention in the context of oil and gas wells refers to a set of activities
aimed at diagnosing, maintaining, or enhancing the performance of a well
without resorting to major interventions or workovers. These interventions are
crucial for optimizing production, addressing specific issues, and ensuring
the continued functionality of the well. Unlike major workovers, which involve
substantial interventions, interventions are generally minor in nature and
focus on improving well productivity and efficiency)

二、常见的井筒干预活动

1.增产(Well Stimulation)

增产包括不同工艺，水利压裂和酸化是两个突出的例子。水利压裂是将高压液体射入到储层，从而产生裂缝，提高了储层的联通性和渗透性。酸化是将酸射入到井内，从而溶解阻碍液体流动的材料，提高井的产量(Well
stimulation involves various techniques, with hydraulic fracturing (fracking)
and acidizing being prominent examples. Hydraulic fracturing entails injecting
high-pressure fluids into the reservoir to create fractures, enhancing
reservoir connectivity and permeability. Acidizing involves the injection of
acid into the well to dissolve materials obstructing fluid flow and improve
well productivity)

2.测井、诊断

测井、诊断是指在井下安放特殊的工具和仪器。这些工具收集有关储层性质、井筒条件、流体特性的数据。通过收集的数据对油井当前状况进行深入了解，有助于储层评估，优化生产策略(Well
logging and diagnostics involve the deployment of specialized tools and
instruments downhole. These tools gather data on reservoir properties,
wellbore conditions, and fluid characteristics. The collected data aids in
reservoir evaluation and helps optimize production strategies by providing
insights into the well’s current status)

3.维护和维修

维护和维修涵盖维护井的正常生产所开展的通常活动。这包括洗井，去除碎屑和结垢，更换损坏或磨损的设备，解决小的机械问题。随着时间推移，对井进行定期维护，从而预防更大的问题发生，维护井的生产效率，这样做是非常有必要的(Maintenance
and servicing interventions encompass routine activities aimed at preserving
the well’s functionality. This includes cleaning the wellbore to remove debris
and scale, replacing damaged or worn-out equipment, and addressing minor
mechanical issues. Regular maintenance is essential to prevent larger problems
and maintain the well’s efficiency over time)

三、与修井作业的区别

油气井干预(well intervention)一般是无钻机条(rigless)件下作业。而修井作业都是在有钻机或(drilling
rig)修井机(workover rig)条件下进行作业。一般well intervention在workovers之前进行。如果well
intervention不能解决，然后再进行workovers。
