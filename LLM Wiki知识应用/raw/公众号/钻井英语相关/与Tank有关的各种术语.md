#  与Tank有关的各种术语

钻井专业中，会看到许多不同术语都与tank有关。但是在这些术语中，tank不都是表示罐的意思。今天小编给大家整理一下，供大家一同学习。

1.Active tank（循环池）

This is the tank from which drilling fluid is pumped downhole and to which
drilling fluid returns from the hole is received.(钻井液从该池泵入井下，从井口返回该池)

2.Reserve tank(储备罐)

These are used for storing fluids according to requirements. Reserve mud is
usually prepared or stored in these tanks or different fluids in preparation
for the next phase.(根据要求，这些罐用于存储液体。储备泥浆通常储备在这些罐中。或准备不同泥浆用于下一阶段)

3\. Trip tank(起下钻罐)

The Trip Tank is a tall, narrow tank located near the flow line and used for
accurately monitoring drill pipe displacement volumes while tripping pipe in
or out of the hole. （起下钻罐是位于泥浆管线附近又高又窄的罐，起下钻同时，用于精确监测钻具排替量）

4.Stripping tank(强行起下钻罐)

The Stripping Tank is smaller than the Trip Tank and is used for monitoring
pipe displacement volumes when stripping in or out of the hole when the
Annular Bag Diverter is closed.（强行起下钻罐比起下钻罐小，当环形防喷器分流器关闭，强行起下钻时，用于监测钻具排替量）。

4.Settling tank(沉沙罐)

又叫Sand Trap.也是沉沙罐的意思。It is usually positioned below the shale
shakers(通常位于振动筛下方)

5.Fuel tank(燃油罐)

6.Mud tank(泥浆罐)

7\. Suction tank(吸入池)

8\. Chemical tank(化学药品罐)

9.Sludge tank/Pill Tank(加重泥浆池)

These tanks are more minor than active and reserve tanks. They are used for
preparing “slugs” (high-density mud that is pumped into the drill string
before tripping out of the hole) and pills to address particular
situations(这些罐通常比循环罐和储备罐小。用来准备重泥浆。也就是起钻前泵入到钻具内的高比重泥浆。用来解决特殊情况。）

10.Diesel tank(柴油罐)

11\. Water tank(水罐)
