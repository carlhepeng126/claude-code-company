#  钻机动力系统（Rig Power System）

1.The power system on a drilling rig provides the power for the other main
systems on the rig and other ancillary systems, such as electrical systems,
pumps, etc. The system typically consists of a prime mover (the component of
the power system that generates the raw power) and a means to transmit the raw
power to the end-use components on the
rig.(钻机动力系统为钻机其它主要系统和其它辅助系统提供动力，例如电气系统、泥浆泵等。动力系统通常包括原动机（原动机是动力系统的构件，能够产生原动力)和将原动力传输到钻机终端使用构件的方式。）  
2.The power system is comprised of:  
the Fuel Storage  
the Engines and Generators

(动力系统由存储的燃油和柴油机和发电机组成)

The transmission of the power can be:  
（1）mechanical;  
（2）direct current (DC) electrical generator;  
（3）alternating current (AC) electrical generator with silicon-controlled
rectifier (SCR) to direct current (DC);

(动力的传输方式可以为：1.机械传动；2.直流发电机；3.交流发电机-整流器-直流)

3.In the mechanical transmission, power is generated with the prime mover and
is transmitted to the end-use components by the application of chains and
sprockets (similar to a bicycle), drive belts, drive shafts, etc. In a direct
current (DC) electrical system, an internal combustion engine operates an
electrical generator (in this case a DC generator) and the electrical energy
is transmitted to the motors, electrical actuators, etc. Finally, in an
alternating current (AC) electrical system, an internal combustion engine
operates an electrical generator (in this case an AC generator) which is
converted to DC with a silicon-controlled rectifier (SCR). AC-SCR power
systems are the most widely used power systems on modern drilling
rigs.（机械传输方式，原动机产生动力，通过应用链条、齿轮、驱动皮带、驱动轴等将动力传递到终端使用构件上。在直流电气系统中，内燃机操作发电机（在这种情况下是直流发电机）。电能传递到电机、电气执行机构等。最终，在交流电气系统中，内燃机操作发电机（在这种情况下是交流发电机），通过整流器，交流转换成直流。交流-
整流器动力系统是现代钻机最广泛使用的动力系统。
