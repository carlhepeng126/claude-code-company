The blowout preventer or BOP is a safety device installed on an oil and gas
well to monitor and control pressure spikes during a kick or in the drilling
process. Kicks in the pressure of the well can cause dangerous or even
catastrophic failures known as a blowout. Blowout preventers activate various
types of hydraulic rams that will close off the wellbore or shear the
equipment downhole during a kick in pressure.  

防喷器也叫BOP，是安装在油气井内的安全设备，在井涌或钻进过程中，能够监测和控制压力突增。在井的压力下，井涌能导致危险或灾难性的后果，我们称之为井喷。在井涌期间，防喷器启动不同类型的液压闸板，这些闸板能够关闭井口或切断井下的设备。

在钻井日报中，有单独的防喷器内容部分。具体如下：

1.Last BOP Test(上一次防喷器测试)

Date the last time BOP pressure tests were conducted; BOP test intervals
should be a maximum of every 21 days  

上次进行防喷器压力测试的日期。防喷器测试每次最佳间隔期就是21天。

2.Last BOP Drill(上一次防喷演习)

Date the last time a drill to simulate a kick in pressure was conducted by the
drilling supervisor  

上一次钻井监督指导进行的模拟井涌的演习日期。

3.Next BOP Test(下一次防喷器测试)

Date of the next scheduled pressure test of the blowout preventer  

下一次计划的防喷器压力测试日期。
