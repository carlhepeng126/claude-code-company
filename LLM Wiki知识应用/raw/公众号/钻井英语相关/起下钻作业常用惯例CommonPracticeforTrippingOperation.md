1\. Preparation  (准备工作)  
The Drilling Supervisor shall approve and provide written instructions to the
Contractor Toolpusher prior to any trip out of the hole. These instructions
shall include the:  
钻井监督应在任何起钻作业前，批准并向井队带班队长给出书面指令。指令内容包括：  
1.sequence of operations i.e. flow check, circulate bottoms up, drop survey,
etc.

作业顺序，如溢流检查、循环一周、投测等。  
2.maximum allowed overpull

可允许的最大过提

3.procedures if tight hole is encountered

如果遇阻，解阻程序

4.maximum running/pulling speeds

最大上提/下放速度  
5.Trip sheet

灌浆记录表

6.preparation of equipment for the following operation when out of the hole

起出井眼时，准备以下作业所需的设备

Pipe Tally  （钻具记录表）  
The drilling contractor shall ensure that a master drill pipe tally book is
maintained at all times. This shall include stand number, single number,
single identification, drill pipe grade, single length, stand length and total
length.  
钻井承包商应确保实时维护主钻具记录表。维护内容包括立柱数量、单根数量、单根标识、钻杆等级、单根长度、立柱长度和总长度。  
Trip Tank  （补给罐）  
Prior to pulling out of the hole, the trip tank shall be filled. The fill-up
pump to the trip tank should never be left running while tripping out.

起钻前，补给罐应灌泥浆。当起钻时，与补给罐相连的灌浆泵应停止运转。  
Circulation  （循环）  
For any trip out of the hole, a minimum of one bottoms up circulation, or
appropriate circulation for deviated wells, shall be performed.  
When circulating to condition mud, a circulating rate of 50 - 75% of the
normal circulating rate shall be used.  
A trip sheet shall be filled out by the Driller and Mud logging contractor for
each trip out of the hole.

对于任何起钻作业，对于斜井而言进行至少一周循环或进行适当的循环。当循环调节泥浆时，循环速度应为正常循环速度的百分之50-70。每次起钻，应由司钻或录井承包商填写灌浆记录表。  
2 - General Guidelines（总体规程）  
Responsibilities（职责）  
The Drilling Supervisor shall be on the rig floor for the first 10 stands
during tripping out to observe for correct hole fill, overpull, etc. He shall
also be on the rig floor for the last five stands when tripping in, tight hole
problems, testing mud motors & MWD, RIH across liner hanger and when casing 3
stands off bottom.  
在起钻期间，起前10柱，钻井监督应在钻台上观察井眼灌注是否正确以及过提等。当下钻时和下入套管离井底3柱时，钻井监督也要在钻台上观察最后5柱的情况，对动力钻具和随钻测量设备进行测试，下钻具穿过尾管挂。  
Pipe Movements（钻具移动）  
Care shall be exercised to minimise surge/swab pressures by controlling the
speed of pipe movements.

注意控制钻具移动速度，将激动/抽汲压力降到最低。  
Pull the pipe carefully and check for swabbing. In the event of the hole
swabbing, the pipe shall be run back to bottom and the hole circulated bottoms
up. Mudloggers shall run the swab programme prior to each round trip to
determine the maximum trip speed.

小心起钻，检查是否有抽汲。如果井眼发生抽汲，钻具应下回到井底，循环井眼一周。在每一次起下钻前，录井工应进行抽汲测试，从而确定最大的起下钻速度。  
Notes: A hole is swabbing whenever the volume of fluid required to fill the
hole is less than the volume of steel pulled out of the hole. THE HOLE CAN BE
SWABBING WITHOUT FLOW AT THE FLOWLINE.  
备注：只要灌入井眼的泥浆量小于起出钻具排开的体积时，井眼就发生了抽汲。泥浆在管线中没有流动情况下，井眼发生抽汲。  
Circulation, Hole Fill, and Pipe Fill（循环，灌井眼，灌管柱）  
During tripping operations, the hole shall be kept full at all times.

在起下钻期间，井眼应一直保持充满泥浆。  
The pipe shall be filled every 10 stands while running in the hole and the
displacement checked.

下钻时，每10柱灌一次浆，并检查替代量。  
When pulling the pipe, the hole shall be kept full from the trip tank. The
hole shall be checked regularly to ensure that it is taking the correct amount
of fluid.

每次起钻时，应使用补给罐使井眼充满泥浆。定期检查井眼，确保井眼中泥浆量正确。  
The volume required to fill the hole every 3-5 stands shall be recorded in the
Trip Sheet until the bit is out of the hole.

在钻头从井中起出前，每起3到5柱灌浆一次，并在灌浆记录中记录每次灌浆的量。  
Tight Hole / Bridging Conditions（遇阻/砂桥堵情况）  
If tight hole or bridging conditions occur during trips, the pipe shall be
pulled back to the nearest stand and reaming/washing commenced in accordance
with stuck pipe procedure.  
If tight spots occur while tripping out, the top drive or kelly shall be
engaged to commence reaming and circulating out.

如果起下钻期间，发生遇阻或砂桥堵情况，应把钻具起到 最近立柱处，按照卡钻处理程序进行
划眼/洗井作业。在起钻时，如果遇阻，应接上顶驱或方钻杆，开始进行划眼和循环作业。  
Reaming trips might be required prior to running LWD/density tools and/or 7in
liner.

在下随钻测井/密度工具或7"尾管前，需要进行划眼作业。  
Directional Wells  （定向井）  
While tripping out of the hole from below the surface casing in directional
wells:  
在定向井中，从表套下部起钻：  
reciprocate at all times and rotate the pipe at intervals while circulating
and conditioning the mud prior to POOH  
record the pick-up, slack-off and rotating weight indicator readings with the
mud pump off. Note: These values shall be used to monitor changes in drag
during pulling out.  

在循环和调节泥浆时，要一直往复、旋转钻具。起钻前，记录停泵时上提、下放、和旋转指重表读数。备注：这些数值是用来监测起钻期间阻力的变化。  
Flow Checks（溢流检查）  
While tripping flow checks shall be taken:  
在以下起下钻情况时，应进行溢流检查：  
just off bottom

离开井底时  
at the cement casing shoe

固套管鞋时

prior to pulling drill collars through BOP stack

上提钻铤穿过防喷器组前  
after BHA out of horizontal section

钻具出了水平段后  
while RIH after drilling out shoe

钻穿浮鞋后，同时下钻时  
while RIH before BHA enters horizontal section.

在钻具进入水平段前，下钻时  
3 - Requirements When Out Of Hole  （提出井眼要求）  

Time Out of the Hole（提出井眼时间）  
The time spent with the pipe out of the hole shall be minimised wherever
possible. Operations such as routine repairs and slipping and cutting of the
drill-line shall be performed with pipe at the casing shoe whenever possible.  
应尽可能缩短钻具提出井眼的时间。常规维修、滑大绳、切大绳等作业，应尽可能在钻具位于套管鞋处时进行  
Requirements When Out of Hole（起钻时要求）  
The trip tank volume indicator shall be set at 1/2 barrel gain or loss
whenever the trip tank is being circulated on the hole.  
循环补给罐时，补给罐体积指示器应设置为+-1/2桶。  
Whenever the blind rams are closed, the Driller and the Assistant Driller
shall open the rams and check the fluid level at least every half hour, fill
the hole as required and report any losses to the Drilling Supervisor. (Before
opening blind ram open choke)  
只要全封闸板关闭，司钻和副司钻应打开闸板，至少每半小时检查一次泥浆液位，按要求灌井眼，将任何漏失汇报给钻井监督（在打开全封和节流阀前）  
During logging operations, the trip tank shall be circulated continuously to
keep the hole full. The volumes shall be recorded every 15 minutes and any
losses reported to the Drilling Supervisor.  
测井作业期间，应持续循环补给罐，从而使井眼充满泥浆。每15分钟记录一次灌浆量，并将发生的任何漏失报告给钻井监督。  
Whenever the mud pumps are turned off with the pipe still in hole, the trip
tank shall be circulated continuously. The volume shall be recorded every 15
minutes and any losses reported to the Drilling Supervisor.  
只要一关泵钻具在井中时，应持续循环补给罐。每15分钟记录一次灌浆量，并将发生的任何漏失报告给钻井监督  
4 - Wiper Trips and Check Trips（通井和起下钻检查）  
Wiper Trips（通井）  
Wiper trips shall be run at the discretion of the Drilling Supervisor and
Drilling Contractor Toolpusher:  
根据钻井监督和钻井承包商带班队长的决定，有以下的状况，进行通井作业：  
during logging when hole conditions deteriorate and become sticky after
logging and before running casing when hole conditions deteriorate and become
stickybefore RFT tools are run  
to push junk down the hole

测井期间，井眼状况变差；测井后，井眼变卡；在下套管前，井眼状况变差；在下入地层测试工具向井下推动落物前，井眼变卡。  
after 500-1000 ft of hole has been drilled

井眼已经钻了500-1000英尺后  
Note: In general, wiper trips shall be made to the shoe. However, when there
are no indications of hole problem but there is a major change in parameters,
a short trip of 10-15 stands shall be made.  
备注：一般来说通井通到套管鞋处。但是，当井眼中没有问题显示，而是参数有了重大变化，应进行短起下10-15柱。  
Check Trips（起下钻检查）  
Check trips shall be run at the discretion of the Drilling Supervisor and
Drilling Contractor Toolpusher but shall be run:  
由钻井监督和钻井承包商带班队长决定是否进行起下钻检查，但是下面情况下必须进行检查：  
whenever significant torque/drag increase is noted before running casing if
hole conditions during logging indicate that this is necessary  
during logging when mud overbalance is limited/low or there is gas cutting of
mudwhen the bit has been on bottom for 24 hours.

在测井期间，如果井眼状况表明需要进行检查；下套管前，注意到明显的扭矩/阻力的增加；测井期间，泥浆过平衡受到限制/变低，或者钻头离开井底24小时，有泥浆气侵显示。  
For casing check trips, the BHA shall include full gauge stabilisers and be at
least equal in stiffness to the casing string. MWD’s, NMDC’s and other
expensive BHA components shall not be run.  
下套管起下钻检查，钻具应包括通径稳定器，钻具刚性至少与套管串的刚性相同。随钻测量设备、无磁钻铤和其它贵重的钻具构件不应入井。  
The following table contains the basic guideline procedure to be followed when
making a check trip:  
进行起下钻检查时，应按照下表所包含的基本规程去做。  
1\. Circulate bottoms up  
循环一周  
2\. Flow check for 15 minutes  
溢流检查15分钟  
3\. Slowly pull 10-15 stands while using the trip tank to ensure that the hole
is taking the correct quantity of mud  
缓慢上提10-15柱，同时使用补给罐，确保井眼充满泥浆的量正确。  
4\. Flow check  
溢流检查  
5\. Run back to bottom, check for fill and for flow again  
下入井底，检查是否灌浆，是否再次流动。  
6\. Circulate bottoms up and condition the mud  
循环一周，调节泥浆  
7\. Check mud returns for gas and salinity and report to the Drilling
Supervisor  
检查返出泥浆是否有气体和含有盐分，并报告给钻井监督  
Note : If a well demonstrates a tendency to swab, pumping out of the hole
maybe necessary.  
备注：如果一口井显示出抽汲倾向，有必要  
5 - Making Connections（接单根）  
The following guideline procedure shall be followed for all drilling
operations in order to minimise the time during which the drill string is
motionless:  
为了将钻具静止时间降到最少，所以在整个钻井作业中，必须遵循以下规程。  
1\. Drill the stand/joint down to the desired point but DO NOT drill-off the
WOB or stop the pumps  
向下钻一柱/根到满意点，但是不要减少钻压或停泵  
2\. Reduce the rotary speed to 20-30 RPM  
转速降到20-30转/分钟。  
3\. Begin upward movement of the pipe while maintaining 20-30 RPM rotation  
保持20-30转/分钟转速同时，向上移动钻具。  
4\. Pull one stand/joint of pipe above the rotary to wipe all newly drilled
hole  
起一柱/一根钻具到钻盘的上部，从而对新钻井眼进行通井作业。  
5\. Lower the pipe and REAM the stand/joint down, if necessary  
如果必要的话，下放钻具，划一柱/一根。  
Note : The Directional Driller shall be notified before any reaming is carried
out  
备注：在进行任何划眼作业前，应通知定向人员。  
6\. Repeat Steps 4-5 until drag and torque are acceptable  
重复步骤4-5直到阻力和扭矩可接受。  
7\. Lower the drill string without rotation and observe drag  
不旋转情况下，下放钻具，观察阻力  
8\. Set the slips and break the tool joint to make connection  
坐卡瓦，卸钻杆接头，接单根。  
9\. Pick-up the new stand/joint of pipe and make the connection  
吊新立柱/新钻杆，接单根。  
10\. Start the pumps, lift the pipe and remove the slips  
开泵，上提钻具，去掉卡瓦。  
11\. When the mud pumps are near required speed, begin rotation, lower the
pipe and resume drilling  
当泥浆泵接近要求速度时，开始旋转、下放钻具，恢复钻进。  
12\. When the pipe is in the open hole, always set the slips on a down stroke
long enough to ensure that the pipe is actually moving down the hole when the
slips are set; never set the slips with drill pipe tension on the BHA.

当钻具处于裸眼井段时，务必在下放钻具行程中放置卡瓦，且下放行程需足够长，以确保放置卡瓦时钻具确实在向井底移动；严禁在下部钻具组合（BHA）承受钻杆拉力的情况下放置卡瓦。
