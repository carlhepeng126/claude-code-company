MGS Mud seal is used to maintain the internal pressure of Mud Gas Separator
(MGS). The parameters used to estimate the internal pressure of MGS are
simulated through the gas kick and Geometry of MGS.

液气分离器回浆管线液封是用来维持液气分离器内部压力。通过气侵或液气分离器几何图形，对用于预估液气分离器内部压力的参数进行模拟。

重点词汇：

1.Mud Gas Seperator(MGS)-液气分离器

2.Gas Kick-气侵

3.Mud Seal-液气分离器回浆管线液封
