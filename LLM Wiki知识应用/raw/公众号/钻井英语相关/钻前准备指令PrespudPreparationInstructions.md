#  钻前准备指令（Pre-spud Preparation Instructions）

_2026年04月02日 06:01_ __ _ _ _ _ _ 黑龙江  _

去阅读

1.Prepare Grounding, RCD’s.

完成电气接地系统及旋转控制设备的准备工作。  
2.Share all new tubulars, BHA and XO’s 3rd party’s inspection certificates.

提供所有新的管柱、钻具和转换接头三方检查证书  
3.Security Audit.

安全审查  
4.Health & Hygiene inspection.

健康和卫生检查  
5.Camp & catering audit.

营房和餐饮审查  
6.Derrick Drop Survey after raising the mast.

起井架后，井架落物检查  
7.Confirm Derrick alignment with big size of BHA parts.

使用大尺寸钻具确保井架居中  
8.Close out critical items in last updated CAR.

关闭上一次更新的整改事项列表中的关键项  
9.Test & confirm the integrity of Draw works & TDS.

测试并确保绞车和顶驱完整性  
10.Confirm that tubular & Handling equipment are being inspected as per
schedule.

确保按照计划进行管柱和管柱移动设备检查  
11.BOP Stump test Annular Preventer tested with largest and smallest Drill
Pipe OD to be used in the well program.

使用最大和最小外径钻杆对环形封井器进行测试  
12.Pressure test choke manifold, BOPs and surface lines with 5000 psi.

5000psi压力对节流管汇、防喷器和地面管线进行试压  
13.Fill out pre-spud check list, and final confirmation will be under CM
approval.

填写钻前检查表，最后确认要经过营地经理同意  
14.Ensure the correct nozzles supplied and addressed to 26’’ PDC bit.

确保提供的水眼尺寸正确，并适用于26’’PDC钻头

15.Ensure all required material, services and equipment are on location for
the first and second sections.

确保一开和二开所需的材料、服务和设备就位。  
16.Install 6-1/2” liners in the mud pumps, ensure backup 6” (to be used in 17
½” & 12 ¼” Sections) and 5 ½” liners, swabs, and spares on location.

安装泥浆泵6-1/2缸套，确保现场有备用的6"(在17 ½” 和12 ¼” 井段使用)和5 ½”缸套、活塞和相关备件。

17.The water well delivery should be between 25-30 m3/hr., Rig engineer to
confirm and to check the water pit linear for any physical damage.

水井供水量保持在25-30方每小时，钻井工程师确认并检查水池内衬是否有外在损坏  
18.Mark cellar to reflect wellhead and Xmas tree orientation.

标记圆井，以指示井口和采油树的朝向  
19.Prepare 5 ½’’ DP’s, HWDP and rest of BHA part as per program on the rack,
inspect and measure.

在按照设计准备好5 ½’’钻杆、加重钻杆和剩余钻具，并对其进行检查、测量  
20.Installed 20“slip-on riser to 20" conductor.

将20“卡瓦式升高短接安装到20"导管上

21.Prepare Racks for 20’’ CSG joints to be ready for inspection by 3rd party.

准备20"套管支架，以备第三方检查  
22.P/U & M/U 5 ½’’ S-135 DP and rack back on derrick, Inspect & clean threads,
drifted each single DP before MU and M/U to recommended S-DP MU torque.

吊起连接5 ½’’ S-135钻杆，并将钻杆摆放在钻台立根盒上，检查清洗螺纹，在上扣前对每根钻杆进行通径，按照推荐的S-135钻杆上扣扭矩上扣

24.Record rotating hrs. of Jars, HWDP, DCs and X-Overs on DDR for each run.

记录每一趟钻震击器、加重钻杆、钻铤和转换接头的旋转时间  
25.Pick up and make up 26" BHA components as per program and rack back the
same.

按照设计配26"钻具，并摆放在钻台立根盒上  
26.The use of “Witches Hat” style DP screen is banned, ensure inline screens
and suction screens on mud pumps are cleaned and installed.

禁止使用女巫帽式钻杆滤网，确保泥浆泵上高压滤网和吸入滤网进行清洗并安装

  

预览时标签不可点
