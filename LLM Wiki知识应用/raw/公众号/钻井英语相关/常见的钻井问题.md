1.Pipe Sticking(卡钻)

During drilling operations, a pipe is considered stuck if it cannot be freed
and pulled out of the hole without damaging the pipe and without exceeding the
drilling rig ’ s maximum allowed hook load.

在钻井作业中，在不损害管柱、不超出钻机最大允许钩载情况下，如果管柱不能自由移动从井底起出，那么管柱就视为卡钻。

2.Loss of Circulation(井漏)

Lost circulation is defined as the uncontrolled flow of whole mud into a
formation, sometimes referred to as thief zone.  

井漏是指泥浆不受控的流入到地层，有时候也叫做漏失层。

3.Hole Deviation(井斜)

Hole deviation is the unintentional departure of the drill bit from a
preselected borehole trajectory.  

井斜是指钻头无意识的偏离预设井眼轨迹。

4.Drill Pipe Failures(钻具失效)

Drillpipe failures can be put into one of the following categories: twistoff
caused by excessive torque; parting because of excessive tension; burst or
collapse because of excessive internal pressure or external pressure,
respectively; or fatigue as a result of mechanical cyclic loads with or
without corrosion.  

钻具失效可以分为以下类别：过度扭矩导致脱扣；过度张力导致裂开；过度内压或外压导致钻具破裂或挤压；在有或没有腐蚀情况下机械循环载荷导致的钻具疲劳。

5.Borehole Instability(井壁不稳定)

Borehole instability is the undesirable condition of an openhole interval that
does not maintain its gauge size and shape and/or its structural integrity.  

井壁不稳定是裸眼井段不理想的情况，即该裸眼井段不能维持现有井径、形状以及井眼结构的整体性。

6.Mud Contamination(泥浆污染)

A mud is said to be contaminated when a foreign material enters the mud system
and causes undesirable changes in mud properties, such as density, viscosity,
and filtration.  

当外物进入泥浆体系，导致泥浆的密度、粘度和滤失量等性能产生了不理想的变化，这就是所说的泥浆污染。

7.Producing Formation Damage(产层破坏)

Producing formation damage has been defined as the impairment of the unseen by
the inevitable, causing an unknown reduction in the unquantifiable. In a
different context, formation damage is defined as the impairment to reservoir
(reduced production) caused by wellbore fluids used during drilling/completion
and workover operations.  

产层破坏是指不可避免的事导致的不可见的损害，在不可量化角度看，导致了未知产量的减少。在另一个不同背景下，产层破坏可以指，在钻井、完井、修井作业中，使用的井筒液体导致的对油藏的损害（产量减少）。

8.Hole Cleaning(清洗井眼)

Inadequate hole cleaning can lead to costly drilling problems such as
mechanical pipe sticking, premature bit wear, slow drilling, formation
fracturing, excessive torque and drag on drillstring, difficulties in logging
and cementing, and difficulties in casings landing.  

清洗井眼不充分，能够产生代价高的钻井问题，如机械卡钻、钻头提前磨损、钻速变慢、地层破裂、钻柱上的扭矩和阻力过大、测井固井困难以及下套管困难。

  

9.Hydrogen Sulfide Bearing Zone and Shallow Gas(含硫化氢层和浅层气)

Drilling H2S-bearing formations poses one of the most difficult and dangerous
problems to humans and equipment.Shallow gas may be encountered at any time in
any region of the world.  

钻遇含硫化氢地层，是其中一个会对人员和设备带来最困难和最危险的问题之一。浅层气在世界上任何地区任何时间都可能遇到。
