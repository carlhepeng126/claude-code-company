Power Tongs(动力大钳)

Power tongs are pneumatically or hydraulically operated tools that serve to
spin the pipe up tight and, in some instances, to apply the final makeup
torque.

Tubing Tongs（油管钳）

Tubing tongs are used to break out and make up tubing. They may be operated
manually, hydraulically, or pneumatically.

Casing Tongs(套管钳)

Casing tongs are used for turning when making up or breaking out casing.

Spinning Chain Tongs（旋转链钳）

Spinning chain is a y-shaped chain that is used to spin up (tighten) one joint
of drill pipe into another. One end of the chain is attached to the tongs.

Power Rod Tongs(动力抽油杆钳)

Power rod tongs are tongs that are actuated by air or hydraulic fluid and are
used for making up or breaking out sucker rods.

Chisel Tongs(凿子钳)

Chisel tongs are pipe tongs that grip the pipe with a chisel-like insert in
the jaw of the wrench.

Chain Tongs（链钳）

Chain tongs are a hand tool consisting of a handle and chain that resembles
the chain on a bicycle. In general, chain tongs are used for turning pipe.  

Mechanical Tongs（机械钳）

Webb-Wilson are mechanical tongs; sometimes used generically for all brands of
tongs.
