#  页岩油开采及压裂开采方法的优缺点

8月25日，中国石油公布大庆油田古龙页岩油勘探取得重大战略性突破，新增石油预测地质储量12.68亿吨。这对于建设百年油田以及保障国家能源需求，具有重大意义。今天我们的双语文章了解一下页岩油有关的知识。  

Shale oil is a high-quality crude oil that lies between layers of shale rock,
impermeable mudstone, or siltstone. Oil companies produce shale oil by
fracturing the rock formations that contain the layers of oil.

页岩油是孕藏在页岩、不渗透的泥岩或粉砂岩层间的高质原油。各大油公司通过对含油层的岩层进行压裂，从而开采页岩油。

Since 2014, U.S. shale oil has created a boom in domestic crude oil
production.Shale oil comprises more than a third of the onshore production of
crude oil in the lower 48 states. As a result, the United States became the
world’s largest crude-oil producer, according to the Energy Information
Administration.U.S. reliance on foreign oil imports has fallen from 9.8
million barrels per day in 2013 to 8.6 million b/d in 2019.  

自2014年以来，美国的页岩油造就了国内原油生产热潮。页岩油占美国本土48个州陆上原油产量的1/3以上。据能源信息署报道，美国由此成为了世界上最大的原油生产国。美国对外国石油进口依赖已经从2013年每日980万桶下降到2019年的每日860万桶。

First, oil companies drill vertically into the shale formation.After operators
drill the well, they curve it at a 90-degree angle. At this kickoff point,
they drill horizontally.  

首先，石油公司垂直钻入页岩层。然后作业者钻入一定深度，开始将井眼弯曲90度，在造斜点开始水平钻进。

This technique allows them to access 10,000 feet of the reservoir rock.They
then pump high-pressure bursts of water, sand, and chemicals to fracture the
shale and release the oil. The sand holds the fractures open. That allows the
oil to seep into the well.  

该技术允许进入10，000英尺储层。然后泵入高压水流、砂岩、化学药品，从而破裂页岩释放石油。泵入页岩中沙粒可以打开页岩裂缝，这样可以使石油渗入到井中。

Companies in Texas used horizontal drilling in the 1990s.It became more
affordable when Brigham Oil & Gas successfully split a single horizontal
leg.The company fracked each leg independently, providing a higher return on
the investment. This technique propelled North Dakota field production from 7
million barrels in January 2010 to 46 million in October 2019.  

德克萨斯州公司在20世纪90年代使用水平钻井技术。宾汉姆油气公司成功对水平井单个分支进行压裂作业，该技术变得更加适用。该公司单独对每个分支进行压裂，这带来了更高的投资回报。该技术推动北达科他州油田产量从2010年1月100万桶增至2019年10月4600万桶。

Shale oil extraction methods are more flexible than traditional oil well
drilling. The initial drilling only accounts for 40% of the total cost.
Extracting the oil costs roughly $1 million for each well. That made shale oil
extraction profitable when oil reached $100 a barrel.  

页岩油开采方法相比于传统石油钻井方法，更加灵活。初始钻井费用只占总费用的40%。每口井的开采费用大致为一百万美元。当油价达到100美元每桶时，开采页岩油就相当有利可图。

Fracking is controversial for three reasons. First, it uses a lot of natural
resources. Second, fracking can affect drinking water.Fracking may also cause
earthquakes.  

因为以下三个原因，压裂开采一直备受争议。首先，该方法需要使用很多自然资源。其次，压裂开采会影响引用水。而且压裂会导致地震。
