Conductor Casing

Initial casing string to add stability; typically short and can be added
before the drilling rig moves onsite;

导管

初始套管柱，用来增加井身稳定性。通常很短，在钻机移到现场前，可以安装。

Surface Casing

Larger diameter pipe set to protect freshwater formations from contamination;
blowout preventer or BOP is typically attached to the surface casing.

表套

下入大直径管柱，防止淡水层污染。防喷器通常连接到表套上。

Intermediate Casing

Protects wellbore stability against pressure abnormalities in the formation
and potential wellbore collapses.

技套

保护井壁稳定，不受地层压力异常和潜在的井壁坍塌的影响。

Production Casing

Typically, this is the final casing string set to the TMD (total measured
depth) of the wellbore across the reservoir. Production casing may be
perforated during the completion process or have other completions components
installed on it.

油套

通常来说，这是穿过油层下入到完钻井身处的最后套管串。在完井过程中，对油套射孔，或者在油套上安装其它完井构件。

OD

This is the outside diameter measurement of the casing. Sizes are standardized
in the industry and displayed in inches, even in counties where the metric
system is used in drill and complete operations.  

外径

这是测量的套管外部直径。在行业中，标准的外径尺寸是以英寸表示，甚至有些国家在钻井完井作业中使用公制系统。

Weight

Weight per foot of a casing string  

重量

每英尺套管串重量。

Grade

This is the classification of the strength of the casing. Letter of the Grade
refers to the tensile strength; the second part (number) refers to the minimum
yield strength. Long strings of casing may require higher-grade materials on
the upper portion to withstand the weight of the entire string.  

钢级

这是对套管强度分类。钢级字母是指抗拉强度。第二部分（数字）是指套管最小的屈服强度。长套管串可能在套管串上部需要更高等级材料，以承受整个套管串的重量。

MD

Measured depth to which the casing is set, as measured along the wellbore.  

测深

沿着井壁开始测量到下入套管位置处的测量深度。

TVD

True vertical depth of the bottom of the casing, as measured from the kelly
bushing.  

实际垂深

从方钻杆补芯开始测量，井底套管的实际垂直深度。
