一、钻井液作用the role of drilling mud

1) cool and lubricate drill bit and drill string(冷却和润滑钻头和钻柱)

2）transport cuttings to surface(运移岩屑到地面)

3）control formation pressure(控制地层压力)

4）form a layer of mud cake on rock surface to reduce filtrate and the risk of
caving in(在地层岩石表面形成一层泥饼，从而降低泥浆滤失和井壁塌陷的风险)

二、钻井液类型（Types of drilling mud）

1) Water-based mud(WBM)水基泥浆

It contains water as the continuous phase; clay to produce initial
viscosity;Weighting agent to increase mud density;chemicals to adjust mud
properties.(水基泥浆包含水，水可以作为连续的相；包含的粘土可以产生初始粘度；包含的加重剂可以增加泥浆密度；包含的化学药品可以调整泥浆性能)。

WBM requires various chemical additives to adjust its properties such as mud
thickener (CMC, HEC, Xanthan, ,chemically modified starch, mud thinner, shale
inhibitors)(水基泥浆需要不同化学添加剂来调整其性能，例如泥浆增粘剂，增粘剂包括羧甲基纤维素、羟乙基纤维素、黄烷胶、化学改性淀粉、泥浆降粘剂、页岩抑制剂)。

Mud thickener can boost viscosity of WBM; mud thinner can reduce mud density
when mud is too viscous; shale inhibitors can battle shale
instability.(泥浆增粘剂能够增加泥浆的粘度；当泥浆太粘稠时，降粘剂能够降低泥浆粘度；页岩抑制剂能够处理页岩不稳定性问题）。

2）Oil-based mud(OBM)油基泥浆

Generally,a petroleum ptoduct such as diesel fuel serbes as the base fluid.Its
advantages include increased lubricity, enhanced shale inhibition, and greater
cleaning abilities with less viscosity.It can also withstand greater heat
without breaking
down.(总的来说，一般使用石油产品如柴油等作为基液。油基泥浆优点包括润滑性增加、对页岩抑制能力增加、较小粘性下清洗能力更强。油机泥浆在不分解情况下能够抵御更大热量。)

3）Synthetic-based mud(SBM)合成基泥浆

SBM is also known as low toxicity oil based mud and it employs synthetic oil
as the base fluid but it poses the same environmental problems as oul-base
mud.(合成基泥浆也叫做低毒性油基泥浆，它采用合成油作为基液，但是与油机泥浆一样，同样会导致环保问题。)

三、泥浆测量（Mud measurement）

1) Mud density（泥浆密度测量）

At drilling site, mud density is roughly measured with mud balance.Mud is
poured in the small cup.Then adjust the rider until the bubble in the level
glass stays in the middle.You can find the mud density from reading on the
arm.(钻井现场，使用泥浆称对泥浆密度进行粗略测量。降泥浆倒在小杯中，然后调整游码，直到水平玻璃杯中气泡处在中间位置，通过读取刻度臂上读数得到泥浆密度。)

2）Mud viscosity(泥浆粘度)

It is measured with mud viscometer.It is a basic torque-type viscometer.
Sleeve and bob are immersed in the mud that fills the cup.The sleeve, driven
by the motor, rotates at varied speed.The resulting torque drives the bob to
deflect to a certain extent.The speed of rotation is controlled at 30r/min,
60r/min,100r/min, 200r/min, 300r/min, 600r/min.The mud plastic viscosity and
yield point can be easily calculated with the readings at 300r/min and
600r/min.(使用泥浆粘度计测量泥浆粘度。该粘度计是一种基础扭矩型粘度计。产生扭矩驱动小锤倾斜到一定程度。转速控制在30转/分、60转/分、100转/分、200转/分、300转/分、600转/分。在300转/分和600转/分情况下，泥浆塑性粘度和屈服点可以很容易通过读数计算出来。)

3）Mud filtrate loss(泥浆滤失量)

Filter press is used to measure mud filtrate loss.For filtrate tests, we often
collect filtrate for 7.5 minutes and report the filtrate loss for
30minutes.The filtrate loss for 30minutes doubles that for
7.5minutes.(使用压滤机来测量泥浆滤失量。针对滤失测试，通常收集7.5分钟滤失量，汇报30分钟泥浆滤失量。30分钟泥浆滤失量是7.5分钟泥浆滤失量的2倍。)
