1.Casing Centralizer(套管扶正器)

Centralizers assist in the removal of filter cake and displacement of drilling
fluid by providing a more uniform flow path for the cement slurry. Close
scrutiny of the mudlog and wireline logs will help in the placement of
centralizers. Zones of increased permeability, doglegs and areas of key

seating, should have centralizers placed around the casing.

扶正器为水泥浆提供更加均匀流道，有助于去除滤饼、顶替钻井液。仔细检查录井和测井图有助于放置扶正器。渗透率增加、狗腿度变大的层位以及存在键槽区域，应该将扶正器放在上述层位和区域的套管周围。

Wall Scratcher(井壁刮刀)

These are most useful when running casing through a high fluid-loss

drilling fluid. There are two types of wall scratchers, rotating scratchers

used when the casing can be rotated (normally in vertical wells), and

reciprocating scratchers used when the pipe is reciprocated (moved up and
down). When

these scratchers are placed in 15 to 20 feet intervals,overlapping cleaning
occurs.

通过高滤失钻井液下套管时，最通常使用的是以下这两种井壁刮刀：旋转式刮刀和往复式刮刀。套管旋转时（通常是在直井中），使用旋转刮刀。当钻柱往复运动（上下移动）时，使用往复式刮刀。这两种刮刀放置在15到20英尺井段时，发生重叠清洗。

Wiper Plug(固井胶塞)

Both top and bottom plugs are used during cementing operations. They are used
to separate the various fluids from one another.

The red bottom plug has a shallow top, is made of rubber, and has a hollow

core. It is used ahead of the cement slurry to prevent cement/drilling fluid
contamination and to clean the casing wall of filter cake. After the bottom
plug comes into contact with the float valve, sufficient pressure (150 to 350
psi) causes the top diaphragm to rupture, allowing the cement slurry to

flow through it.

固井作业中使用顶塞和底塞。使用顶塞和底塞可以将不同钻井液彼此分开。红色的底塞浅顶，空芯，由胶皮构成。打入水泥浆前使用底塞，可以防止钻井液污染，清除套管壁滤饼。底塞与浮阀碰压后，足够的压力（150到350psi）憋坏橡胶膜，允许水泥浆流过底塞。

The black top plug has a deep cup on its top and has a solid, molded rubber
core. It is dropped after the cement slurry has been pumped, to prevent
contamination with the displacement fluid.The top plug also signals the end of
displacement by forming a seal on top of the bottom plug, causing a pressure
increase.

顶塞是黑色的，其上部有一个深杯。顶塞含有一个实心、磨制的胶芯。泵入水泥后，下放顶塞，可以预防顶替液污染。顶塞在底塞上部形成密封，意味着置换结束，导致压力增加。

Chemical Wash(化学冲洗液）

Chemical washes are fluids containing surfactants and mud thinners, designed
to thin and disperse the drilling fluid so that it can be removed from the
casing and borehole.Washes are available for water-based and oil-based
drilling fluids.

They are designed to be used in turbulent flow conditions.

化学冲洗液是含有表面活性剂和泥浆降粘剂的液体，可以用来降低钻井液粘度、分散钻井液，重而将钻井液从套管和井筒内去除。冲洗液有水基和油基钻井液两种形式，通常使用在紊流的条件下。

Spacer(隔离液)

Spacers are fluids of controlled viscosity, density and gel strength used to

form a buffer between the cement and drilling fluid. They also help in the

removal of drilling fluid during cementing.

隔离液是指粘度、密度和切力受控的液体，常用来在水泥和钻井液之间形成缓冲。隔离液有助于固井期间清除钻井液。
