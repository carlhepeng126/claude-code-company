1.装车所有泥浆罐，做放井架准备；

Load mud tanks upon the truck, prepare to lower the mast

2.开放底座和井架安全会

Hole a safety meeting about lowering the substructute and mast

3.卸上船和人字架连接销，放底座

R/D derrick upper structure and A frame connecting pin, lower the substructute

4.拆井架U型卡子

R/D mast U-type clamp

5.放井架至高位

Lower the mast to elevated position

6.甩二层台

L/D monkey board

7.收钻井大绳，拆立管操作台和水龙带

Retrieve drilling line, R/D standpipe stabbing board and rotary hose

8.拆钻台动力电缆线，装车泥浆泵

R/D power cable on drill floor, load mud pump onto the truck

9.等天亮继续拆甩搬家

Wait on daytime, continue to R/D&L/D and move the rig

10.开搬家安全会

Hold a safety meeting about rig move

11.卸车就位泥浆罐，30"导流器，泥浆罐电缆槽，药品及爬犁

Unload and spot mud tank cable tray, 30" diverter, mud tank , chemicals, drill
sledge in place

12.检查远控房气瓶预充压力及保养远控房

Check the precharged pressure of gas cylinder in remote control unit and
service the remote control unit

13.顶驱导轨检测，三方落物检查，检测液气分离，检测吊装设备，继续检测5寸钻杆及变扣接头。

Inspect the TDS guide rail, drop inspection performed by third party, inspect
the poor boy, lifting equipment, and continue to inspect 5"DP and x-over sub
