How Shale Oil Is Produced and Extracted

First, oil companies drill vertically into the shale formation.﻿ After
operators drill the well, they curve it at a 90-degree angle. At this kickoff
point, they drill horizontally.

页岩油是如何生产开采？

首先，石油公司垂向钻入页岩层。钻达预定层位后，开始把井眼弯曲成90度。然后，在造斜点处开始水平钻进。

难点解析：

1.shale oil-页岩油

2\. kick off point-造斜点

They then pump high-pressure bursts of water, sand, and chemicals to fracture
the shale and release the oil. The sand holds the fractures open. That allows
the oil to seep into the well.

泵入高压水、砂和化学药品来破裂页岩，从而释放石油。沙子将岩石裂缝撑开。这样使油渗透到井中。

难点解析：

3.fracture the shale这里fracture是动词，表示压裂的意思。而hold fractures
open这里的fracture是名词，意思是裂缝。

Companies in Texas used horizontal drilling in the 1990s. It became more
affordable when Brigham Oil & Gas successfully split a single horizontal leg.
The company fracked each leg independently, providing a higher return on the
investment. This technique propelled North Dakota field production from 7
million barrels in January 2010 to 46 million in October 2019.

20世纪90年代，美国德克萨斯州的石油公司使用水平钻井技术。Brigham油气公司成功分离了单个水平段后，该技术变得更加适用。对每个水平段单独压裂，给公司带来了更高的投资回报。该技术推动了北达科他州油田的产量增加，产量从2010年1月7百万桶增加到2019年10月份4千六百万桶。

难点解析：

4.horizontal leg:水平段

The Bakken Field in North Dakota and Montana is the largest producing shale
oil reserve.The field has layers of dense, oil-bearing rock about two miles
underground. In these fields, drillers use multistage fracking to create
longer cracks.They perforate short segments of the production casing. That
allows them to concentrate the bursts of water in targeted spots.

北达科他州和蒙大纳州Bakken油田可开采的页岩油储量最大。该油田拥有稠密的含油岩层，岩层厚度大约2英里。在这些油田，钻工使用多级压裂技术来生成更长的裂缝。对一小段油套射孔，这样可以将喷射水集中在目标点。

5.oil-bearing rock-含油岩
