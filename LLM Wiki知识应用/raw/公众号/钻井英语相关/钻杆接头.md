Tool joints are short sections of pipe that are attached to the tubing portion
of drill pipe by means of using a flash welding process. The internally
threaded tool joint is called a “box”, while the externally threaded tool
joint is the “pin”.

钻杆接头就是指通过闪光焊工艺，连接到钻杆本体的短管柱。内螺纹连接的钻杆接头叫做母扣，而外螺纹连接的钻杆接头叫做公扣。

API specifications also apply to tool joints:

•Minimum Yield Strength = 120,000 psi

•Minimum Tensile Strength = 140,000 psi

Because tool joints are added to drillpipe, the weight of given to pipe in
many tables is the “nominal weight”. The exact weight will require adding the
weight of the tool joints to the tubing portion. Since two joints do not

weigh the same, it is difficult to determine the weight of a joint of
drillpipe and so an “approximate weight” is used in many calculations.

最小屈服强度=120,000psi

最小抗拉强度=140,000psi

因为钻杆接头连接到钻杆上，所以许多表格中给定的钻杆的重量是公称重量。确切的重量需要将接头重量加到本体重量上。因为公扣和母扣的重量不一样，所以很难确定一根钻杆的质量，因此在许多计算中使用近似重量。

The tool joints on drill pipe may contain internal and/or external upsets. An

upset is a decrease in the ID and/or an increase in the OD of the pipe which

is used to strengthen the weld between the pipe and the tool joint. It is
important to note that under tension, the tool joint is stronger than the
tubular.

钻杆上钻杆接头可能含有内加厚和外加厚两种形式。加厚就是指钻杆内径减少，外径的增加。使用加厚来提升钻杆和工具接头之间的焊接处强度。需要重要注意的是钻杆接头在拉力作用下，比管柱还更牢固。

flash welding-闪光焊

tool joint-钻杆接头

box-母扣

pin-公扣

internal upset-内加厚

external upset-外加厚

ID-内径

OD-外径

nominal weight-公称重量

yield strength-屈服强度

tensile strength-抗拉强度
