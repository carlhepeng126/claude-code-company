load the main camp onto the truck

2.装车药品爬犁，药品

Load chemicals sledge, and chemicals onto the truck

Load bulk materials in the wellsite onto the truck

R/U surface manifold, surface line, choke manifold, rotary hose

Commission the drawwork and rotary table

String the guide line, drilling line

Secure the live end of wire rope, tighten the deadline anchor

9.井架升至高支架

Derrick raised to upper support structure

10.安装二层台，井架落物检查

R/U monkey board, fall object inspection

11.起井架

Raise the derrick

12.固定井架U形卡

Secure U-shaped clamp

13.起底座，砸底座人字架连接销

Raise the substructute, hammer the connecting pin for A-frame of derrick

14.安装钻台铺板，安装钻台梯子

R/U drill floor matting, drill floor ladder

15.安装气动绞车，挂起架大绳

R/U air winch, raising line

Transport BOP to the proximity of ladder, connect bleed-off line and flare
