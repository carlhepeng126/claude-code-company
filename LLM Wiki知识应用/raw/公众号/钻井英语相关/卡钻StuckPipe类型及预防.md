#  卡钻（Stuck Pipe）类型及预防

一、定义

卡钻是指在油气钻井中，钻柱不能旋转、上下移动的一种状况。这能对钻井作业造成严重破坏，而且解决卡钻问题是非常耗钱费时(  A stuck pipe
refers to a situation in oil and gas drilling where the drill String cannot
rotate.This can result in significant disruptions to drilling operations and
can be a costly and time-consuming problem to resolve  )

二、卡钻机理

起钻过程中实际承受拉力与预计承受拉力之间的差异叫超拉力。下钻过程中的这种差异叫遇阻重量。移动钻柱所需的超拉和遇阻重量理论上可在零至极大值之间变化”。若其值为0，可以说井眼畅通。如果随着其值增加，可认为井眼遇阻情况逐渐加剧，因此卡钻并非能用绝对标准界定的非此即彼的情况。在实践中，当上移钻柱所需拉力大于钻杆顶部接头的抗拉强度，就可以认为是卡钻。当然，当钻柱本身的重量不足以让钻柱沿井眼向下移动时，不一定意味着卡钻，因为仍有可能将钻柱从井中提出(
The difference between the actual and expected forces while pulling out is
called the overpull; the difference while running in is called the setdown
weight.  The overpull and the setdown required to move the string can have any
value from zero to (in theory) infinity. If they are zero, the hole is said to
be “free”; as they increase, the hole is said to be getting “tight”. Thus, a
stuck pipe is not an on/off situation that can be defined in absolute terms.
In practice, the pipe is said to be stuck when the force required to move it
upwards is greater than the tensile strength of the top joint of the pipe
(after applying a safety factor). It is, of course, not necessarily stuck when
the weight of the string itself is not sufficient to take it down the hole, as
it may still be possible to pull it back out  )

三、卡钻后果

卡钻可能导致部分钻具断裂或损失在井眼中。其代价是昂贵的。这些后果包括:损失钻时（活动卡钻的钻具）;打捞落物所花费的费用和时间;进行侧钻所浪费的时间和费用(
A stuck pipe can cause the breaking or loss of a part of the drill string in
the hole. Its result is costly. They include:  Drilling time lost (working on
the stuck).  Cost (check also drilling cost per foot) & time spent on working
on the fish (fishing operations) (to  .  Perform a sidetracking well, which is
also a waste of time and cost  )

四、卡钻种类

钻井过程中，有三种与井眼有关的卡钻机理:井眼几何形状导致或机械卡钻;地层特性导致;压差卡钻。

During drilling, three categories of sticking mechanisms related to the hole:
Hole geometry(key seating键槽、well curvature井眼曲率、ledge岩架、undergauge
hole井眼缩径、sand bridge砂桥、pack-off坍塌封堵、) or MechanicalSticking;
Formationcharacteristics(plastic formation塑性地层、fractured and
faulted破裂断层、highly stressed formation高应力层、settled cuttings沉沙);  D  ifferential
sticking.

五、预防措施

合理的井筒规划：  充分的井筒规划，包括井筒稳定性分析，有助于识别并避免潜在的卡钻点(Proper wellbore planning: Adequate
wellbore planning, including wellbore stability analysis, can help identify
and avoid potential sticking points)
合理的钻井液选择:合理的选择和维护钻井液，有助于将压差卡钻风险降到最低(Proper drilling fluid selection: Proper
selection and maintenance of drilling fluids can help minimize the risk of
differential sticking)

合理的钻井做法:  遵循好的钻井做法，如，保持恰当的钻压、控制钻井液性能、监测井筒条件，能够预防卡钻这一问题发生(

Proper drilling practices: Following good drilling practices, such as
maintaining proper weight on bit, controlling drilling fluid properties, and
monitoring wellbore conditions, can help prevent the problem  )

使用卡钻预防工具:  使用不同的技术及工具，例如震击器和井下传感器，都可以识别并预防这些事故的发生(Utilizing stuck pipe
prevention tools: Various technologies and tools, such as drilling jars and
downhole sensors, are available to detect and prevent such incidents)
