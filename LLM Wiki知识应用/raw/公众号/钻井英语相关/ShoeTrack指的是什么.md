#  Shoe Track指的是什么

一、定义

网络油气词汇给出的英文解释是:  The space between the float or guide shoe and the landing or
float collar. 对应的中文意思是浮箍和浮鞋之间的空间部分。进一步说就是指浮箍到浮鞋之间的这一段短套管部分。

二、应用例句场景

1.The shoe track is a section of casing below the casing shoe, typically
containing a float collar to prevent cement backflow during cementing
operations."  
（Shoe track是套管鞋下方的一段套管，通常包含浮箍，用于固井时防止水泥浆回流。）

2.The length of the shoe track is usually 40-60 feet to ensure adequate space
for drill-out after cementing."

（Shoe track的长度通常为40-60英尺，以确保固井后有足够空间进行钻水泥塞作业。）

3.A successful pressure test on the shoe track confirms the integrity of the
cement bond and casing connections."

（对shoe track进行压力测试成功，可验证水泥胶结质量和套管连接的完整性。）

4.If the shoe track becomes plugged with debris, a mill shoe may be used to
clean out the obstruction before drilling ahead."

（若shoe track被碎屑堵塞，可在继续钻进前使用磨鞋清除障碍物。）

5.The drilling report noted that the shoe track was drilled out efficiently,
with no signs of cement contamination in the drill cuttings."

（钻井报告指出，shoe track已顺利钻穿，钻屑中未发现水泥污染迹象。）
