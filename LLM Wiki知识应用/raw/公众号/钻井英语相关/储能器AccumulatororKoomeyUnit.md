一、简介

An accumulator or Koomey unit is a unit used to hydraulically operate Rams
BOP, Annular BOP, HCR and some hydraulic equipment. There are several of high
pressure cylinders that store gas (in bladders) and hydraulic fluid or water
under pressure for hydraulic activated systems. The primary purpose of this
unit is to supply hydraulic power to the BOP stack in order to close/open BOP
stack for both normal operational and emergency situation. Stored hydraulic in
the system can provide hydraulic power to close BOP’s in well control
operation, therefore, kick volume will be minimized. The accumulator should
have sufficient volume to close/open all preventers and accumulator pressure
must be maintained all time. According to API RP53, your reservoir tank should
have a total volume at least 2 times of usable volume to close all BOP
equipment.

储能器（或称
Koomey单元）是一种通过液压驱动闸板防喷器、环形防喷器、液控阀及其他液压设备的装置。其核心由多个高压缸体组成，内部通过胶囊储存气体，并加压储存液压油或水，为液压执行系统提供动力。
该装置的核心功能是为防喷器组提供液压动力，以实现在正常作业 和 紧急情况下启闭防喷器组。系统中储存的液压能量可在井控操作中快速关闭防喷器，从而
显著减少井涌量。

设计要求：

1\. 储能器须具备足够的 容积，确保所有防喷器的启闭操作，且 压力须实时保持。

2\. 根据API RP53标准，储液罐的总容积至少为关闭全部防喷器设备所需 可用容积的2倍。

二、储能器构成

There are 4 main components of the Koomey unit as follows:

• Accumulators

•Pumping system (electric and pneumatic pumps)

• Manifold system

• Reservoir tank

储能器装置主要有以下四个部分构成：

•储能器

•泵送系统（电动泵和气动泵）

•管汇系统

•储罐

三、储能器类型

The two (2) basic types are bladder and float which are available in
cylindrical or ball styles. The accumulators can either be bottom or top
loading. Top loading means the bladder or float can be removed from the top
while it is still mounted on the accumulator unit. Bottom loading accumulators
must be removed from the accumulator unit to be serviced. Bladder and buoyant
float-type accumulators can be repaired in the field without destroying their
stamp of approval

储能器的两种基本类型为胶囊式和浮子式，可设计为圆柱形或球形结构。储能器的安装方式分为底部装载和顶部装载。

顶部装载：储能器单元保持安装状态时，可直接从顶部拆卸胶囊或浮子组件进行维护。

底部装载：需将储能器从单元整体拆下后方可维修。

胶囊式和浮子式储能器支持现场维修，且维修过程不会破坏其原有的认证资质。

四、储能器装置反应时间

The closing system should be capable of closing each ram preventer within 30
seconds. Closing time should not exceed 30 seconds for annular preventers
smaller than 18 3/4 inches and 45 seconds for annular preventers 18 3/4 inches
and larger.

关闭系统能够在30秒内，关闭每一个闸板防喷器。对于通径小于18 3/4"环形防喷器来说，系统关闭时间应不超过30秒；对于通径大于等于18
3/4"的环形防喷器来说，系统关闭时间应不超过45秒。

五、储能器工作压力

Accumulators commonly have minimum working pressures of 1200 psi and maximum
working pressures of between 1500 and 3000 psi.

通常，储能器最低工作压力为1200psi,最高压力介于1500和3000psi之间。
