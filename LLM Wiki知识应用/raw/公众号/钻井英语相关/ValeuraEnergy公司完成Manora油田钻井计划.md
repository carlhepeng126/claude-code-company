#  Valeura Energy公司完成 Manora 油田钻井计划

Valeura Energy Inc. completed a five-well infill drilling campaign comprising
three production- wells and two appraisal wells at Manora field in License
G1/48 offshore Gulf of Thailand.  
Valeura能源公司，完成在泰国湾G1/48区块Manora油田5口加密井钻井计划。5口加密井中，3口为生产井，2口为评估井。  
A34 was drilled for infill development targets within the deep 600-series
sands in the field’s eastern fault block. The well was successful and has been
completed as a multi-zone comingled producer.  
A34井为加密开发井，储层为油田东部断层区块600系列砂岩。该井成功完井，作为多层合并开采井进行生产。  
A38 was drilled horizontally into the eastern fault block, with the objective
of developing the shallower 300-series sands. It was completed as a producer,
with the well design incorporating an innovative downhole autonomous inflow
control device (ICD) to manage water vs. oil production. Valeura is monitoring
the impact of this and other ICDs to optimize the application of this
technology across the portfolio.

A38井，水平钻进到东部断层区块，目的是开发更浅层300系列砂岩层。该井作为生产井完井，其设计采取了一种创新的井下自主进流控制装置，来控制油水产量比。Valeura公司正在监测这种装置和其它的进流控制装置带来的影响，从而在整个投资组合中最大化应用该技术。

A36 targeted sands across several known producing intervals in the field’s
main fault block and has been completed as a multi-zone infill development
well. Only the deepest targets are currently producing and the shallower zones
will be brought on production later.  
A36井目标层位为砂岩层，该岩层穿过油田主要断层的几个已知产层段，并作为多层加密开发井，进行完井。当前，仅是开发最深层目标层位，更浅产层，将在以后进行开采。  
A35 successfully appraised several zones of interest within the shallower
300-series sands. While this appraisal well will not be a producer (and
accordingly has been plugged and abandoned), the results encountered have
indicated the potential for three further development wells within this
reservoir section, which will now be further studied and modelled for
inclusion in future development drilling.  
A35井对浅层300系列砂岩层中几个产层成功地进行了评估。然而该评估井并不作为生产井（已经相应地进行封堵和弃井）。钻遇的地层结果表明，在该储层区域内具备再钻三口开发井的潜力。接下来将对这一情况作进一步研究和建模分析，以便将其纳入未来的开发钻井计划中。  
A37 was drilled horizontally and is a combined appraisal and development well.
The well encountered an encouraging appraisal target in the 500-series sands
which is now being matured for inclusion in a future drilling campaign. The
well’s development target, within the deeper 600-series sands, was completed
as a producer.  
A37井为水平井，具有评估和开发的目的。该井钻遇评估目标层段500系列砂岩层，目前时机成熟，可以将该砂岩层开采放在将来的钻井计划中。该井开发的目标层位为更深的600系列砂岩层，可以作为生产井完井。  
Campaign appraisal objectives have yielded between three and five potential
future drilling targets, which will be further evaluated for inclusion in a
future drilling program. The company’s Manora field working interest share oil
production before royalties has increased to 2,866 b/d for the last 14-day
period from a 2,144 b/d average in December.  
钻井计划评估结果将在3到5个潜在的钻井目标中产生，该结果将包括在以后的钻井计划中进行进一步评估。在过去的14天期间，Valeura公司在Manora油田扣除特许权使用费前的开采权益分成石油产量，从去年12月份平均2144桶/天增加到2866桶/天。  
Following completion of the Manora drilling campaign, Valeura’s contracted
drilling rig has mobilized to License B5/27 (100% operated interest) where it
is currently conducting a drilling program on the Jasmine C wellhead platform.  
完成了Manora钻井计划后，Valeura公司承包的钻机将动迁到B5/27区块（拥有百分之百的作业权益）。该区块当前正在Jasmine
C井口平台进行钻井项目。  
Valeura Energy is operator of License G1/48 with 70% working interest.

Valeur能源公司是G1/48区块作业者，拥有该区块70%的工作权益。
