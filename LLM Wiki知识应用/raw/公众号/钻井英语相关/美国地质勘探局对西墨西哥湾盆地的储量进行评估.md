#  美国地质勘探局对西墨西哥湾盆地的储量进行评估

The US Geological Survey (USGS) released its assessment of potential for
undiscovered oil and gas in Maverick basin formations in the Western Gulf
basin province.

美国地质调查局（USGS）发布了其对西墨西哥湾盆地省内马弗里克盆地地层未发现油气资源潜力的评估结果。

USGS estimates that there are technically recoverable resources of 366 million
bbl of oil and 11 tcf of gas in the area, as much gas as the United States
consumes in 4 months at the current rate of consumption and is more than 3
times the total historic gas production from Maverick basin.

美国地质调查局（USGS）预估，该区域技术可采资源量达3.66亿桶，天然气达11万亿立方英尺。该天然气储量相当于美国当前消费速率下4个月的全国用气需求，是马弗里克盆地历史上天然气总产量的3倍以上。

The assessment area includes Maverick basin and adjacent areas in southwestern
Texas. The USGS looked at nine assessment units in the basin, including both
conventional and continuous reservoirs of oil and gas in the Escondido, Olmos,
and San Miguel formations of the southwestern Texas coastal plain.

美国地质调查局（USGS）本次评估范围涵盖得克萨斯州西南部的马弗里克盆地及其邻近区域。研究团队在盆地内划定了9个评价单元，包含得州西南海岸平原埃斯孔迪多组（Escondido）、奥尔莫斯组（Olmos）及圣米格尔组（San
Miguel）地层中的常规油气储集层与连续型油气储集层。

Since exploration began in the area in the 1930s, the Maverick basin has
produced at least 306 million bbl of oil and 3.4 tcf of gas.

自20世纪30年代勘探启动以来，马弗里克盆地已累计产原油至少3.06亿桶、天然气3.4万亿立方英尺。
