Load mud chemicals sledge, BOP, remote control panel

6.新井卸车并就位钻杆盒子、录井房、定向房

Unload and spot drill pipe box,mud logging house, orientation house in new
wellsite.

R/D crown section monkey board, BOP handling equipment, standpipe manifold,

8.新井卸车司钻房、钻台区加宽台、偏房、灌浆罐、冷却水罐、节流管汇、钢木基础、二层台。

Unload driller cabin, expandable table, doghouse, trip tank, cooling water
tank, choke manifold, rig matting, monkey board in new wellsite.

9.就位钻井液罐、备用水罐、钻井液处理剂爬犁。

Spot mud tank, spare water tank, mud additive sledge

10.液气大钳、试压泵、氧气爬犁、钢木基础、井架装车

Load hydraulic tong, pressure test pump, oxygen sledge, rig matting, derrick

11.拆人字架井架底座

R/D A-frame and substructure of derrick.

12.老井拆甩并装车井架、人字架、下船，库房、发电机组、柴油罐、配电柜、顶驱控制房并装车

R/D and load mast, A frame, structure, warehouse, generator sets, diesel tank,
distribution cabinet, top drive control room.

13.新井卸车并就位配电柜，连接钻井液罐之间的连接管线、梯子及过桥，

Unload and spot distribution cabinet, connect lines, stairs, corridor between
mud tanks in new wellsite.

14\.

卸转盘、钻台台板、加宽台、立根台、司钻房、偏房，钻井液转接罐，电缆转接房、高架水罐、井架。

Unload rotary table, rig floor matting, expandable table, setback board,
driller cabin, doghouse, mud transfer tank, wireline transfer room, elevated
water tank, derrick.

15.新井就位发电机房、库房、柴油罐、钳工房，营房就位，连接营区电缆、水管线。

Spot generator room, warehouse, diesel tank, work shop, camp and connect
wireline, water line in camp area.

16.拆甩100%，搬迁100%，安装30%

R/D 100%, Rig Moving100%, R/U 30%.

Install and commission CCTV, level and fence sewage pit in camp area and
garbage pit in wellsite.

18.安装钻井液罐上水泵，挖钻井液罐区排水沟

Install suction pump of mud tank, and dig trench in tank area.

19.进行吊点、吊具和下船销子检测

Perform inspection on lifting point, lifting sling and pin of substructure.

20.检测安全绳套、卸扣。

Inspect safety sling, shackle.

21.检测左右上船、偏房支撑支架、底座连接横梁、斜立柱及封井器吊装导轨

Inspect L&R upper structure, doghouse support, substructure connecting beam,
inclined upright post, BOP handling guide rail.

22.安装左偏房、司钻房、钻台台板等

R/U left doghouse, driller cabin, rig floor pads,etc.

23.安装起井架大绳、天车等；穿钻井大绳；

R/U mast raising line, crown block, etc. String in drilling line.

24.就位套装水罐、灌浆罐和液气分离器等

Spot nested water tank, trip tank, and poor boy.

25.调试绞车，安装泥浆泵电机，保养泥浆泵

Commission drawwork, install mud pump, maintenance of mud pump.

26.功能测试循环系统；地面管线及内防喷工具试压

Function test circulation system, pressure test surface line and IBOP.
