During the course of well,there is a tremendous amount of engineering data
which must be collected to ensure an accurate and comprehensive Final Well
Report. Make use of this information when writing the Drilling and Engineering
section of Final Well Report.

钻井过程中，需要收集大量的工程数据，确保最终完井报告的准确、全面。在撰写完井报告的钻井工程部分，会用到这些信息。

Drillstring/BHA make-up（接钻具）

Drill Bit-type,name,size

钻头-类型、名字、尺寸

Drill Collars-type,size(OD&ID),number

钻挺-类型、尺寸（内径和外径）、数量

Specialized Tools- Stabilizers, X/O,PDM,Turbine,MWD,Jars,etc

特殊工具-稳定器、转换接头、螺杆、涡轮、随钻测量仪、震击器等

Heavyweight-size(OD&ID),number

加重钻杆-尺寸（内径和外径）、数量

Drill Pipe-size(OD&ID)

钻杆-尺寸（外径和内径）

Reasons for using-Location of neutral point,build/hold/drop assembly,packed or
inverted assembly,etc.

使用原因-中性点位置、增斜/稳斜/降斜钻具、满眼钻具、倒钟摆钻具等

Casing Program（套管设计）

Hole Size-areas of washout,tight spots,caliper size

井眼尺寸-冲蚀区、遇阻位置、井径尺寸

Casing Size-Grade,OD,ID,Weight Per Foot

套管尺寸-等级、内径、外径、每英尺重量

Casing Shoe Depths-Measured and True Vertical

套管鞋深度-测量和实际垂深

Accessories-float collars, centralize r,scratcher,liner hanger

套管附件-浮箍、扶正器、刮管器、尾管挂

Cementing Program(固井设计)

Cement Class-Lead and tail slurry

水泥级别-前置液和后置液

Washes and Spacer-pre-flush type,volumes

冲洗液和隔离液-预冲洗液类型、体积

Additives-types and amounts

添加剂-类型和数量

Cement Volume Requirement-sacks,ft3,weight,yield

水泥体积要求-袋数、立方英尺、密度、

Mixing water requirement-gallon per sack

Displacement rate,pumping time and setting time

混水要求-每袋加仑、顶替率、泵注时间、候凝时间

Drilling Fluid Program-type of mud system,depth changed or modified

钻井液设计-泥浆体系类型、深度变化或更改

Additives-depth when additions are made,why added

外加剂-加入外加剂深度、加入原因

Mud Rheology-depth changed,reasons changed

泥浆流变性-深度变换、改变原因

Bit Program(钻头设计)

Type of Bits-Size,IADC code and manufacturer's name

钻头类型-尺寸、国际钻井承包商协会代码、生产厂家名字

Bit Operating Parameters-weight on bit,rotary speed,pump pressure

钻头操作参数-钻压、转速、泵压

Bit Hydraulics-pressure losses,nozzle velocity,nozzle/TFA size

钻头水利-压力损耗、水眼流速、水眼/总流体面积尺寸

Bit Grading-IADC Format

钻头分级-国际钻井承包商格式

Directional Drilling Program(定向钻井设计)

Location-coordinates,Area, and block

井位-坐标、区域、区块

Drillstring assemblies-building,holding and dropping

钻具组合-增斜、稳斜、降斜

Type of well-Vertical,Directional and Horizontal

井型-直井、定向井、水平井

Survey data and calculations

测斜数据和计算

Type of survey-single-shot,MWD,Gyro

测斜类型-单点测斜、随钻测斜、陀螺仪

Borehole Trajectory-Build-and Hold,Build- Hold-and Drop,Modified Target
Location

井眼轨迹-增斜-稳斜、增斜-稳斜-降斜、更改的目标方位

Well Problems（钻井复杂）

Stuck Pipe-Type,causes,location,remedial measures,results

卡钻-类型、原因、位置、补救措施、结果

Fishing Operation-Tool used,technique

打捞作业-使用工具、工艺

Lost Circulation-Type, causes,location,remedial measures,results

井漏-类型、原因、位置、补救措施、结果

Well Kicks - pressures,pit gain,kill procedures,results

井涌-压力、泥浆池增量、压井程序、结果

Twist-off-Causes,Location,Remedial Measures,results

脱扣-原因、位置、补救措施、结果

Shale Problems-Type,Location,Remedial Measures,Results

页岩问题-类型、位置、补救措施、结果
