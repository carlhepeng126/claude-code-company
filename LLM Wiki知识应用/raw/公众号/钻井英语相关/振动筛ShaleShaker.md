#  振动筛(Shale Shaker)

一、背景

钻井液是现代钻井工艺中一个重要的构件。它能润滑、冷却钻头、从井筒传送岩屑。钻井液以油基或水基形式，混合了昂贵和对环境敏感的化学药品。为了减少钻井作业成本和对现有环境的担忧，所以使用振动筛，用机械方法过滤掉岩屑和固相物质，从而再循环利用钻井液。振动筛是对返出的钻井液使用机械处理方法进行固控的第一道设备。必须经过振动筛对泥浆进行预处理，否则没有其它的机械设备能够对固相进行控制(Drilling
fluid (mud) is an essential component of modern drilling processes: it
lubricates and cools the drill bit and conveys drilled cuttings away from the
borehole. This fluid is a mixture of expensive and environmentally sensitive
chemicals in a water- or oil-based solution. To reduce drilling operational
costs and existing environmental concerns, shale shakers are used to
mechanically filter cuttings and solids, enabling the drilling fluid to be
recycled.The shale shakers are the first mechanical treatment of the returning
drilling fluid for solids control. None of the other mechanical devices can
cope with solids control without the pre-treatment of the mud in the shale
shaker)

二、工作机制

振动筛将泥浆和固体颗粒输送到振动筛网上。钻井液和细的固体颗粒通过筛网，返回工作泥浆系统中。比筛网孔眼粗的固体颗粒，通过振动筛的振动运动被输送出筛网。振动筛是唯一的依据物体实际颗粒大小进行分离的清除固体颗粒设备。水力旋流器和离心机根据固体颗粒相对质量的差异来分离固体(A
shale shaker channels mud and solids onto vibrating screens. The drilling mud
and fine solids pass through the screens and return to the active system.
Solids coarser than the screen openings are conveyed off the screen by the
vibratory motion of the shaker. The shaker is the only solids removal device
that makes a separation based on physical particle size. Hydrocyclones and
centrifuges separate solids based on differences in their relative mass)

三、振动筛种类

主要有三种类型的振动筛:单层振动筛、差速单层振动筛、两层和多层筛网振动筛。按运动轨迹划分还可以分为分非衡椭圆运动振动筛、圆形运动振动筛、直线型运动振动筛、均衡椭圆型运动振动筛(There
are three significant types of shale shakers:Singledeck shakers;

Differential single-deckshakers;

Double and multiple screen shakers;

unbalanced elliptical motion;

circular motion;

linear motion;

balanced elliptical motion;)

四、  振动筛构成

振动筛的三个主要构件是漏斗、筛篮和振动器(The three main shale shaker components are the hopper,
the screen basket and the vibrator)
