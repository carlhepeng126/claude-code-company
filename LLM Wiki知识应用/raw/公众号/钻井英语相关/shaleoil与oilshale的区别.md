#  shale oil与oil shale的区别

Hydraulic fracturing (also called fracking) technology has enabled oil and gas
producers to tap reserves in shale formations across North America. The oil
coming out of these shale formations is referred to as shale oil.

水力压裂技术（也叫压裂）可以使油气生产者们开发整个北美页岩层的油气储量。来自这些页岩层的油叫作页岩油。

However, shale oil should not be confused with oil shale, which is a rock that
contains a compound called kerogen and is used to make oil.  

但是不要把页岩油与油页岩混淆，油页岩指的是含有干酪根化合物的岩石，该岩石可以用来制作石油。

Liquid oil—called crude oil—is made up of plant and animal remains that have
been subject to pressure and heat for millions of years. Over time, this
organic matter breaks down into hydrocarbons. There are stages in the
transformation process over the years from organic material to crude oil.
Kerogen is one of those stages.  

液态石油叫做原油，由植物和动物遗骸构成，这些遗骸数百万年来受到压力和热的影响。随着时间推移，这种有机物质分解成碳氢化合物。从有机物质转变成原油的过程中，经历几个阶段，干酪根就是其中的一个阶段。

Oil shale is essentially a type of rock that contains solid bits of kerogen.
Kerogen is a petroleum product (essentially, a precursor to oil). In order for
the kerogen in oil shale to be converted into something useful, one of two
methods must be used.  

本质上说，油页岩是一种岩石，该岩石含有固态干酪根。干酪根是一种石油产品。（主要是石油的前身）。为了将油页岩中干酪根转变成有用的物质，有两个方法可以使用。

One method is to mine the oil shale and then heat it later in a low-oxygen
environment. This process converts the kerogen—the petroleum product in the
oil shale—into a useful product.  

一个方法是开采油页岩，然后在低氧环境下对油页岩进行加热。该过程将油页岩中的干酪根即石油产品转换成有用的产品。

The other method is to heat the oil on-site by applying heat to the shale
formation. After this process is completed, the resulting liquid is pumped out
of the reserves. The major difference between these methods is that the first
method—the mining method—requires more heat than the second method—the on-site
method, where heat is injected on-site.  

另一个方法就是通过对页岩层加热从而现场对油加热。完成该过程，然后将所得液体从储层中开采出来。这两种方法的主要区别就是：与第二种方法即现场注入热量的加热法相比，第一种方法即采矿法所需的热量要更多。

There are also other additional benefits to the on-site method because the gas
produced from this process—a byproduct of the process—can be recycled to
produce more heat. In addition, the end product is of higher quality. That
said, both methods result in a product that costs more per barrel to extract
than conventional oil products.  

Shale oil refers to hydrocarbons that are contained in formations of shale
rock. Shale oil is closer to a finished product than oil shale, but the
extraction process still involves drilling and fracking.  

现场加热法还有其它额外的优点，因为在该过程中生产出来的气（该过程的副产品）可以进行回收产生更多的热量。此外，该过程终端产品质量很高。也就是说开采每桶由这两种方法生成的产品的成本要高于开采常规的油气产品。页岩油指的是页岩层中所含有的碳氢化合物。与油页岩相比，页岩油更接近成品，但是页岩油开采过程仍需要钻井和压裂。

Fracking refers to an extraction process whereby oil companies drill down
horizontally into layers of shale in order to open up the shale rock
formations so that oil can be extracted. Shale rock is not very porous. As a
result, the hydrocarbons contained in the rock cannot easily flow out into a
pipe.  

压裂指的是开采工艺，石油公司通过该工艺，水平钻入页岩层，从而打开页岩层，将石油开采出来。页岩渗透性不是很好，因此包含在岩层中的油气不能轻松地流到管柱中。

Instead, the oil is accessed by drilling horizontally across the deposit and
then opening up the rock and allowing the oil to flow. Fracking injects water
under high pressure into the layers of shale to release the oil.  

相反，可以通过钻水平井到达储层，然后打开储层岩石，让石油流出来。使用压力技术就是在高压下将水注入到页岩层，从而释放石油。

重点词汇：

hydraulic fracturing-水力压裂

kerogen-干酪根

drilling horizontally-水平钻井

shale oil-页岩油

oil shale-油页岩

hydrocarbons-烃，碳氢化合物，油气

pump out-开采
