1.PPE requirement:hard hat,safety glasses,work gloves,steel toe shoes,back
support, and anti-fall protection. Fit for duty check on all hands.

劳保要求：安全帽、安全眼镜、工作手套、钢趾鞋、后背支撑、防坠落设备。对所有工作人员进行工作适应性检查。

2.Review JSA for pressure testing BOP

防喷器试压工作安全分析进行审查。

3.Rig manager to discuss procedures with pressure testers. This is to include:

-stack design 

-the proper size and type of test plug to fit the casing head. 

-position,size and pressure rating of choke manifold and valves 

-wellhead rated working pressure 

-the drill pipe grade and operating pressure of the drill pipe 

-casing burst pressure 

-the use of methanol -water for testing in freezing weather 

-outline the overall testing and specific valves that are to be tested 

-inform testers of low-high test requirement 

平台经理与压力测试人员讨论测试程序，这包括以下方面：

-防喷器组设计 

-适合套管头的测试塞合适的尺寸和类型 

-节流管汇和阀门的位置、尺寸和额定压力 

-井口额定工作压力 

-钻杆等级和工作压力 

-套管破裂压力 

-在寒冷天气使用甲醇和水进行测试 

-概述出需要进行总体测试和具体测试的阀门 

-通知测试人员低高压测试设备 

4.verify the following items are ready as required:

-adequate tie off points are in place to assure 100%tie off 

-flurosecent vest 

-designate qualified man to be flagger 

-two way communication 

-pproper API ring gasket is used if repairing a leak 

-proper size hammer wrenches and hammers,if required 

-cellar covers are to be reinstalled as soon as practical 

-all wellhead,blowout preventers,valves and fail saves shall be pressure tested from direction they would be exposed to well pressure 

-all BOP equipment should be tested every 14 days or when the integrity of BOP stack has been comprised (ie. changing rams,seals or lifting the stack ) 

-casing head"wear bushing" is not installed 

-have correct "wear bushing" retrieval tool on location 

确定按照要求，准备好以下事项：

-备好充足系点，确保百分之百系上 

-准备荧光马甲 

-指定有资质的人作为指挥人员 

-保持双向沟通 

-如果修理泄露处，使用适合的API垫圈 

-如果需要，使用合适尺寸的敲击扳手和榔头 

-一旦可行，重新安装原井封盖 

-所有的井口、防喷器、闸门和防故障装置，重其暴露在井压的方向，进行压力测试 

-所有的防喷器每14天进行一次测试，或者是防喷器组整体性受到破坏（例如换闸门、密封装置或上提防喷器组） 

-套管头防磨补心不用安装 

-现场有正确的防磨补心取回工具
