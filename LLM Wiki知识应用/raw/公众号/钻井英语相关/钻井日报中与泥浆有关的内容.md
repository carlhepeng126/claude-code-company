One.Density

Determines mud’s ability to suspend cuttings or clear obstructions to the
surface.  

密度

确定泥浆悬浮岩屑或清楚表面障碍物的能力。

1). Mud Weight In(MWI)

Density of the drilling fluid as measured before entering the wellbore  

入井泥浆比重

钻井液入井之前所测量的密度。

2）.Mud Weight Out(MWO)

An important comparison to mud weight in, mud weight out is the density of the
drilling fluid as measured after exiting the wellbore.  

出井泥浆比重

与入井泥浆比重形成重要对比，出井泥浆比重是泥浆重井口返出后所测量的钻井液密度。

Two.Rheology

Determines the flow properties of the mud.  

流变性

决定了泥浆的流动性能。

1）.Funnel Viscosity（FV）

Basic measure of the viscosity profile of the mud observed in seconds per
quart when poured through a Marsh funnel.  

漏斗粘度

通过马氏漏斗倒入泥浆时，数秒内所观察到的每夸脱泥浆年度剖面的基本测量值。

2）.Plastic Viscosity(PV)

Viscosity profile of the mud observed when measured by a rotating viscometer
or Rheometer.  

塑性粘度

通过旋转粘度仪或流变仪测量时，所观察到的泥浆粘度剖面。

3）.Yield Point(YP)

Minimum amount of shear stress required for the fluid to begin to flow.  

屈服点

液体所需流动的最小剪切应力量。

4）.Gels(10s)

Gel strength measure reading from the dial on a rotating viscometer after 10
seconds of being undisturbed.  

胶凝强度（10秒）

10秒钟未被干扰测试下，旋转粘度仪表盘胶凝强度测量读数。

5）.Gels(10M)

Gel strength measure reading from the dial on a rotating viscometer after 10
minutes of being undisturbed.  

10分钟未被干扰测试下，旋转粘度仪表盘胶凝强度测量读数。  

6）.Gels(30M)

Gel strength measure reading from the dial on a rotating viscometer after 30
minutes of being undisturbed.  

30分钟未被干扰测试下，旋转粘度仪表盘胶凝强度测量读数。

​  ​  ​  ​Three. Fluid Loss  

Determines the loss of fluid to the formation and the maintenance of
hydrostatic pressure  

滤失量

确定流失到地层的液体以及维持静液压力所流失的液体。

1). Filtrate Volume

Volume of mud filtrate measured after 30 minutes in API static filtration
tests.  

滤液体积

在API静止滤失测试中，30分钟后，测量的泥浆滤液体积。

2）.Cake Thickness

Measurement of the thickness of the filter cake, usually recorded in 32nds of
an inch.  

泥饼厚度

测量滤饼的厚度，通常以32分之一英寸记录。

3）.High- Pressure ,High- Temperature Filtration Test(HTHP)

Measure of the static filtration behavior of mud at an elevated temperature
and pressure  

高压高温滤失测试

测量在高温高压情况下，泥浆的静态滤失特性。

4）.Water Loss(WL)

Volume of liquid measured in the filtration tests performed according to API
specifications, in units of cm3/30 minutes.  

失水量

依据API标准，在进行滤失测试中所测量的液体体积，以立方厘米/30分钟为单位。  e  ​  ​  ​  ​

Four. Mud Additive

Based on the indications of the five mud properties mentioned above, the mud
engineer will perform calculations and determine the best mud additives to
employ to optimize drilling and wellbore conditions.  

泥浆添加剂

基于所提到的5个泥浆性能的指示，泥浆工程师进行计算，确定使用最佳的泥浆添加剂，重而优化钻井和改善井筒环境。

1）.Mud Product

Any product added to mud to perform a specific function, such as a weighting
agent, viscosifer, or a lubricant.  

任何加入到泥浆中发挥具体作用的药品，例如：加重剂、增粘剂、或者润滑剂。

2）Units

Measure of the additive product.  

单位

添加剂药品的测量。

3）.Amt. Received

Cumulative amount of product received to date.  

接收量

目前所收到的累计泥浆药品量。

4）.Amt. Used

Cumulative amount of product used to date.  

使用量

目前累计使用泥浆药品量。
