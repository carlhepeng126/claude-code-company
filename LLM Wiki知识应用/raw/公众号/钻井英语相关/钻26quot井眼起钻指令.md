#  钻26"井眼起钻指令

_2026年04月16日 06:01_ __ _ _ _ _ _ 黑龙江  _

1.Pick up the 26"bit(ensure bit and jets are clear of obstruction)

吊起26"钻头，确保钻头和喷嘴无堵塞

2.MU 26"spud BHA as per the tally（ensure float valve is installed
correctly;ensure TOTCO Ring has been installed, Rig engineer and WSL to
confirm that; All connections cleaned and torqued to optimum torques）

按照钻具记录表配26"开钻钻具(确保浮阀安装正确，TOTCO仪器坐放短节已经安装，钻井工程师和现场负责人已经对此进行了确认;确保所有螺纹进行了清洗，按最佳扭矩上扣)

3.Wash down with HWDP stand at FR 1500 to 2000lpm, tag TOC, circulate to get
homogeneous mud at surface

使用加重钻杆向下冲洗，流量1500-2000升每分钟，探水泥顶，循环钻井液，直到返出到地面泥浆性质均匀

4.Water and Hi-Vis sweeps will be utilized during the section with 3m3Hi-Vis
pumped every stand to aid hole cleaning

在该井段使用清水段塞和高粘段塞，每钻一柱泵入3方高粘，辅助清洁井眼

5.Drill out 30"shoe/Cement

钻30"套管鞋/水泥塞

6.Continue drilling 26"section with 5 stands of 5"HWDP to allow PU of the
remaining BHA components

使用5柱5"加重钻杆继续钻26"井眼，以便提起连接剩余的钻具部件

7.The cellar should be monitored to confirm that there are no washout
occurring during initial drill out and staging up of the mud pumps.

在初次钻塞及泥浆泵逐渐增加泵速和排量期间，监测圆井，确认没有刺漏现象发生

8.Ensure adequate water hoses,shovels,mops,and wash gun ready next to shale
shakers.

确保震动塞旁准备好足够的水龙带、铁锹、抹布和清洗枪

9.PU,MU 26"Rotary BHA with 9"NMDC as per the BHA tally.

按照钻具记录表吊起连接26"旋转钻具（含9"无磁）

10.Ensure auto driller set up to maintain WOB and ROP

确保安装自动送钻装置，以便维持钻压和机械钻速

11.NMDC should be P/U&M/U under supervision of WSL&Rig Engineer with M/U TQ
84,000lb,ft.Follow NMDC make up and break out precedures when picking the
NMDCs.

在现场负责人和钻井工程师监督下吊起连接无磁，上扣扭矩为84,000lb,ft。吊起无磁时，遵循无磁上扣和卸扣规程。

12.WSL to be on the rig floor making up the BHA and installing the EMS Ring.

现场负责人在钻台上连接钻具并安装多点测斜仪坐放短接

13.Record BHA weight below the jar.

记录震击器下方钻具重量

14.RIH and wash down last stand to BTM.

下钻，冲洗最后一柱到井底

15.Cont. drilling 26"section to section TD

继续钻26"井眼到完钻井深。

16.Wash up and ream down full stand after each stand drilled.

每钻完一柱就上提循环下放扩眼

17.Pump 3m3 Hi Vis every stand drilled

每钻一柱打3方高粘

18.Drill with controlled mud properties.

钻井使用的泥浆性质可控

19.Run the mud cleaner,centrifuge and dump and dilute to keep drilled solids
at a minimum

运行泥浆清洁器，离心机，采用排液和稀释措施，将钻屑固相含量控制在最低水平

20.If shakers overflow occurs, and mud density increases, clean the shakers
with high pressure guns.Reduced ROP and flow rate temporarily until the
overflow issue is rectified.

如果震动塞发生溢流，泥浆密度增加，用高压喷枪清洗震动塞。暂时降低机械钻速和流量，直到溢流问题解决。

21.Pump 8m3 40ppb Nut Plug pill on observation of balling.

观察到泥包时，泵入8m3浓度为40ppb胶塞

22.Reduce ROP to 3m/hr and continue drilling, looking for drilling break.

机械钻速降至3米/小时，继续钻进，留意是否有放空

23.If at 5m below top formation prognosis there is still no lithology
observed, and no indication of drilling break, stop drilling and circulate
bottoms-up.

如果在顶部地层预测深度以下5米处，仍未观察到预期岩性，也没有钻井放空迹象，停止钻进，循环一周

24.At bottom,sweep borehole with 10m3 Hi Vis Pill, circulate hole clean with
minimum 2*bottom up.

在井底，用10方高粘段塞扫井，然后循环清洁井眼，至少循环2周

25.Perform 15min flow check rotating string at 20rpm.

进行15分钟溢流检查，以20转每分速度旋转钻柱

26.POOH to the back to+/-50m to avoid handling 9"DCs.

起钻到离井底50米距离，以避免操作9"钻铤

27.Care to be taken when tripping through conductor shoe with stab and bit and
any restriction reported.

起下钻时，若带有稳定器和钻头的钻具通过导管鞋时，需格外小心，注意是否有遇阻情况，如有立即上报

28.Spot 20m3 of hi-vis mud on bottom.

井底打20方高粘泥浆

29.Breakout one single of 5"DP from the drill string and drop EMS.

从钻柱上卸掉一根5"钻杆，投电子多点测斜仪

30.POOH to surface and L/D BHA

起钻到地面，甩钻具

31.Flush all tools with fresh water, ensuring the EMS engineer is satisfied
with the condition of the EMS tool and the data is acquired as required.

用清水冲洗所有工具，确保测斜仪器工程师对入井工具条件满意，同时按照要求获取数据

32.The jar must be laid out-limit of 50 rotating hours for 8"jar in 26"hole.At
this point the MP liners can be changed to 6" at any time, but must always
have pumps available for well control.

必须甩震击器，8"震击器在26"井眼中旋转时间不超过56个小时。此时泥浆泵缸套可以随时换为6"，但是必须保证有泵可用于井控。
