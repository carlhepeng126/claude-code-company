#  英文钻井程序中常用的缩写

中东地区汇聚了欧美各大石油公司，在其钻井程序中会见到许多术语缩写，今天笔者列出了常见的英文术语缩写，希望对国外施工的钻井作业人员提供一定的参考。

1.AZI(Azimuth)-方位角

2.BHP(Bottom Hole Pressure)-井底压力

3.BPV(Back Pressure Valve)-回压阀

4.BHA(Bottom Hole Assembly)-井底钻具组合

5.CHH(Casing Head Housing)-套管头本体

6.DDR(Daily Drilling Report)-钻井日报

7.DHL(Down Hole Loss)-井下损耗

8.DPZ(Distinct Permeable Zone)-明显渗透层

9.DOT(Drill Off Test)-试钻

10.DROPS(Dropped Object Awareness and Prevention )-坠落物防范

11.ECD(Equivalent Circulating Density)-当量循环密度

12.ERP(Emergency Response Plan)-应急计划

13.ESIPC(External Sleeve Inflatable Packer Collar)-外套筒膨胀式封隔器接箍

14.FIT(Formation Integrity Test)-地层整体性测试）

15.HKLD(Hook Load)-大钩载荷

16.Inc(Inclination)-井斜

17.KOP(Kick Off Point)-造斜点

18.LCM(Lost Circulation Material)-堵漏材料

19.MOC(Management of Change)-变更管理

20.MW(Mud Weight)-泥浆比重

21.MSIPC(Multi Stage Inflation Packer Collar)-多级膨胀式封隔器接箍

22.PPE(Personal Protective Equipment)-劳保

23.PPFG(Predicted Pressure of Formation Graph)-地层图表压力预测

24.PV(Plastic Viscosity)-塑性粘度

25.ROP(Rate of Penetration)-机械钻速

26.RPM(Revolutions Per Minute)-转速

27.SCE(Solid Control Equipment)-固控设备

28.DV(Stage Collar)-分级箍

29.SPM(Stroke Per Minute)-冲次每分钟

30.TOC(Top of Cement)-水泥返高

31.TDS(Top Drive System)-顶驱系统

32.TFA(Total Flow Area)-总流道面积

33.TVDMSL,TVDSS(True Vertical Depth Measured fromMean Seal Level)-海拔垂深

34.TVDBRT(True Vertical Depth Measured From Rotary Table)-从转盘面所测垂深

35.THA(Tubing Head Adapter)-油管头接头

36.THS(Tubing Head Spool)-油管头四通

37.UCS(Upper Casing Spool)-套管上四通

38.WSL(Well Site Leader)-现场负责人

39.WSG(Well Site Geologist)-现场地质师

40.POP(Put On Production)-投产

41.PRC(Pad Release Certification)-井场解除证明
