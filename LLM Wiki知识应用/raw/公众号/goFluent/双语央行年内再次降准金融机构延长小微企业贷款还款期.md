O  n  November 25,  China's central bank announced to cut the  **reserve
requirement ratio** for banks, a key move to boost financing of the real
economy and buffer the economic impact of a rise in COVID-19 cases, experts
said.  11月25日，中国央行宣布降低银行的 **准备金率** ，这是促进实体经济融资和缓冲新冠肺炎病例上升对经济影响的关键举措。

_reserve requirement ratio(RRR) 存款准备金率_

_finance 融资_

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/E7N3RfrpJysn68Hlu3Y1rWYr3GOaRicV5nv5GMw36bDDPwoX6HU0MJbnOzMLicr6Fuphffhludd03LKLiauJq52IQ/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)
_The People's Bank of China. [Photo/VCG]_ People's Bank of China, the
country's central bank cut the RRR for financial institutions by 0.25
percentage point on Dec 5, which will release about 500 billion yuan ($69.75
billion) in  **long-term funds** .
中国央行中国人民银行于12月5日将金融机构的RRR削减0.25个百分点，将释放约5000亿元人民币（697.5亿美元）的 **长期资金** 。

_long-term funds 长期资金_

  
The RRR refers to the proportion of money that lenders must hold as reserves.
China last cut the RRR in April by 0.25 percentage point.
RRR指的是贷款人必须持有的作为准备金的资金比例。中国上一次将RRR下调是在4月，下调了0.25个百分点。  The PBOC said the
latest cut is aimed at keeping liquidity reasonably ample, helping the economy
achieve reasonable growth while lifting development quality and increasing
long-term funding sources of  **financial institutions** so that they can
better support industries and ** smaller businesses  ** severely affected by
COVID-19.  中国人民银行表示，最近的削减旨在保持合理的流动性，帮助经济实现合理增长，同时提高发展质量，增加 **金融机构**
的长期资金来源，以便更好地支持受新冠肺炎严重影响的行业和 **小型企业** 。

_financial institutions金融机构_

_smaller businesses 小型企业_

"The cut can boost market confidence and buffer the  **economic downward
pressures** brought about by a rise in COVID-19 cases, thus helping stabilize
economic growth and employment," said Wang Qing, chief macroeconomic analyst
at Golden Credit Rating International.
东方金诚国际信用评估有限公司首席宏观分析师王青表示，此次降准可以增强市场信心，缓冲新冠肺炎病例上升带来的 **经济下行压力**
，从而有助于稳定经济增长和就业。

_economic downward pressures 经济下行压力_

_economic growth经济增长_

The RRR cut may trigger a decrease in the over-five-year  **loan prime rate**
— a key ** benchmark lending rate  ** on which lenders base their ** mortgage
rates  ** — next month, and thus help bolster homebuying demand and stabilize
the real estate sector, Wang said.  RRR的削减可能会引发下个月五年以上 **贷款市场报价利率** 的下降——贷款人
**抵押贷款利率** 所依据的关键 **基准贷款利率** ，从而有助于提振购房需求并稳定房地产行业。

_loan prime rate贷款市场报价利率_

_benchmark lending rate 基准贷款利率_

_mortgage rates抵押贷款利率_

"Whether there will be more cuts in interest rates and the RRR depends on the
COVID-19 situation and economic data," said Shan Hui, chief China economist at
Goldman Sachs.  高盛首席中国经济学家Shan Hui说，是否会进一步降息和RRR取决于新冠肺炎疫情和经济数据。  While China's
**monetary policy** is projected to remain overall accommodative in the first
half of next year, policy normalization may start from the second quarter if
the economy gradually overcomes COVID-related disruptions, Shan said.
Shan说，虽然中国的 **货币政策** 预计将在明年上半年保持总体宽松，但如果经济逐步克服疫情导致的中断，政策正常化可能会从第二季度开始。

_monetary policy货币政策_

Moreover, the PBOC flagged the possibility of a future rise in ** inflation
** in its third-quarter monetary policy report, which experts said has implied
a more cautious stance in maintaining an accommodative monetary policy.
此外，中国人民银行在其第三季度货币政策报告中指出了未来可能 **通货膨胀** 上升，专家表示，这意味着在保持宽松的货币政策方面采取更谨慎的立场。  

_inflation通货膨胀_
