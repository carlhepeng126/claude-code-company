The Customer Success Management (CSM) supports the sales representative during
the post-sales customer lifecycle (customer adoption, on-going support,
programme optimization.) CSMs are the main interface for all programme-related
inquiries, they position themselves as trusted advisors. They are ensuring
customer retention by optimizing the client’s ROI by delivering day to day
support. CSMs work closely with the Sales representative to make sure the
solution delivered is aligned with what was sold by the Sales representative.

  

**Job description**

-Executing portal manager demo 

-Monitoring of procedure of training project 

-Creation manual report 

-Implementation communication plan 

-Help prepare written material for pre-implementation and deployment of new  clients 

-Coordinate between teams to ensure coherent and timely deployment of training 

-Be the key contact person for the Client Project Manager 

-Schedule global steering committee meetings as agreed on with the client 

-Make sure that all client deadlines and requirements are met 

-Creation of ppt docs to show trainings' results 

-SalesForce task Management 

-Management samitaly account 

-Request for extraction 

-Request for manual report 

-Other tasks as requested 

**Qualification**

-Bachelor degree 

-Analytical thinking 

-Organizational talent 

-Customer-oriented attitude 

-Communication skills 

-Passion for service quality 

-Social skills / ability to work in a team 

-Problem-solving skills 

-Sales mindset
