ENGLISH TALK SHOW  

  

** Topic: Making speeches more formal.  **

  

It is imporant for us to make speech more formal when we are on the
conference, in the competition, group work and other business environment.
Also, if we want to analysis or share sth seriously to sb., we need to speak
formally. Because, formal s  peech makes speakers more professional and
reliable.

  

The most effective way to make a formal speech is using formal language.
Firstly, we could avoid to use some personal words like I,you. As an example,
we could say the sentence like it is always argued that instead of I think
that. Secondly, the passive voice is also useful. Thirdly, complex sentence
with plenty of linking words is also a typical feature of formal language. For
example, the original sentence is “Apple is on the table. Lemon is on the
table too”, we could make it more formal like “In addition to apple, another
fruit on the table is lemon”.
