**路演主旨**

  

本作品展示  **武汉长江新城项目**
具有高投资回报率和创新性投融资结构的明星项目，对公司布局长江经济带，打开华中市场具有重大的经济与政治意义。同时介绍面对疫情如何运用 “金点子”解决
**项目落地** 将遇到的各种困难。

  

**路演要点**

  

作品主要从项目与众不同的亮点，投标过程中将面临的重点难点以及独辟蹊径的解决方案三部分介绍了武汉长江新城项目从前期跟踪到促进落地全过程展望。  

  

首先，阐述了武汉长江新城项目整体的  **重要性** 以及起步区在长江新城项目中的  **核心地位**
，通过数据直观展示其拥有高投资回报率和优质投融资结构的独特优势，实现  **小投资撬动大工程** 。

  

其次，项目将面临  **四个重难点**
：项目包装难（政府缺乏独立财政，难以包装），投融资难（传统银团模式弊端），竞争压力大（当地央企强势）以及年初新冠疫情带来的影响（进度）。

  

最后，针对重难点研发出  **对症下药**
的特色解决方案：1）对接武汉市财政局，打包成PPP项目；2）创新性引进长江大保护基金和松散银团模式；3）联合六家行业顶尖企业并发挥旗舰作用；4）发扬中信精神，各岗位疫情时期各司其职，完美完成任务。

  

**路演文稿**

  

Dear leaders and colleagues, as representatives of the Wuhan Yangtze River New
City project, it is a great honor to share our experience and stories with
you. Our presentation will introduce the project, list possible difficulties
and the “golden ideas” which we used to solve them.

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/x3uBG25kUEoOkLCiaiae6gSKfThqHgZr7Op0686Jy8xWJJ1WCHJ7QRRmqgz4wTdKrDy4HeWTdUWS31ds4V4dicYtw/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

Firstly, I will introduce  **the background information**

The planning and construction of Yangtze River New City is based on advanced
concepts and global vision. The Yangtze River New City will be built as an
exhibition area for the highest achievement of urban development and a
demonstration area for future cities. It will become  **an important carrier**
to support Wuhan's transformation from middle and late industrialization to
the post-industrialization.

And now, Yangtze River New City has been declared as  **a state-level new
area** , which is similar to Xiong’an New area. This project has great
regional influence, strong social attention, wide coverage of industries and
high investment and construction standards. This project is of strategic
significance for CITIC Construction and CITIC Group. It is helpful to develop
infrastructure business and build CITIC brand.

The starting area project is important for the construction of Yangtze River
New City; it is  **the largest PPP project** supported by Wuhan Finance Bureau
over the years. And now, the starting area project has become the primary
reconstruction project in Hubei Province and Wuhan City  **after the
COVID-19** . This project can bring substantial benefits while responding to
the national call for reconstruction.

CITIC Construction has invested less than￥200 million, but obtained this 4
billion project, and the income of project management fee is nearly  **￥500
million** . The cost can be recovered at the end of the construction period,
realizing the concept of leveraging large project with small investments.

  

And next, my teammate Zhang Li will introduce the possible difficulties.

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/x3uBG25kUEoOkLCiaiae6gSKfThqHgZr7Ow3IuaNBGTwy13Gg61fS7jDo5NVubDFJU5sTriabDEhLhmuGGsHa5Xkg/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

  

We briefly summarize  **the difficulties** into four aspects：

  

The first difficulty is  **project packaging** . Yangtze River New City hasn’t
become a national district, which doesn’t have independent finance. Although
the funds can be gathered through selling lands, it will slow down the
development of new district dramatically.

  

And the second difficulties are  **investment and financing** . In terms of
investment, a strong financial investor with low cost of capital is required
to fulfil the investment demand. As for financing, the traditional syndicate
model will reduce the project return rate significantly. And its management
process is inefficient.

  

The next difficulty is  **the pressure from external competitors** . Local
construction enterprises such as China Construction Third Engineering Bureau,
Gezhouba Group and China Railway Construction have controlled Hubei market for
many years. So, it is more like “pull a tooth out of tiger’s mouth” if we want
to stand out and acquire this project in the market full of fierce
competition.

  

The last difficulty is  **the influence of COVID-19** . As the center of
domestic epidemic in this year, Wuhan, this heroic city, was totally closed
from January 23rd to April 8th to prevent the spread of Corona Virus. As we
all know, this virus not only influence the global and China economy, but also
bring massive uncertain troubles to this project. So, these are the four main
problem we meet. Now please let me introduce my colleague Xie Boyang to tell
you how we use the “golden ideas” to solve these problems. Thank you.

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/x3uBG25kUEoOkLCiaiae6gSKfThqHgZr7O2xWmv4CR7wHpMODk6N2YHlRlhRBUMWPuoclxC2wL24Upgh217CPVcg/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

  

Thanks to my colleague Zhang Li for point out the difficulties we faced in
this project. To solve them, we summarize the experience from the other
projects and overcome the challenge with golden ideas. First of all,  **pack
it as PPP project** . I explored the core needs of our clients, we knew that
Changjiang River New City does not have independent finance. So, that means
they cannot use the traditional EPC model to set up this new project. So we
innovatively pack three individual projects into one PPP project by using
Wuhan Finance Bureau as the repayment source to solve this problem.

  

Secondly, it is to  **optimize investment and financing structure** . In terms
of capital, we take the advantage of characteristics of this project include a
recycling water plant. So we introduce Changjiang River Protection Fund and
raised 700 million with very low cost. As for financing, instead of syndicate
banking, we contact multiple banks individually, which is like an open bidding
for banks. So, it helps us to reduce the loan interest rate dramatically.

  

The third one is  **combined fleet model** . According to the feature of this
project, six joint members are all the leaders in their industries, are
selected to form a combination. Some of them are good at tunnel and bridge
construction, some of them are good at urban road construction, some of them
are good at urban planning, and some of them are the top one in the water
treatment. So, CITIC plays the role as a flagship to enable these six joint
members to work as a team. As a result, we achieve six combinations are larger
than six.

  

Finally, it is  **the CITIC sprit** . In face of Wuhan COVID-19 in this year,
as employees of CITIC, we did not back off. Colleagues outside Wuhan worked at
home, colleagues in Wuhan tried their best to use resources to finish the
schedule.
