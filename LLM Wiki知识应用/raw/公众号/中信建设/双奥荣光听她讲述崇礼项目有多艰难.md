“百年正风华·奋进创未来”中信建设2021年职工英语业务技能竞赛演讲季军作品，国内区事业部方媛媛的《Ice and Snow Town of Taizi
City Chongli – the “Double Olympic builders”》。
崇礼太子城冰雪小镇项目因其特殊的政治意义，公司将此项目定义为“一号工程”。此项目传承了鸟巢项目“建设奥运，一生光荣”的精神。我们的员工在抗击严寒和疫情的各种挑战下，工作却始终没有放弃，做好了核心配套工程，写好了中信建设“双奥建设者”的故事。

  

作品要点为：1.  由于气候、疫情等原因，崇礼项目的建设变得十分困难。  但我们的建设者始终坚守一线，攻坚克难，团结协作，为顺利实施做出更大的贡献。2.
在中信集团的关怀和支持下，由各参建方组成的联合舰队展示了高水平的项目实施能力，  这是中信建设服务奥运建设的荣耀。  3\.
我们要把建设崇礼项目当作一项政治任务，以“不忘初心，牢记使命”，提高政治站位，坚持党建引领项目实施，按照习近平总书记对冬奥会指出的“办好冬奥会，是党和国家的一件大事。
”4.  在竞争激烈的市场中，作为新一代的年轻人，我们要敢为人先、开拓创新，坚持不懈，追求更高的目标。  最后，让我们共同努力，共创更美好的未来！

  

  
** 文  ** ｜方媛媛（国内区事业部）

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/x3uBG25kUEr0aLY8sia5YCCaGjyCic3cqs8eQRJTx8DHAHicculGkwaBs7zDYXqt4rIRUqibQaP3LnhR38l5c5Zgmw/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

  
Before I begin, I would like to share a short story that motivates me
everyday. During the Covid-19 outbreak, as the world changed, I witnessed my
coworkers who are constructors working in the front line pushed through in a
very difficult time. It was freezing outside in Chongli, the temperature could
be down to 40 below zero degrees. There were many obstacles for my coworkers
to overcome in such extreme condition, whereas they always take the
responsibility and task in hand to ensure the project is on the right track.
As a CITIC employee, I am proud, and I feel our team is getting stronger
because of their attitudes.

  

Some of you may ask, what is Chongli project? Today I will give an overview of
the project, how it strives to support 2022 Beijing Winter Olympics, and what
we as the younger generation can contribute for a better future.

  

Firstly, as an important supporting facilities for the 2022 Beijing Winter
Olympics, Chongli Project is comprised of a culture and commercial street,
exhibition venues, hotels, ski schools, sport rehabilitation centers, and
green commercial residential housing. Due to its special political
significance, the company has defined the project as "No. 1 project".

  

Here you can watch a promotional video of the 2022 winter Olympic games. It is
a beautiful song right? The mission of Chongli project is to support Winter
Olympic Games and inherit the spirit of our previous Olympic project which is
Bird Nest Stadium of the 2008 Beijing Summer Olympic Games. As the General
Contractor, us Citic Construction will become the “Double Olympic builders”.
It is our honor to serve the Olympic Games for our country.

  

During the construction process, leaders from our organization and Citic Group
have visited the project site. They visited the front line employees and
guided work. Following President Xi’s idea of "four ways to run the Olympic
Games", we should strengthen our political responsibility, stimulate the two
centenaries goals and design our construction work elaborately. Han Shaolong,
the general manager of domestic business division, announced that in order to
achieve the goal of high standard performance and improve the effectiveness of
our construction team, the company has specially established six "youth
commandos" as the vanguard of Chongli project.

  

Now, in the highly competitive industry, we the younger generation witness the
growth of our motherland. We must pioneer with innovative insight to build a
faster development path such as pursuing higher goals and setting feasible
plan. It is necessary to be persistent. Any great success requires passion and
persistence to capture our dream. Although we may encounter obstacles, we can
always rise from hardships. Not constraining ourselves to thinking inside and
box but be open minded and non-judgmental. Chongli project provides us a great
opportunity to strengthen our responsibility and team spirits. By applying
ourselves with learned skills, I believe we will be able to make greater
contributions to our country and succeed in today’s globalization of
businesses.

  

In conclusion, my dear fellows, let us make great efforts and build a bright
future together!

** Q:  ** I think you communicated the spirit of the project really
effectively, the question I have is what do you think will be the legacy of
the project for chong li and also for the wider range?

  

** A:  ** I think the legacy is the honor for the city as well as for the
country. It is a place where gathers people from all over the world to see the
game, and you can also bring your friends and your family there. It is also a
great place to practice cross culture communication. And even after the game,
you can still visit there and enjoy the city and China. It is a very beautiful
place for everyone to gather.
