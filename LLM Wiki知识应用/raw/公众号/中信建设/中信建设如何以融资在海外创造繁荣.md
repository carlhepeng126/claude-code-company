中信建设2019年英语竞赛个人英语演讲“最佳表现奖”作品，计划财务部杨文潇带来的  ** Fund Prosperity  ** _**** _
介绍了项目融资对于“一带一路”海外项目开发的重要意义，项目融资工具的类型、发展历程以及关键要点，希望未来能够利用融资工具为公司赢得更多项目。

  

Good morning. I am Yang Wenxiao from Planning and Finance Department，a member
of the project financing team. My topic today is about financing’s role in
international project development and what’s more, how to fund prosperity.  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/x3uBG25kUEoLrDiaDiadUTrT3ruDengKY6wKwNdsfv5qMdFYCicjv1XXXMpOt7nVD5UmQuAJChMbyI2I147aUZ7Qg/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

  
First, let me ask a question: what comes to your mind when you hear Belt and
Road Initiative? Some might say it’s just a giveaway of money. But I think
people in this venue, we know better. It is China’s plan on facilitating
worldwide development and prosperity.  
Let’s look at some figures, the World Banks says that by 2040, the world needs
more than 97 trillion USD for global infrastructure investment, and in Asia
alone there is a financing gap of more than 450 billion USD per year.  
So financing is the key. How do we fund projects?  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/x3uBG25kUEoLrDiaDiadUTrT3ruDengKY6vjXUCbweOqr98lZZtJ27Ttv6FTd8nW6HkicMvRPpK5KHSK0zSQqgeQw/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

  
Most of the financing tools dates back to history. First is Export Credit.
During the 1950 to 1960s, the newly independent countries in Africa and Asia
have huge infrastructure demand. That’s when the Western countries invented
export credit financing, such a buyer’s credit，seller’s credit, to help their
companies to win these projects. Traditional export credit is based on
sovereign credit, it requires sovereign government or SOE as the borrower or
guarantor.  
  
Then in the 1970-1980s, after the oil crisis, government support in
infrastructure slowed down. Then project financing is introduced. It uses
future cash flow of the project instead of guarantee, as security for
repayment.  
Despite of these tools, we still face a world has huge financing gap. And
that’s why Belt and Road is introduced as a comprehensive solution. You might
ask, why China, why do we believe we can solve the problems the West couldn’t
have done in centuries?  
I will try to explain by the example of a company I worked with in South
Africa in 2015. It’s called Bridge Taxi Finance. It made big success from
providing mortgage to poor taxi drivers for new vehicles. Being called taxi,
they are actually small buses carries 10-20 people, and they are the major
commuting tool for millions of South Africans from rural areas. That’ s when I
realized financing is not just about business, but about connecting people
with their livelihood and future, like a bridge.  
China is in the right position to provide new solution, because there is more
than one million Chinese in Africa, workers, managers, technicians, farmer,
salesman, small business owners, who are showing Africans how to get connected
with the world economy with their own hands. This is the one thing that the
has not been done in Africa. And CITIC Construction has also contributed to
this process. Highways and phosphate mining investment in Algeria, housing,
schools, agriculture projects and research centers in Angola, these are
connecting local people with a diversified and sustainable economy. And we,
members of the financing team are proud to be part of this. We hope we can
work with our colleagues and partners to forge more connections, more bridges
of financing between people and between countries.  
Thank you very much.  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/x3uBG25kUEoLrDiaDiadUTrT3ruDengKY6s1J33rhzzARicibSAM5XCpzRJudoEvLk0dXMomZQXl4EQntruOyTXb4w/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

Q: My question is, in your opinion, what is the biggest challenge in your job？
A: I think the biggest challenge is balancing between the requirement of the
owner and our responsibility of keeping the best interest of CITIC
Construction.  Q: Can you name five of your biggest competitors in the world?
A: Yes. ACS from Spain, Hochtief from Germany, Vinci from Francce, Skanska
from Sweden and Kajima from Japan.  Q: And which one is the biggest?  A: ACS.
Q: Where is it from?  A: Spain.  
Q: Do you think you can compete with them and become world’s biggest
contractor in 10 years? And how?  A: Yes. We have seen rapid growth of Chinese
companies in many fields. We work hard and surpass leading western companies.
Because we are Chinese, that’s what we do.
