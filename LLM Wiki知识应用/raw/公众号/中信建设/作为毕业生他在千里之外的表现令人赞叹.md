** 文  ** ｜花诚屿（非洲区事业部）

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/x3uBG25kUEoBKZuCmdlmAUNUBtBSpQt6UyR8xnAickWfyptNx46wxdcVRGxsvXGfgflanDDgfFMJC5xdiaFnZRRg/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

  
Ladies and Gentlemen, good afternoon, my name is 花诚屿，and I come from the
African Division, today I would like to start my speech with a photo here.  

  

On April 7th of 2018, a suspected gas attack in an area controlled by Syrian
opposition forces killed more than 70 people. At that time, the Syrian
opposition, the United States, and the United Kingdom made groundless
accusation against Syrian government forces of using chemical weapons to
attack civilians.

  

On the 9th, the UN Security Council held an emergency meeting on Syria's
chemical weapons. The Syrian representative to the UN—this man in the photo,
raged against the US for launching a war of aggression based on lies and evil
deeds.

  

But as soon as his speech began, the U.S. and British representatives left
their seats and turned away. This "diatribe" did not prevent the U.S., British
and French forces from launching airstrikes against Syria on the 13th. Poor
Syrians, the light of peace was extinguished again!

  

** The world is still a jungle society where the strong oppresses the weak  **
, so what if the weak and small countries are justified? Weapons of criticism
cannot replace the criticism of weapons.

  

As a Chinese, did this remind you of anything?

  

For me, when I understood the story in this photo, I was immediately taken
back to China a century ago.  **Gu Weijun** , who spoke fiercely at the Paris
Peace Conference in 1919 and did everything he could to defend the interests
of his country, was also ignored by the powers.

  

** A hundred years ago  ** , something almost similar happened, except that it
was not Syria, but China, which was also ravaged by war and turmoil.

  

At the Paris Peace Conference, the rights of the defeated Germany in Shandong
were transferred to Japan instead of being returned to China. And China, as
the victorious country in World War I,  ** suffered such humiliation of a
defeated country  ** , Chinese representative Gu Weijun's speech before
refusing to sign was also deafening and heartfelt, but there was nothing that
could be done about it. In the situation where the might is right, the refusal
to sign was the only thing that the poor and weak Chinese representatives
could do.

  

Faced with the humiliation of the time, our forefathers endured the
humiliation and forged ahead, the victory in the war against Japan, the
founding of New China, reform and opening up, regaining Hong Kong and Macao,
and economic take-off. ...... These milestones,  **set up by the blood and
sweat of our martyrs** , lifted China up step by step.

  

Today, we are a nation with  ** independent diplomatic power  ** ; a nation
with  ** the second largest economy in the world  ** ; a nation that has
**achieved comprehensive poverty eradication** ; a nation that is  ** striving
to build a socialist modern power  ** . Looking at Syria today, I can't help
but sighing for the truth that almost a hundred years have passed and China
has change while the world is still the same. And the reason why China now no
longer has to worry about the recurrence of the nightmare is not because the
powers have become kind, but because: "  **The truth is within the range of
the cannons** .  "

  

We are lucky enough that our youth was born in an era when food and clothing
are plentiful. There is no war, and our country is making progress along the
way.  **We have no reason not to fight in this precious era, only struggle can
reflect the value of youth** . Strive for ourselves!

Thank you very much for your question, as I studied Portuguese in Portugal for
1 year, and my major work here in Angola is translation between Chinese and
Portuguese. I think the biggest challenge for me when I first came here,
because I’ve only stayed here for about  **3 months** .

  

And when I firstly came here I found that the Portuguese spoken here by the
locals is  **not quite the same as what I heard in Portugal** , in other
words, my communication with the locals was ** not very smooth  ** , but I
kept looking for  **in-work opportunities to communicate with the locals** ,
later in my work, and now I gradually ** adapted to the local accent  ** .

  

And I was also always dreaming to travel the world by working and studying, so
here in Angola I think will be new phase in my life for me to ** explore the
world  ** , this is my answer, thank you.
