01

视 频 文 稿

  
The Yellow River is a symbol of surging waves in the famous story "Yu the
Great Controls the Water," and it embodies the grandeur described by Li Bai as
"flowing to the sea, never to return." It holds a special place in the hearts
of Chinese people as the nurturing "Mother River" filled with hope. On
September 18, 2019, President Xi Jinping elevated ** the Yellow River Strategy
to a national level  ** . After two years of careful planning, ** the Jinan
International Ecological Port PPP project  ** was born as a result of this
strategy. On September 18, 2021, the progress meeting for  **193 key projects
promoting ecological conservation and high-quality development** in the Yellow
River Basin took place at the site of the sub-project's sewage treatment
plant. This marks the official start of CITIC Construction's story on the
banks of the Yellow River, at the foot of Mount Tai. This project, situated in
the northeastern part of Jinan, is surrounded by the Yellow River and Xiaoqing
River. It adjoins Jinan Yaoqiang International Airport, covering an area of 26
square kilometers. Additionally, it lies within the starting region for the
transformation of old and new energy sources in Jinan, becoming the second
such zone after Xiong'an New Area on a national scale. Furthermore, the
project enjoys close proximity to Jinan East Railway Station and Dongjia
Freight Station. The "Qilu" China-Europe freight train, originating from
Dongjia Freight Station, directly reaches 24 countries and 55 cities along the
Belt and Road Initiative.  

This project, including  **14 sub-projects** involving a span of categories
varying from civil engineering, municipal development, urban afforestation to
road construction, has a total investment scale of 9.899 billion. Among the
scale of the road construction project are Xinhang Avenue, Jiaxuan Road and
Yuntong Street. The three roads will serve as urban trunk roads upon
completion and shall establish the spinal axis of regional transportation,
providing arterial support for the formation of regional economic and
logistics center.

  

Regional development  **relies on talents** . To ensure the residence of
talented personnel, constructions of talent apartments and expert buildings
have also been stepped up.  **The construction of apartments for young
talents** is beneficial to improving the life quality and work enthusiasm of
entrepreneurial talents and is conducive to urban development goals of the
Experimental Industrial Park, catalyzing the Sino-European Equipment
Manufacturing Town to develop into an Industrial Park both salubrious and
suitable.

  

" ** Lucid waters and lush mountains are the most valuable assets.  ** " To
solve the urgent sewage treatment needs in the region while preserving the
ecological environment and high-quality development of the Yellow River Basin,
the sewage treatment plant is among the earliest completed sub-projects. It
adopts international leading treatment processes and integrates MVR
technology, striving to become one of the top-class sewage treatment plants
throughout the country. The treatment for industrial and civil sewage is
officially in operation and has maintained an outstanding performance. The
plant's advanced equipment and professional team have ensured the efficiency
and quality of sewage treatment. The processed sewage meets the discharging
standards of the Xiaoqing River Basin, providing adequate environmental
protection, and has made significant contribution to the ecological balance of
surrounding regions.

  

The project has clearly demonstrated scale effects in terms of industry
clustering in the surrounding area. The airport economic has three leading
industries: new generation of information technology, the intelligent
manufacturing, and airport orienting modern service industries. At present,
there are over  **1,000 companies including 19 Fortune Global 500 and 23
China’s top 500 companies** . This is a cluster zone for German automotive
companies, such as Bosch, Continental, and Hangster, producing instruments,
steering systems, and turbines. Here, we have Fudi Power Battery with the
latest generation of fully automated lithium battery production processes.

  

And Yifei Technology,  **a high-tech industrial robotics enterprise** . Its
independently developed Xunyi series of high-speed parallel robots have broken
foreign monopolies and filled domestic gaps. The park has made great efforts
into research and development. It has made a cooperate intention with the
world top three institutions, the Fraunhofer-Gesellschaft in Germany. The
relevant laboratory will be located in our PPP sub-project, the China-Europe
Innovation Center, which can graetly enhance the region's level of digital
empowerment.

  

“  **The Yellow River descends from the sky and journeys to the East China
Sea, etching thousands of miles in my heart.** ” All young employees of Jinan,
Project Company will continue to pour our youthful energy into our motherland
with unwavering enthusiasm and a professional work attitude. Guided by the
enterprise spirit of "prioritizing the common good, upholding integrity and
harmony, daring to lead, and pursuing excellence." We are committed to
fulfilling the mission of "practicing national strategies and contributing to
national rejuvenation." Together, we will constantly create new possibilities
for the future.
