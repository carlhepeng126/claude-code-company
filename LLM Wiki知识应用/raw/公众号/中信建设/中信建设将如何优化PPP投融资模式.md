**路演主旨**

  

本作品提出  **Co-struction（联合建设）** 概念，展现中信建设在新常态下积极探索国内项目联合开发新模式。公司将通过
**优化PPP投融资模式** ，引入  **BIM技术** 搭建协同作业新平台，以及  **整合合作伙伴** 商业优势及业务资源，为业主提供项目
**全生命周期** 的优质服务。

  

**路演要点**

  

本作品通过将Construction替换为Co-struction（“联合”建设），展现中信建设积极携手各合作伙伴，探索项目联合开发新模式。  

  

首先，公司在坚持“小投资撬动大工程”的前提下，以PPP模式与地方政府展开合作，并积极探索投融资新模式，  **引入险资、PPP基金** 等财务投资人。

  

其次，技术创新。装配式技术在建筑行业中得到了广泛的应用，结合公司为客户提供项目全生命周期服务的定位及需求，拟采用建筑信息模型（Building
Information Modeling，BIM）技术，搭建一个业主、工程师、施工方等各方  **协同作业的平台**
，实现设计、施工、运营等环节的信息化管理。

  

最后是加强内部协同和外部合作，在项目实施的不同阶段做好  **“牵头人”角色**
，整合伊藤忠和盛裕等合作伙伴商业优势及业务资源，促进产业链整合，带动区域经济发展。

  

**路演文稿**

  

**Intro:**

Hi everyone, we are so honorable to be here again. So today, we’re gonna show
you something you already know, and something you don't know yet. The thing
you’ve already known is PPP, and when we talk about PPP, we talk about the
government cooperate with private capital to share both interest and risk. So
far, we have finished many great PPP projects, including the industrial parks
in Nanjing and Ziyang.

This year has been a turning point for all of us, we suffered, we fought, and
we recovered. We have to find something unique to fit this ‘New Normal’
economy and our 5-year plan.

So today, let me proudly introduce  **‘CITIC Co-struction’,** a refined model
that could guide our next five years.

What is Co-struction? It is a new way of construction with 3 new parts, the
finance, the technology, and our favorite word, synergy. And now, let me
invite my teammates to show you each part of Co-struction.

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/x3uBG25kUEqTK8tNy3qQWAtiaDfiacf0Kqv8jV8j8wDmYngltSSJZ8OibS2OChGKsjQ262bhlmyWR5UD6hicpsoTYg/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

  

**Finance:**

Financing demand of PPP projects can be tough. According to regulations
enacted by the state council, the self-owned fund of a PPP project must reach
**20% -25%** in order to implement the project. Thus, the financing of the
**self-owned fund** is one of the most crucial part in project financing. But
don’ t worry, CITIC Construction has the power to raise money quickly and
ensure our project. We have established two fund companies,  **Xinheng and
Xinchuang** . Their rolls are fund managers, and help us to organize the
industrial funds.

Right now, our financial investors are mainly PPP funds, and we are also
constantly seeking opportunity to innovate our financing structure. For
example, we are trying to introduce  **Insurance capitals** as our financial
investors.

Take  **CITIC prudential** as an example. As an insurance company, their fund
is super  **stable and abundant** to cover our project, and they are
interested in long-term return project with national key zones. Our PPP
project’s return and characteristics can just match their interest so I’m sure
our project is just what they are looking for. Investing method is negotiable,
they can do either equity or debt, it depends on how we would like to design
our capital structure. Not least to mention, if we work with CITIC prudential,
both of us will have less due diligence work to do since we are familiar with
each other’s background, and it’s easier to corporate within one group. Thus
we can squeeze the working time, speed up the paperwork, and launch our
project as soon as possible.

Now let’s invite our technology guy!

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/x3uBG25kUEqTK8tNy3qQWAtiaDfiacf0KqaURurLkOSnolE2YaVRCwXiayyw0bOTiaVF7gdy0MNbKgaC4JPWkaamVw/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

  

**Technology:**  

Prefabricated building is not new. We have used this stuff in our  **Nanjing
project** , but the out-coming is not good, far from our expectation, why?
Because prefabricated building has  **two weak points** : one is
transportation cost is expensive, the other is poor quality control. These two
results in severe delay in our projects.

But this time, we heavily introduce  **BIM** into our prefabricated building.
With BIM, we can  **virtually plan** , construct and manage our buildings or
projects. We can also use BIM to choose  **the right material** ,  **the
perfect supplier and the optimal route** to deliver these material. And to
enable BIM in construction, we can dramatically reduce the mistake and time
cost.

What’s more, PPP rely on project management. With BIM, we can manage the
building’s temperature, the air quality, even the parking lot efficiency. And
to achieve the precision management, BIM can even calculate the best escape
route, which can meet government’s regulation and need.

Now let me bring our company’s secret weapon, synergy.

  

**Synergy:**

Nowadays our country is embracing dual circulation development pattern in
which  **domestic econo** **mic cycl** **e** will play a leading role while
international economy will serve as an extension and supplement. Accordingly,
we follow the regional development strategies and successfully launched  **9
PPP projects** in China, with the total investment exceeding  **100 billion
yuan** . Our services cover  **the whole life cycle** , from industrial
planning, financing, to construction, and operation. We dare to try and
challenge a diverse set of projects, from highways to industrial parks，or even
a new town. You may wonder: how can one single construction company manage
such comprehensive projects? The key is that we are not working alone. Now I
will introduce our synergy mechanism in details.

First of all, we have  **powerful business partners** . We emphasize both
internal and external collaboration. Our internal collaboration includes other
subsidiaries of CITIC Group which nearly covers all business segments such as
financial service, manufacturing, real estate, etc. In terms of external
collaboration, we have cooperated with many business partners such as PPP
fund, the Itochu Japan, Surbana Jurong, as well as local governments.

Okay, so now that we have so many partners on our side, but how can we make
the best of all these resources and investigate an optimal cooperation
strategy? The answer is we encourage each partner to  **maximize their
strength** . As we have mentioned before, we are more than an engineering
contractor, we integrated all sorts of resources and provide one-stop service
for our clients. For instance, if we want to develop an industrial park
project, which is usually complicated. We will invite Surbana Jurong, which is
a company based in Singapore to deliver urban planning and infrastructure
consulting. Then we can have Itochu from Japan to help with industrial
introduction and investment promotion. Especially for many Sino-Japanese
industrial parks, such as the ones in Suzhou, Chengdu, and Dalian, we are
working closely with Itochu to ensure that we can bring in high-quality
Japanese enterprises, capitals, and talents into the park. On Sept. 21, the
joint venture of CITIC construction and Itochu was established, which brings
our strategies collaboration to the next level.

Apparently, one single company cannot become the industry expert in all
fields, but CITIC construction provides  **a perfect platform** involving its
partners, who are the best players in each business, in the grand project.
Together, we will provide a sustainable solution tailored for our client, and
integrate industrial chain to boost the local economy.

  

**Closure:**

So, the idea of Co-struction is only the beginning of our journey. We not only
construct projects for our clients, but also integrate the Industrial Value
Chain to enable the economy’s prosperity to thrive.

We are not a simple construction company, we serve, we finance, we build and
we deliver! In the future, we will explore the Co-struction model in global
market.

So Mr. Chairman, what do you think! Would you like to approve the Co-struction
plan and start a new era for our company?

**Q &A： **

1\. What is the  **most concerns** of these companies, when they are looking
at your projects? What is the key point when they make the investment
decision?

  

We want to invite those insurance companies as our financial investors. The
most important part is that we can provide  **the whole life circle** services
, that is important because all the SOEs can do the construction, but we, as
we instructed before, we have built strategy collaborations with international
companies that they can help us with planning with local government to boost
local economy.

I think the most important thing of us is to  **organize all of our partners**
. We can introduce them to the local government, also the financial investors,
that they would know that we can handle the comprehensive project.

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/x3uBG25kUEqTK8tNy3qQWAtiaDfiacf0KqxeImsLoqOdaBmky7vsuY9YbPM1IOvCWJLmcvDhlNgFlsS5t5v5CDaQ/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

  

2\. How do you overcome these two  **shortcomings** of pre-fabricated
buildings in Nanjing project?

  

The first shortcoming is  **the higher transportation cost** , but with BIM we
can calculate which part should be delivered in the first place; how many
labor do we need; and what’s the nearest supplier near the project, like what
we did in Wuhan.

  

The No.2 shortcoming is  **poor quality control** , so with BIM we can know
which piece need to be repaired or replaced, that’s how we achieve higher
return and lower cost.
