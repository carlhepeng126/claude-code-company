** 正：企业转型发展更该考虑自身发展  **

** Enterprise transformation and development should consider its own
development  **

** 反： ** 企业转型发展更该考虑外部环境  ** **

** Enterprise transformation and development should consider external
environment  **

  

** 陈杨帆  ** ：  I think enterprise transformation should consider its own
development, because it’s just like our family. If you are the member of the
family. If you develop well, and your family will develop well. So I think
enterprise transformation should consider its own development. Of course we
can’t without external environment perform our capacity, but if we don’t
develop ourselves well, the environment will have no mean, will have no things
of it. That’s my opinion.

  

** 徐乐坤  ** ：  I think enterprise transformation should consider external
development. For example, when to consider the government and the environment
or policy. Development, as example for this Coronavirus, we can do many things
during this period, and the government will help us and give us more
economical support.

**6进3：知识问答**

  

** What does SWOT stand for? Why is that important?  **

  

S stands for strength, W stands for weakness, O stands for opportunities, T
stands for threats, S and W are internal factors, O and T are external
factors. The internal factors determine the external performance. And it is
really important, because we can use SWOT strategy to put ourselves in the
right position, understand our situation, find the opportunities, and avoid
the threats. And we can use SWOT strategy to make efficient strategy.
