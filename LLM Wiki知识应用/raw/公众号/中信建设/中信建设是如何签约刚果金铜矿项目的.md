6月20日，中信建设正式签约刚果（金）卡莫阿铜矿回填厂项目。本路演采用了拟人化的手法，主讲人以卡莫阿铜矿的口吻从第三方的视角讲述了卡莫阿铜矿出现的生产困境，以及中信建设如何依托集团协同效应参与到了矿区建设，从而成功地帮助卡莫阿解决了危机。

**Part I Opening**

  

Hello everyone. My name is Kamoa-Kakula. I am the 4th largest undeveloped
copper mine in the world located on the Western Foreland in the Democratic
Republic of the Congo on the wild African continent. You see, I was a sleeping
prince of the Congolese Kingdom before anyone ever found me.

  

I have been lying dormant since the beginning of the world waiting to be
discovered and appreciated for the limitless economic potential I have.

  

I have waited and waited for centuries, only to be rejected and abandoned by
one company after another until Ivanhoe and Zijin came along and pulled me out
of this miserable darkness. I am so happy that I finally could see the world
for myself and offer my unique value for the Congolese Kingdom.

  

Ivanhoe and Zijin were the ones that truly appreciated me for what I am really
worth. They explored me and geared me up and did everything that was boring
but necessary. Now I am just a few projects away from entering my adulthood.

  

One day, Ivanhoe praises my beauty as he always did. He said to me, “you are
the most magnificent copper mine I have ever seen. let me introduce you to a
friend of mine who is very, very rich. I think you will be immensely
interested. He has brought a surprise for your coming of age ceremony.

  

I look at Ivanhoe as he talks nonstop at me and my gaze moves to this little
guy standing next to him that goes by the name CITIC Metal.

  

I smile at him and I couldn’t help but feel incredibly surprised and honored
at the same time because CITIC is such a big name and this billion-dollar
investment from CITIC Metal is definitely going to be game changing and take
things to a whole new level.

  

**Part II Encountering**

  

As the ceremony celebrating my adulthood draws near, all these friends of mine
suddenly run into a big trouble. COVID-19 is in town. This is a Monstrous
virus that drives everyone apart from each other.

  

All the little companies that helped to gear me up and do the construction
work at the site can’t make it back because they are stuck in China where they
have been staying for vacation. As days go by and the date for the ceremony
drawing ever closer, I start to worry about my fate into the future.

* * *

As I start to fell hopeless, CITIC Metal says, I have a brother company right
next door in Angola. His name is CITIC Construction. As members of the same
group, we have in between us an exceptionally strong bond and great synergy.
By bringing into bear this synergy, I think I will get CITIC Construction to
help us out in the middle of this crisis.

Zijin goes，“That being said, has anyone heard any mining activity that CITIC
Construction has been involved in?”

CITIC Metal goes, “CITIC Construction is a force to be reckoned with. Let’s
give him the floor to introduce himself.”

CITIC Construction goes, “We have been working in the neighboring country of
Angola for more than ten years, and have established a unique business model
of joint fleet with our partner companies. By making full use of this joint
fleet model, we have developed the ability to integrate resources in terms of
personnel, machinery and equipment. We firmly believe that with the model of
joint fleet, the level of synergy among CITIC subsidiaries and our experience
in project implementation, we will ride everyone through this emergency and
get everything back on track.”

* * *

  

Everyone: Angola? Your Portuguese speaking employees will be useless in the DR
Congo because we speak French.  

CITIC Construction goes, “Don’t worry. Internally we also have a lot of
synergy. Look, the Algeria Division is sending over French translators as we
speak.”

Everyone, “Ok. We will give you the Backfill Plant project to prove yourself.”

CITIC Construction goes, “Don’t worry. As the Chairman of CITIC Group Zhu
hexin would like to say: Just give me the chance to do it. I will get it done
quickly and perfectly.”

  

* * *

  

I am surprised to find out that our new friend CITIC Construction is not only
bold but also highly organized. By tapping into synergies and the joint fleet
model, he is now fully engaged in the orderly preparation for my coming of age
ceremony. Never had it occur to me that a new player in mining like CITIC
Construction could get used to a new area so quickly and get this project
started so well. He is a wonderful addition to this big family and he deserves
the respect from the others. When being asked about how he has come such a
long way, CITIC Construction always says, as China’s president and general
secretary Xi Jiing encouraged us, we forge an opportunity from a crisis, and a
new horizon from a challenge. I would always smile with joy at this
interesting explanation.

  

**Part III Looking ahead**

  

How time flies. The ceremony marking my adulthood has finally come to a close
as scheduled. I had thought to myself that as the COVID-19 pandemic swept
across the world there’s no way to see the ceremony through.  

  

I couldn’t thank enough my new friend CITIC Construction for his assistance in
that darkest hour. I hear that he has built a good reputation among all the
players and companies around me by opening up the westbound logistic
transportation corridor, using synergy and the joint fleet model to benefit
all of Congo and beyond. And I also see him later everywhere in the DR Congo-
my kingdom building infrastructure, turning this once poverty-struck country
into a much more beautiful place using the fortunes that I have created. One
day when my value is fully explored, I think I will rest in peace.
