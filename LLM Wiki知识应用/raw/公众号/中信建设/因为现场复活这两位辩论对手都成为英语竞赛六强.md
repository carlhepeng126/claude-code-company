**10进6：1对1同题辩论PK**

  

** 正：跨文化沟通，语言是关键  **

** Language is the Key for cross-cultural communication  **

** 反： ** 跨文化沟通，语言不是关键  ** **

** Language is not the Key for cross-cultural communication  **

  

** 张永旭  ** ：  Hello everyone, this is really a good question and my point is
language is the key for cross-culture communication. For communication, we
will use language. Without language, the other party won’t know what’s in your
mind. If they can’t know what’s in your mind, they will not know what you want
to say and what you want them to do, so language is the key.

  

** 万晓宁  ** ：  I think the language gap is not the most important thing in
doing some cross-culture communication, because there is a large culture gap
between us.  T  he culture shock i  s the feeling of uncertainty and
confusion, that we might experience when visiting or doing business in a
society that is different from our own.  And the culture shock is actually the
big deal in the communication, because we don't know how they work, how they
cooperate,  and we might misunderstand each other, that might lead to a bad
business cooperation.

**6进3：知识问答**

  

** How to handle difficult negotiators?  **

  

** 张永旭  ** ：  Ok, how to handle the difficult negotiators? First, we can speak
more quietly than the other party, just like this. Second, we can leave more
space in between our conversation than the other party. And the third, we
should ignore any threat. The threat is only in the negotiation. The last,
it’s better to make a complete conversation and ignore the interruption. Thank
you!

  

** How to enter the conversation in a business situation?  **

  

** 万晓宁  ** ：  So first we should look for people who is already talking. Then
we need to establish eye contact with them and smiling, making they notice me.
And be the first to introduce myself, and also ask them about an open ending
questions, to just continue this conversation.  After that, I need listening
and remembering every people's name, so I can build some potential networking
or make some friends with them.  that’s quite important in business, because
you don’t mess up people’s name.  Besides, I think we could also have some
small talks about weathers or office, just to draw the distance closer.
