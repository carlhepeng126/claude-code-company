![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/x3uBG25kUEpnlxDM34t2cD4bD66HmUbdYKTIFcrRAgckV8qIGeMFSFDmWCZmnyyhByVYE6sNyomMf5vFgv1pdw/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

  
Well, to be honest, just about two minutes ago when I was about to step on the
stage, my hands were shaking and my legs were trembling. I was terribly
nervous, having no idea how I, just  **a green hand of non-English-major-
speaker** , can compete with a group of outstanding peers. But now, your
encouraging eyes and applause make me realize that I am here not to compete,
but to learn and to cooperate.

  

And I do know cooperation is the key word in  **the spirit of our CITIC
Constructors** .

  

01

Local Cooperation

for High Tech Future

Well, let me begin with where I am from, Nanjing. I may highlight the
cooperation of our Nanjing Project with two words: “Huge” for “Tiny”. By the
word “Tiny”, I mean that little device,  **chip** . As we all know, the chip
is the focus of international technology competition in future, and we must
increase our self-sufficiency rate of chips. As for the word “Huge”, I mean
the huge effort Nanjing Project has put into building  **our own City of
Chips** , in order to cooperate with our national high-tech business. For this
purpose, huge building area is under construction at the moment. Phase I, with
its planned area east to Puyun Road and west to Yikai plot, covers more than
400,000 square meters and is to develop with cooperation of all CITIC
Constructors in Nanjing. More top-class technicians and scientists will be
involved into this project, and it is certain that, the problem of chip
blocking will be solved with our joint-effort.

  

02

National Cooperation

for All People’s Well-Being

  

As to our joint-effort for our national future, the buildings of City of Chips
by Nanjing project is just one out of a thousand. Actually, in response to our
Nanjing Project,  **Ziyang** Airport Economic Zone Project together with
**Wuhan** Yangtze River New Town Project, the Domestic Division has formed a
situation of triangle zone along Yangtze River Economic Belt. Over the past
years, our Domestic Division has cooperated together to confront a variety of
great challenges. So as a result, many  **iconic landmark buildings** have
emerged all over the country. So now, let us just review one of them,  **China
National Stadium Project** . It has been recognized as the largest building of
the same type with the most complex inner-structure and the highest technical
requirements. And this landmark in Beijing is constructed with cooperation of
all different sub-units of our company.

  

03

Global Cooperation

for a Shared Future of Mankind

As an international contractor, CITIC Construction has more than  **100
projects** including 79 main projects worldwide. However, as a contractor but
more than a contractor, we are committed to providing diversified services. I
mean, we intend to form cooperative relationship with the other party in the
long term, for which, the  **Algeria** project is telling an example.
Stretching for hundreds of miles across the country, the road will bring
benefits to many locals along the line. In this way, we are providing  **a
belt connecting various social systems** in order to achieve  **a win-win
future** . Thus we are providing our spirit of cooperation. In fact, in many
countries along the Belt and Road Initiative, we are there to construct, to
cooperate.  

  

Here I am, a young trainee among all the industrious CITIC Constructors. I
come to share, and I come to learn. To learn the skills to construct, and to
learn the spirit of cooperation. Hand in hand, and heart to heart. With a
common faith, we CITIC Constructors are together for one dream,  **a shared
future of mankind** . Thank you very much and I am ready for your questions.

So what’s your understanding of this saying and how would you like to
interpret this?

Thank you for your question and I love it.

  

You know, as to my own opinion, the spirit of cooperation is the key thing of
the development of our CITIC Construction. And you know the spirit of
cooperation is also the main point I wanna share with you in my speech. And I
think the spirit of cooperation is also the key thing that make us make
progress. And now I’m going to share with you a story that really happens to
me last year in December. In this story, I think it shows the spirit of
cooperation of our CITIC Constructors.

  

You know, one day in December, one of my leaders gave me an order take photos
for all those pipes in the construction site of our Nanjing project. And you
know, the pipes, are really of great number. And some of them are underground,
and others are not. So at that day, I really have no idea how to do that. So I
asked some of my colleagues to help me. And we together, separated the pipes
into several different groups. And we finished the job very perfectly. Till
now I still remember that day I taking pictures and collecting them late
night.

  

So that’s my answer, thank you, thank you for your question.
