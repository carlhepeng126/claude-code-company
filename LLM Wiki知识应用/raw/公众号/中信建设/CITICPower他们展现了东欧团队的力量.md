疫情当下，中信人  **敢于担当、勇于作为** 的意志品质正是中信力内化于心、外化于行的积极体现。同时，中信力也是一个不断发展的概念，随着我们

**路演要点**

  

何为中信力？  我们将  **CITIC** 五个字母拆解为“使命担当（  **C** ontribution）、融合发展（  **I**
ntegration）、技术进步（  **T** echnology）、开拓创新（  **I** nnovation）和联合协作（  **C**
oordination）”五个方面进行深入阐述，并根据所属层面提出五个金点子，即：

  

1、组建一支长效化、机制化  **公共政策研究团队** ，从政策的参与者向政策的影响者转变；

2、打破部门、区域的划分，搭建一个包含客户、合作伙伴、目标市场等在内的  **数据平台** ；

3、筹建一套以中国优势技术为主的  **对外技术输出模式** ；

4、构建  **本地化和国际化并行** 的项目执行和开发模式；

5、营建一支以人为本、以人为基的  **升级版联合舰队** 。

  

而上述五方面内容有机结合、共同构成“中信力”这一概念，更是我们提出的金点子中的金点子。

\- 2020 is destined to be an extraordinary year.

\- Despite external and internal challenges, we didn’t back down.

\- Against all odds, we gave the owner an extremely short response, only
including four words:  **We Will Be There** !

\- So how do we understand and define our name CITIC?

I-C, to reintroduce ourselves to you with 5 plus golden ideas.

\- And we’d like to call it CITIC Power.

  

\- Let’s start with the first letter C.

  

**Contribution**

\- That is Contribution.

\- In  **Belarus** , every 4 minutes a Geely car comes off from the production
line.

\- In  **Kazakhstan** , a herdsman family uses clean new energy from the Solar
and wind power station.

\- In  **Uzbekistan** , 5000 tons of potash are mined from mountains every day
and turned into fertilizer.

\- Through these successful projects, we’ve grown into the cornerstone of
carrying out the Belt and Road Initiative, and other national policies.

\- However, we believe that we can run faster!

\- Yes, faster!

\- Which means we should make a role shift, to consider more, to be deeply
involved in the policy-making procedures, to actively contribute wisdom.

\- To this end, we highly suggest establishing  **a normalized team, which is
responsible for the policy research work** , and collaborate with government,
national policy banks and industry associations.

\- Being the influencer of the national strategies, industrial policies and
other important protocols, we will not only obtain the first-hand beneficial
information, but gradually become the forerunner as well.

  

\- That’s a great idea.

\- But how do we get integrated into the target countries?

  

**Integration**

\- Nowadays, we keep extending both ends of our service chains.

\- In the Upper Reach, we positively take part in the industrial and
construction plan of the target markets, truly consider the needs of the
owners and help them choose the best deal.

\- In the Lower Reach, we’re pursuing the projects’ spillover effects.

\- The Bel-Gee project filled the gap of the country’s car production, and
created more than 1,500 jobs for the local people.

\- As our service chains grow longer, we have to better integrate them.

\- So how can we do this?

\- The Internet gives us the best answer.

\-  **Big data, block chains, IOT…** That’s what we need in our future
business.

\- Here, we propose to  **build our own data base** of all the potential
clients, partners, target markets and perhaps all aspects.

\- With this systematical proceeding process, we can easily get the whole
picture of our business, and look thoroughly into the details at the same
time.

\- We won’t omit a single chance!

  

\- That’s fabulous!

\- As a platform company, is technology crucial to us?

  

**Technology**

\- YES, definitely!

\- Technology allows us to avoid fierce competition in the Red Sea.

\- Cement plants, potash fertilizer plant, soda ash plant, asphalt plant…

\- In fields of chemical industry, agriculture, infrastructure…

\- For years, we’ve succeeded in carrying out many Star Projects on the basis
of unique technologies.

\- From scratch, we’ve overcome hundreds of technical difficulties and won
dozens of patents.

  

\- While in practice, our technologies have been limited due to the lack of
discourse power abroad.

\- In order to bridge the gap, we need to figure out  **t** **he unique techs
widely applied in China, absorb them and adapt to the target markets** .

\- Well, this requires us to keep digging and acquire more discourse power.

  

\- Despite the development of technology, what else can we do?

  

**Innovation**

\- Just give me a second.

\- People say, it’s the winner-take-all era.

\- The competition is becoming much fiercer.

\- So how can we do better and stand out?

\- The answer is innovation.

\- Innovation is the driving force for us to surpass and go forward.

\- We need innovative thinking to find new partners, new opportunities, and
new possibilities.

\- Facing the physical barriers, brought by Covid-19, localization has become
an urgent task for us.

\- We are now making positive attempts on the Belarusian Slavkaliy project,
more local engineers and teams are involved in the project management work and
design work.

\- By now, most of our project financing is limited within domestic banks and
SINOSURE; and they need sovereign or quasi sovereign guarantee for commercial
loans, which now presents some limitations.

\- So we suggest to introduce  **foreign financial institutions for the
project financing** .

\- To make the long story short, we think that project localization and
internationalization are really worth trying and extending to other projects.

  

\- That seems a lot of work to do!

  

**Coordination**

\- Don’t worry!

\- CITIC Construction is not John Rambo, but a part of the Joint Fleet.

\- On the bottom layer, every CITICer is a single component of the Joint
Fleet.

\- While with the sparks collided among us, the Fleet of Construction starts
to sail.

\- On the mid layer, collaborative strategy with other brother companies, has
always been a unique competitive advantage to us, which we should make full
use of it.

\- On the top layer, new partners, new thinking, and new mode of cooperation
are what we’re seeking to make the Joint Fleet stronger.

  

\- On each layer, no matter horizontal or vertical, cohesive force is the
perpetual motion for the upgraded Joint Fleet.

\- So it is indispensable to  **cultivate cohesiveness among us** .

\- Just like this the Youth English Contest, interactive activities allow us
to know each other, to talk, to create, to crash with sparks of Golden Ideas.

\- Please bear this in mind: Companies don’t have ideas, only people do!

CITIC Power!

Actually, at the beginning, we did say that we’re providing you with more than

CITIC Power is an organic whole.  It’s like a person, like a body.  Like

And today we’d like to present CITIC Power to you, because as President Xi
