Hi everyone, my name is Zhang Dingxin from Jinan project department. The topic
I gonna share today is the Urban Partner of Jinan Project and Government.

Jinan is a city with a rich cultural heritage. The culture of a city is the
power source of its development. One livable city not only has a picturesque
landscape but also has urban city facilities.

The Jinan International Ecological Port of the Yellow River Strategy project
aims to build a livable city with mutiple functions. As the pictures shows
here. It consists of 14 sub-projects and is scheduled to complete in 5 years,
with an investment of 9 billion yuan.

President Xi proposed at the 20th National Congress of CPC, "  We must uphold
and act on the principle that lucid waters and lush mountains are invaluable
assets, and we must remember to maintain harmony between humanity and nature
when planning our development  ." To actively respond to this proposal and the
call of “  promoting green and high-quality development  ", Jinan government
and CITIC Constraction jointly initiate the Airport Ecological Regeneration
Water Plant project. It is the first one out of the 14 sub-projects and is
designed to be a landmark.

The project, with an investment of 1 billion yuan,includes the electronic
integrated sewage treatment plant, municipal sewage treatment plant and
related supporting facilities. The project will achieve a sewage treatment
capacity of 47,500 tons per day, and it’s expected to reduce the 7,000 tons of
COD and 5,000 tons of TDS per year for Xiaoqing River, which contributes to
the organic integration of industrial development and ecological protection in
the airport economic zone.

The construction of the project began in September 2021. By October 2022, the
installation and the performance test of all process equipments of phase 1
have been completed, and the water has been officially supplied. To meet the
needs of the industrial development,  we shortened the construction period
from two years to one year  . You may ask, how to make it happen? This is
because Jinan Government and the CITIC Construction jointly initiate the
Airport Ecological Regeneration Water Plant,and we achieved three agreements
of the Urban Partnership.

Firstly,  Agreement  on The Basic Vision of Project Cooperation.

In partnership, we help the construction and development of the Airport
Ecological Regeneration Water Plant and the area to  achieve a high-quality
development process  .

Secondly,  Consistency  between Jinan Project Department’s Objectives and the
Needs of Urban Development.

Consider on the developing of the government and the need of the Jinan
project, we provide the  high-tech supporting to the Jinan government and the
project.

Thirdly,  Coordination  between CITIC Construction's  Corporate
Responsibility and The Government's Ideology of Development

The most important thing is to improve the competitiveness of the
semiconductor industry in the Jinan High-tech zone and help them to  reduce
the performance, coordination, and the maintenance cost  .

In the following sub-project construction, Jinan Project Department will
continue to demonstrate the responsibility of the central enterprises and
polish the CITIC brand with glorious achievements.
