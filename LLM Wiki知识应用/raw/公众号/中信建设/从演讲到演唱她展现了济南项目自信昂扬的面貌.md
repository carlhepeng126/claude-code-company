When I first saw the topic of this speech, I felt that although they seem to
be commonplace, they were abstract and I did not know where to start.

  

At this time, when the CPC was organizing the 20th National People's Congress,
a familiar name was mentioned in the news report- Su Mingjuan. Maybe the name
rings a bell, but you must recognize a picture of her in her childhood.
Apparently she was well-known, given a nickname “  **big eyes eager for
schooling** ”.

  

Now, turning 40, she is no longer a frail girl longing for help but she can
offer a helping hand. Since 2002, when she was admitted to the University, she
began to  **pay back to the society** . Each semester, she would donate all
the school grant to needy students. After graduation, Su Mingjuan joined ICBC
Anhui Branch and she helped launch a student aid fund in 2018. By 2020, she
has raised about  **4.9 million RMB funding the construction of five Hope
Primary Schools** . This year, she been selected as a deputy member to
participate in the 20th National Congress of the Party.

  

Her life experience is an embodiment of the Chinese young generations striving
for the nation, which corresponds with the notion advocated during the 20th
NPC that "  **a community of social governance in which everyone fulfills
their responsibilities and shares in the benefits** .”

  

At present, the Jinan Project Company of CITIC Construction has been
established for a year, and all the staff members have always been making full
commitment, performing their job responsibilities under the guideline of the
enterprise spirit that "  **When the way of virtue and justice prevailed, the
whole world was one community; dare to be the first and pursue excellence** “.
Only a year’s time, in order to solve the urgent need for sewage treatment in
industrial zone, the airport ecological regeneration water plant has been
successfully put into trial operation, with industrial and domestic waste
water being processed in sewage treatment plant. Meanwhile all raods projects
have also been stepped up. In general, the project company exceeded its
performance indicators.

  

At the most critical moment during pandemic in Jinan in May this year, the
government prohibits people from commuting, in order to ensure the process of
the construction project, all employees spontaneous offered to live on the
construction site,  **sticking to our posts in the frontline** and living up
to our work commitments as well as our duties. At the same time, a number of
people were assigned to  **help the government on guard** . When asked to
share great qualities, most of them appeared very humble and honest that they
had never considered their acts as  **admirable or remarkable** . They believe
they have just performed the responsibilities assigned on them. There is no
reason to shun from it as a member of the company. These touching incidents
winning the approval of local authorities.

  

Being a newcomer, from ignorance to proficiency in work; from confusion to the
present, i may well still feel confused about those jargons, but I feel more
and more responsible as a member of the project company; As a member of CITIC
Construction, I have been helped and promoted, and I no longer feel perplexed
about the path I am heading for, to realize my calling.
