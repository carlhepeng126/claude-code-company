近年来，全球化已成为中国企业发展的关键环节之一，尤其是在后疫情时代，为了增强自身的生存能力，越来越多的中国企业去往海外探索出路，“走出去”成为许多企业发展战略中的必要一环。
** 如何加快企业出海进程，提高企业在海外市场的竞争力？企业出海的痛点和难题又在何处？  **
以下小耐姐精心摘录的有关中国企业全球化扩展现状的文章，相信会对各位有所启发：  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG55QtREEUUhwZUp6GfgsfDuTmqgR5KibSJ6LWQRyw8eWsMRpuMQLZJwKRDKpgq5XcvPUhqqhzECAZQQ/640?wx_fmt=png)

  
Despite external uncertainties, Chinese enterprises are actively expanding
their overseas footprint, with investments mainly focusing on advanced
manufacturing and technology, according to a report from global consultancy
Accenture.
全球咨询公司埃森哲(Accenture)的一份报告显示，尽管外部存在不确定性，中国企业仍在积极扩大海外足迹，其投资主要集中在先进制造业和技术领域。  
Going global is a key way for Chinese enterprises to build long-term
competitiveness,  said Yue Bin, operations lead for Accenture Greater China,
adding that the main driver of expanding activity around the globe is
gradually shifting from state-owned to private enterprises.  埃森哲大中华区运营总监岳彬表示，
“出海”是中国企业建立长期竞争力的一个重要途径。  他补充说，全球拓展的主要驱动力正逐渐从国有企业转向私营企业。  
The report showed that 92 percent of enterprises interviewed said
globalization is a necessary step for them to maintain fast growth and 55
percent said they expect to optimize resource allocation and build up supply
chain resilience through overseas expansion, while 49 percent aim to boost
their innovative capacity through interaction with other markets.
该报告显示，92%的受访企业表示，全球化是他们保持快速增长的必要步骤，55%的企业表示，他们希望通过海外扩张优化资源配置、建立供应链韧性，而49%的企业旨在通过与其他市场的互动来提高创新能力。  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/2iaIDwqRtG56LKK9wwggRZRsjUZaxQSPa2ykDb1Hzzt1xc16oPLROo978noggChWvdIfU69POadfyrJuA0WokIQ/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)  
Chinese enterprises are confident in their internationalization push despite
rising geopolitical tensions and continued COVID-19-related disruptions. The
report noted that 95 percent of the surveyed Chinese enterprises believed that
their overseas businesses would see a compound growth of more than 5 percent
in the next three years, and 52 percent of the enterprises anticipated that of
over 20 percent.
尽管地缘政治加剧紧张的局势，新冠肺炎相关干扰持续，但中国企业对其国际化进程充满信心。该报告指出，95%的受访中国企业认为，未来三年他们的海外业务复合增长率将超过5%，52%的企业预计超过20%。  
The report used a four-tier category to rank the level of internationalization
displayed by Chinese companies based on overseas revenue proportions — an
initial stage, growth stage, established stage and fully globalized stage.
该报告根据海外收入比例，对中国企业的国际化水平分为了四类——起步期、开拓期、腾飞期和全球化阶段。  
Given the country's vast domestic market, 32 percent of Chinese enterprises
are still in the initial stage of globalization, with their overseas revenue
accounting for less than 10 percent of the total. However, there were a
significant number of enterprises in the established stage (26 percent), whose
overseas revenue accounts for 20 to 50 percent of the total.
鉴于中国巨大的国内市场，32%的中国企业仍处于全球化的起步期，其海外收入占总收入的比例不到10%。然而，有相当数量的企业处于腾飞期（26%），其海外收入占总收入的20%至50%。  
"We found that energy companies are inclined to choose South Africa and
Brazil, while internet enterprises prefer to expand their presence in
Southeast Asia," Yue said, noting that services and trade companies tend to
prefer the European market.
“我们发现，能源公司倾向于选择南非和巴西，而互联网企业更喜欢扩大在东南亚的业务，”岳彬说道，并指出服务和贸易公司往往更喜欢欧洲市场。  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/2iaIDwqRtG56LKK9wwggRZRsjUZaxQSPaGw8SdxLZRayDrN7EkUlsNAEia3ReBZpnHYtIZTXEFTADvJDW7O08Ylw/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)  
However, there are some obstacles and challenges that hinder the development
of Chinese enterprises in the varied and sometimes fickle political and
economic landscape of overseas markets.  Yue pointed out issues like risk
management, regulatory compliance, cultural differences, inefficient
operations and slow decision-making.  然而，在海外市场多变的政治和经济环境中，有一些障碍和挑战阻碍着中国企业的发展。
岳彬指出了风险管理、法规遵循、文化差异、运营效率低下和决策迟缓等问题。  
He suggested that enterprises build tailor-made operational models suitable
for globalization, establish reliable, safe and regulation-compliant digital
infrastructure, and leverage cutting-edge technologies to empower global
operation.  他建议企业建立适合全球化的量身定制的运营模式，建立可靠、安全、合规的数字基础设施，利用前沿技术赋能全球运营。  

不难看出，中国企业出海的机遇和前景，毋庸置疑是相当可观的。但相对的，语言和文化差异也为企业的全球化发展带来了非常大的挑战。站在全球化的角度，培养员工的跨文化沟通能力可以说是企业进军海外市场的首要条件。
