2021的意义非同寻常——疫情使得全球经济政治格局发生变化，在这个大环境背景下，企业要加强自身软实力，人才培养是重中之重。当就业者在专业技能和通用技能（如语言能力、沟通能力和其他软技能）方面做好准备时，2021将成为职场人的进阶之年！  
语言能力的提升离不开听、说、读、写。传统英语教育更多是在读、写方面发力，而忽视了听、说等语言实际运用的能力。
有没有一种英语学习方法，可以实实在在提升听说能力， **让我们听听英耐的学员Jack如何说吧：**  
*以下内容为在英耐学习的学员亲自撰写，为了保证学员反馈真实性，仅隐去本人和老师姓名，不做语法、句型等内容修改。    

I have studied English in Impact for half a year until now. I have learned a
lot of knowledge from the teacher and the APP. Thank you for the help of my
teacher Sean over the past six months. I think I've really made progress in
listening and speaking  . I  '  d like to make a summary of what I  '  ve
learned here.

  

1\.  The 5 step study method  is very helpful. Here is how I understand it.

Getting the gist  —Know the mean idea.

Intensive Listening&understanding  —repeat to listen, understand every
sentence.

Speaking&Imitating  —speak every sentence loudly.

Recording&comparing  —improve pronunciation.

Summarizing&personalizing  —learn the English words and use them into the
normal life.

  

2.If you want to improve your  spoken English  quickly, you must overcome your
fear of speaking English. In the class, I can open my mouth and chat with my
teacher Seanna. She always encourages and guides me to speak English around
the topic.

  

3.If you want to improve your English, you need to expose yourself to the
English environment. In the Wechat group, I always complete the learning task
every week. In my free time, I usually watch some English movies and TV plays.

In future, I will keep learning English, insisting on listening, speaking,
reading and writing.

  

_——英耐学员Jack_

  
磨好耳朵是说好口语的第一步。学语言不是纸上谈兵，一味对着文章埋头苦读是不行的。多说才能将积累的知识运用出来，让自己融入各种情景中去交流，真正将英语日常化。
英耐五步学习法： **泛听——精听——跟读模仿——回听对比——总结活化** ，符合语言学习规律，实实在在助力英语提升！
