** 一句话告别NG  **

  

  

1周1次的例会上，部门同事需要汇报本周的工作内容及下周的工作计划。  **"我下周要出差。"** 用英语来说就是：  ** I'm going on a
business trip next week.  **  

**不NG必备词**

on business  出差  on the road  在旅途中，到处奔波  be out of  离开  business trip  公务出差
stressful  有压力的  arrangement  布置，安排  souvenir  纪念品  reimburse  补偿，报销  schedule
安排  travel expense  旅行费用  compensate  补偿  

**不NG必备句**

**出差准备** 要跟老板出差了，别太紧张哦！出差前的准备要做好。  ● There are several things that haven't
been decided yet before the trip.  出差前还有一些事情没决定好。  ● Do you have any questions
about the business trip?  你对出差还有什么疑问吗？  ● How do I get compensated for the
money I spent on my business trip?  我怎么报销我出差的费用？  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG55Nlp2nPeA35WXzicnXrJCHZawegbibygHIc9tic1Snksicyt6uxBpFUibA1tyvDibBN0iaFjVLH44c2kyjA/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)
**  
** **谈论出差** 对于上班族来说，出差是常有的事，和朋友们谈论出差时都会说点啥呢？  ● I was in Osaka on business.
我去大阪出差了。  ● I'll be on the road most of next week.  下周大部分时间我都会出差。  ● Sorry,
he'll be out of town for a while.  对不起，他要出差一段时间。  ● Nothing is more stressful
than going on a business trip with our boss.  和我们老板一起出差让人非常有压力。  ● You don't
know how tight the schedule is for this business trip.  你不知道这次出差行程安排得有多紧张。  
**出差事宜** 出差可没有那么简单，对于出差前、出差中和出差后的工作和生活都有做到心里有数哦！  ● The first thing you will
do is to look for a comfortable hotel.  你首先要做的就是找一个舒适的旅馆。  ● The company is
sometimes very cheap with the travel expenses.  公司在出差的费用上有时非常小气。  ● Every time
l'm back, I have to write a detailed report of the business trip.
每次出差回来，我都要写一份详细的出差报告。  
**出差参加的各类研讨会** marketing seminar  市场推销研讨会  problem seminar  问题讨论会  research
seminar  科研讨论会  

**不NG实景对话**

**● I'm going on a business trip next week.** **我下周要出差。** A: I'm going on a
business trip next week.  我下周要出差。  B: Another business trip? You just came
back last Sunday.  又出差？你上周日刚回来啊。  
** 词组点睛：business trip 公司事务出差  ** **  
**
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG55Nlp2nPeA35WXzicnXrJCHZq6MWEcAv7MWgibtUSPABVbK2wb4tMU68hGbrpa0M18Cnl4KaIFTmmLg/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)
**  
** **● Have you fixed with the specific trip schedule?** **你整理好出差时间计划表了吗？** A:
Have you fixed with the specific trip schedule?  你整理好出差时间计划表了吗？  B: Yes. Do
you need to check it right now?  是的，您需要现在看吗？  
** 单词点睛：schedule 时间表  **  
**● How long are you going to be out this time?** **你要去多久？** A: How long are
you going to be out this time?  你要去多久？  B: It depends.  看情况。  
** 词组点睛：be going to do sth．打算做某事  **  
**● l'd like you to make the necessary arrangements for me.**
**我想让你帮我做必要的活动安排计划。** A: What can I do for you?  我能为您做什么？  B: l'd like you to
make the necessary arrangements for me.  我想让你帮我做必要的活动安排计划。  
**● Will the company reimburse everything?** **公司会报销一切费用吗？** A: Do you have
any question about the business trip?  你对出差还有什么疑问吗？  B: Will the company
reimburse everything?  公司会报销一切费用吗？  
** 单词点晴：reimburse 偿还，归还  ** ** 词组点晴：reimburse sb. for a loss 赔偿某人的损失  ** **  
**
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG55Nlp2nPeA35WXzicnXrJCHZ4mfyu01jsvwsO45C5Biaiab2DtS70R02dpNiaXyhIjJNqByUiba00l5Eng/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)
**  
** **● He always gets some souvenirs for other colleagues when he's back from
a business trip.** **他每次出差都会给同事们带回来一些纪念品。** A: Everybody loves Jack. Why?
每个人都喜欢杰克，为什么？  B: He always gets some souvenirs for other colleagues when he's
backfrom a business trip.  他每次出差都会给同事们带回来一些纪念品。  
** 单词点睛：souvenir 纪念品  **
