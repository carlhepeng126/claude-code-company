几乎所有的岗位，在日常工作中都需要做数据汇报，从数据中提出有效信息并总结最终的结论。因此，打工人通常会接触到各种不同类型的图表，能否清晰地分析图表数据，也是领导考量你的工作能力的维度之一
。  **那你知道该如何用英语针对不同的图表，做出令领导一目了然的汇报吗？**
如果你觉得汇报数据是你工作中的短板，那这篇文章你可得赶紧收藏，小耐姐汇总了几类比较常见的表格的描述方法，必须能在工作中帮到你！

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_gif/2iaIDwqRtG57bVlr7ng5ttFGu5sVretNvQoarv2WDJYicfJCHEhXwtva2KOCWiaLqhdcoQUKqsFG5T8EeUe8prqPg/640?wx_fmt=gif)

  

**line graph 线形图**

横轴  **horizontal axis** , 纵轴  **vertical axis**  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG55eJm6DOD6ncP3xPFuHtzFucyGfKiaCdgwvD6Z1s0lJoObgJb2yCOcECZ6UqREVDzXZfXVSGQwNZqw/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)  
1\. This is a curve graph which describes the trend of...  这个曲线图描述了...的趋势  
2\. As can be seen from the graph, the two curves show the fluctuation of...
如图所示，两条曲线描述了...的波动情况。  
3\. The graph provides some interesting data regarding...  该图为我们提供了有关...有趣数据  
4\. As can be seen from the diagram, great changes have taken place in...
从图中可以看出，...发生了巨大变化。  
  

**pie chart 饼状图**

饼状图内部用于表示所占分量，那一块一块叫做“  **segment** ”  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG55eJm6DOD6ncP3xPFuHtzFuiaf0yVLuVsqdBkJmflSjiaad3mNItG9NicmM4Z7APggicbomtwcvsIOKZA/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)  
1\. 占据：take up, occupy, account for, take over  The pie graph depicts
(that).... 该饼状图揭示了...  
2\. The percentage of...stayed the same between...and...  至...期间...的比率维持不变。  
3\. The percentage remained steady at...  比率维持在...  
4\. The percentage of...is slightly larger/smaller than that of...
...的比例比...的比例略高(低)。  
5\. The pie chart reveals how...  这个饼状图向我们揭示了如何...  
6\. The graph, presented in a pie chart, shows the general trend in...
该图以圆形图形式描述了...总的趋势。  
  

** bar chart 柱状图  **  

每一个矩形就叫一个  **bar**  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG55eJm6DOD6ncP3xPFuHtzFuFI0k84ibwibC1pYurG52bI94suKibg6V19qU318MVHEwQIklC0xK3g7zg/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)  
1\. The bar chart illustrates that...  该柱状图展示了...  
2\. This is a column chart showing...  这是个柱型图，描述了...  
3\. As is shown/demonstrated/exhibited in the diagram/graph/chart/table...
如图所示...  
  

**table 表**

表的“行”是“  **row** ”，“列”是“  ** column  ** ”  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG55eJm6DOD6ncP3xPFuHtzFuYeTXes6I6J9VvGceuDiaxSXp31QtAHOeMkczP1daB5Aa8kGDWohpicBQ/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)  
1\. This table shows the changing proportion of a & b from...to...
该表格描述了...年到...年间a与b的比例关系。  
2\. From the table/chart/diagram/figure, we can see clearly that...or it is
clear/apparent from the chart that...  从图表我们可以很清楚(明显)看到...  
3\. According to the chart/figures...  根据这些表(数字)...  
4\. As is shown in the table...  如表格所示...  
5\. The figures/statistics show(that)...  数据(字)表明...  
  

** describing change 描述变化  **  

1\. 向上的趋势 (  ** upward movement  ** )  
－ to increase/rise/go up  － to grow/expand  － to rocket/boom/soar  e.g. Our
sales increased.  e.g. we expanded the market.  e.g. we raised our prices.
(注意：raise是及物动词)  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG55eJm6DOD6ncP3xPFuHtzFuxpjgibrkpNcIlCQjYRjTay4zgCws9pzibMiaibD1Bcoy3iatLTRHsoIAAOQ/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)  
2\. 向下的趋势 (  ** downward movement  ** )  \- to decrease/fall/drop/decline/go
down  \- to slump/collapse/plummet  
3\. 不再变动 (  ** an end to movement  ** )  － to level off  
4\. 无变化 (  ** no change  ** )  － to remain constant/stable  － to stay the same
－ to maintain/hold/keep  
  

**degree of change 变化的程度**

** dramatically/considerably/significantly/  **

** moderately/slightly  **

e.g. profits rose slightly. 利润缓慢上升

  
  

** speed of change 变化速度  **  

** rapidly/quickly/suddenly/gradually/steadily/  ** ** slowly  ** e.g. sales
went up rapidly. 销售迅速增长
