**开场语**

** 1\. Greeting (问候):  ** ·  Dear [Name),  ·  Hello [Name),  ·  Hi [Name),  
** 2\. 引言部分(Introduction):  ** ·  I hope this email finds you well.  ·  I am
writing to you regarding...  ·  Thank you for your prompt reply.  ·  I am
reaching out to you in connection with...  
  

**主体部分**

**1.表达意图 (Stating the Purpose):** ·  I am writing to inquire about...  ·  I
would like to inform you that...  ·  We are interested in...  ·  I am pleased
to inform you that...  **  
** **2.详细信息(Providing Details):** ·  Attached, you will find...  ·  Please
find the details below...  ·  I would like to draw your attention to...  ·
Allow me to explain in more detail...  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG56jvOaJibaAwrDA4omT9pOmia0lDtmqSODJkneBDuPG4ibbYRk9tPspnnYmicCgjgibGHKr2E1dCWga35g/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)
**  
** **2\. 请求或建议(Request or Suggestion):** ·  Could you please...  ·  I kindly
request that...  ·  I suggest that we...  ·  It would be great if you could...  
**3\. 提供帮助(Offering Assistance):** ·  If you need any further assistance, feel
free to contact me.  ·  Please do not hesitate to reach out if you require any
additional information.  ·  I am here to help with any questions you may have.
·  Let me know if there's anything else I can assist you with.  
  

**结尾部分**

**1\. 总结(Summarizing):** ·  To sum up...  ·  In conclusion...  ·  Thank you
for your attention to this matter.  ·  I appreciate your time and
consideration.  
**2\. 结束语(Closing):** ·  Best Regards,  ·  Sincerely  ,  ·  Kind Regards  ,  ·
Yours Faithfully,
