**场景二** **Making enquiries**

** ·  ** ** Questions are about the subject of a sentence:  **

Who takes you to shcool?

What annoys you the most?

When is it convenient?

** 当问题是关于句中主语的时候我们不需要加助动词do,does,did.  **

  

** **·** Questions are about the object of a sentence：  **

Where do you want to meet?

Who did you invite to the meeting?

Why couldn't you go?

** 当问题是关于句中宾语的时候我们记住要加助动词do,does,have,can.  **
