You should try to let yourself shine first, so that the right person can walk
to you in the light.

先努力让自己发光，对的人才能迎着光而来。

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/2iaIDwqRtG56KCcHG2Zw8exDmibl8YZdxTFdcAaXQ8e60TM4uL28PibZdoOtPD1NCy2le2ssdNhIfMSFlRklIyvzw/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

What we really want is not easy to get. We must spare no effort in all the
gains.

真正想要的东西，不只是踮踮脚尖那么简单，所有的收获，一定要全力以赴，奋不顾身。

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG56KCcHG2Zw8exDmibl8YZdxTaqlOekuIibwxCKe2tWO2mia4OMnKXbx0SdXkglcXRFWkOsrQibIeuuX8Q/640?wx_fmt=png)

  

♥

  

It is better to go ahead with a lamp than to complain about being in a dark
place.

抱怨身处灰暗，不如提灯前行。

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG56KCcHG2Zw8exDmibl8YZdxT8c9sxQQ9uyQ8zluVhhWGqeAYHrMaMVYyeGXwIHcxmsDia1NJp8q2Pxg/640?wx_fmt=png)

  

♥

  

Even standing in the same place, different people will see different
landscapes through their lives.

不同的人，即使站在同一个地方，透过各自的人生，看到的风景也有所不同。

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/2iaIDwqRtG56KCcHG2Zw8exDmibl8YZdxTJazyWgichXP0Iia9ibW5OwgdRdb6Dn66KyibhXYX1ibTPXB3pEn0lOk1Fyg/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

  

♥

  

One of the signs of maturity: coming to understand people and things in life,
being transparent, rational and gentle.

一个人变得成熟的标志之一：慢慢理解生命中所遇到的人和事，通透、理智且温和。

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/2iaIDwqRtG56KCcHG2Zw8exDmibl8YZdxTtFPT1pu2HNdDQGFRuVpr7HwgGT860RVRHZRPRDOCv5AusWdXoAhvXQ/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

  

♥

  

Sometimes, it would be better to say no than say yes. and you can learn two
other skills: say no to a jerk and say yes to your sweetheart.

随口答应，还不如认真拒绝。承诺有限，精力紧张，大家可以学习另外两种技能：随口拒绝讨厌鬼，认真答应心上人。

——张嘉佳

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG56KCcHG2Zw8exDmibl8YZdxTs0ZiaBhc2xOAaFasaTicjLcA9NUu7MYScic6QJKPdVfh0dQF7dAo1vrhw/640?wx_fmt=png)

  

♥

  

I'm waiting for someone to tell me that the world is worth my tenderness. If
not, I'll try my best to live and being soft.

等着一个姗姗来迟的人告诉我，人间值得我也很温柔，如果没有，那我就好好努力生活努力变温柔。

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/2iaIDwqRtG56KCcHG2Zw8exDmibl8YZdxTCHicoZrtUs5Ikicj6PHJ34coEtOSKhlGmBGaOt1JhpdwssIZicNKqO11w/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

I used to think it's not important to earn money. Now I know that stars and
the sea need tickets to visit. Long distance traveling is expensive.

以前觉得挣钱不重要，现在オ知道星辰和大海都需要门票，诗和远方的路费都很贵。
