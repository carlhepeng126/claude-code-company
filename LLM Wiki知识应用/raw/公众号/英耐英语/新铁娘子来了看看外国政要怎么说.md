经过一场旷日持久的角逐，利兹·特拉斯 (Liz
Truss)当选为英国新首相，这将是英国历史上第三位女首相。对于特拉斯的当选，海外政要基于不同的政治立场，作出了各具特色的反应。现摘录部分典型回应，供大家学习参考。  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/2iaIDwqRtG55sfXVibMibEhPEqSKf9tMNT7QWtQqgic6bZDwdicerKqPYjex9fmjhMjVBmib2IswFmUJc3WmD8mjk2bA/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)  
  
**欧盟委员会主席乌尔苏拉·冯·德莱恩(Ursulavon der Leyen)**  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG55sfXVibMibEhPEqSKf9tMNT7mGE8MxhsIPpjHy0hHxCIsrSPEjicwcoEahKyyeQqiadZNN14DriaIHzNQ/640?wx_fmt=png)  
自英国脱欧后，由于《北爱尔兰议定书》(Northern Ireland Protocol)
和英法渔权等争议，双方关系可以“strained”来形容。保守党现正推动议会立法，计划单方面修改《北爱尔兰议定书》的部分条款以使北爱和英国其它地区的贸易更加便捷。对于这种单方违约的行为，欧盟当然是不接受的。这就是为什么冯·德莱恩会在祝福特拉斯当选的同时，不忘提醒她“in
full respect of our agreements”。

  

**词汇学习**

  

向上滑动阅览

  

**Look forward to something** ** 期待着某事或做某事，后接名词或动名词  ** **·** I'm really
looking forward to my holiday.  **·** She was looking forward to seeing the
grandchildren again.  
**With respect to/in respect of** **就…而言，关于…** ** ·  ** Parents often have
little choice with respect to the way their child is medically treated.  ** ·
** Where Dr Shapland feels the system is not working most effectively is in
respect of professional training.

  

  

**欧盟委员会副主席兼首席英国脱欧谈判代表马罗斯·塞夫科维奇(MarosSefcovic)**  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG55sfXVibMibEhPEqSKf9tMNT7ia4QXkOCZ8NMQmPsWaEycQdJBbZiamlwu2E1iaQ6fyzicopvCLJicgJFd5g/640?wx_fmt=png)  
这位副主席也紧跟冯主席的步伐，在表示期待着与特拉斯合作的同时，也旁敲侧击地说要“我们要守约(in full respect of our
agreements)”。

  

**词汇学习**

  

向上滑动阅览

  

**Of importance**  
** 等同于important,表示重要的意思  ** ** 同样的用法还有：of great significance=significant  **
**·** The discovery of the new drug is of great significance for/to people
suffering from heart problems.  
** Intensively  ** ** 强烈地，强度很大  ** ** ·  ** She is studying intensively,
working toward her degree.

  

**Constructively** **有建设性地** ** ·  ** We ought to deal constructively with the
problems.  
** Interlocutor  ** ** 对话者，代表  ** ** ·  ** Abraham was able to act as
interpreter and interlocutor for our group.  
  
**法国总统埃马纽埃尔·马克龙 (Emmanuel Macron)** **  
** "I say welcome to Liz Truss. I express all of France's congratulations,and
we are ready to work together, as one would say, as allies and friends."  
在8月份底的竞选辩论会上,当被问道“马克龙是敌还是友(if Macron was a friend or foe)”时，特斯拉的回复是"The jury
is out. If I become prime minister, I will judge him on deeds,not words."
相比起来，马克龙的回复就显得更专业和友善。

their concerns.  
** The jury is (still) out  ** ** 表示“尚未定论”的意思  ** ** ·  ** The jury is still
out on whether the new healthcare system will work.

  

  

**美国总统约瑟夫·拜登(Joe Biden)**  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG55sfXVibMibEhPEqSKf9tMNT7WfkO0TjFsgqdlbIqRHE0E05qdTYPhPRzMD4JElHfrZibro4jp0AAK8A/640?wx_fmt=png)  
循惯例，拜登强在其祝福推文上强调了英美之间的特殊关系(special relationship)。这个术语最早由因英前首相温斯顿·丘吉尔 (Winston
Churchill)在1946年的铁幕演说(Iron
Curtain)中提出，主要是用来描述英美之间的特殊关系。这种关系建立于共同的语言、历史和价值观。但也有人嘲讽英美之间是“夫唱妇随”的关系。

  

**词汇学习**

  

向上滑动阅览

  

**Deepen** **加深、加固、深化和日趋严重的意思** **·** One way of preventing further flooding
would be to deepen the river bed.  Over the years, her love for him deepened.
It certainly helped to deepen my understanding of the situation.

  

**Cooperation** **合作** ** ·  ** Without the cooperation of local residents,
this movie could not have been made.  
**Aggression** **侵犯、侵略** ** ·  ** To launch a pre-emptive strike would be an
act of aggression against that country.  ** ·  ** When a cat wags its tail,
this is usually a sign of aggression.

  

  

**德国总理奥拉夫·朔尔茨 (OLAFSCHOLZ)**  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG55sfXVibMibEhPEqSKf9tMNT7R7sH7NqZCp4qfhnGpG9MC4AWTukMnTrMtXT3q6nUQ91VmwFKyy9vZA/640?wx_fmt=png)  
朔尔茨保持他一贯不温不火的作风，对特拉斯的当选表示祝贺并期待着与她的合作。这让我想起了每次给客户的客套回复"It was so nice too meet
you, and I look forward to working with you soon."  
  
**爱尔兰总理米歇尔·马丁(Micheal Martin)**  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG55sfXVibMibEhPEqSKf9tMNT71kbodPOfECbW6QX7VibYovNyflJXl3abapza68OmItMHgQEyKu7LibHQ/640?wx_fmt=png)  
当1949年爱尔兰彻底从英国独立出来时，北部六郡（也就是今天的北爱）选择继续留在英国。爱尔兰是欧盟成员国，如果英国单方修改《北爱尔兰议定书》的话，影响最大的将是爱尔兰。马丁总理的回复还是比较正面的，期待着与特拉斯解决共同面临的问题(important
issues we face together,both bilaterally and
globally)。不过，从语序上，我们能看出他应该是在强调"bilaterally",也即北爱问题。  
  
**克里姆林宫发言人德米特里·佩斯科夫(Dmitry Peskov)**  
"I wouldn't like to say that things can change for the worse, because it's
hard to imagine anything worse. But unfortunately, this cannot be ruled out,
given that the contenders for the post of British prime minister competed with
each other in anti-Russian rhetoric, in threats to take further steps against
our country, and so on.Therefore, I don’t think that we can hope for anything
positive."  由于俄乌冲突，俄罗斯和欧洲特别是英国的关系比较紧张。今年2月中旬，俄外长拉夫罗夫(Sergei Lavrov)
在会见特拉斯后做了这样的评价"like the conversation with a deaf person… who is here, but does
not hear"。

  

**词汇学习**

  

向上滑动阅览

  

**Rule something or someone out**  
**把...的可能性排除在外，认为某人或某事不合适** ** ·  ** The management seems to have ruled him
out as a candidate for promotion.  
** ·  ** The CEO said she could not rule out further job cuts.

  

** Given that  ** ** 考虑到，鉴于  ** ** ·  ** Usually,I am sensible with money, as
I have to be, given that I don't earn that much.  ** ·  ** It was surprising
the government was re-elected, given that they had raised taxes so much.

昨天也在推文上再次感谢离任首相约翰逊对乌的一贯支持。

**词汇学习**

  

向上滑动阅览

  

**Grateful**  
**感谢，感激** **·** I'm so grateful (to you) for all that you've done.  ** ·  **
I'm just grateful that I'm not still working for him.  
** Multifaceted  ** ** 多方，多面的意思  ** ** ·  ** It's a multifaceted business,
offering a range of services.  
** Tangible  ** ** 真实的，可被感知的  ** ** ·  ** We need tangible evidence if we're
going to take legal action.  ** ·  ** Other tangible benefits include an
increase in salary and shorter working hours.  
** Sovereignty  ** ** 主权  ** ** ·  ** Talks are being held about who should
have sovereignty over the island.

  

** Independence  ** ** 独立  ** ** ·  ** Mexico gained its independence from
Spain in 1821.  
** Inviolability  ** ** 不可侵犯、不可冒犯  ** **·** Both sides agreed to respect the
inviolability of their joint border.

  

** Sum(something/someone) up  ** ** 总结、概括  ** ** ·  ** The best way of summing
up the situation in our office is to say that it is "absolute chaos".  ** ·
** I'd just like to sum up by saying that it's been a tremendous pleasure to
work with you.

  

** On behalf of someone  ** ** 代表  ** ** ·  ** I’d like to say on behalf of
the whole group that we wish you well in your new job.

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG55sfXVibMibEhPEqSKf9tMNT7rscyaaf1MaCffpv9R5yqSIQ0H8ypicWkf1Lnj2DiakTxKT2vdzW6O1sg/640?wx_fmt=png)

**词汇学习**

  

向上滑动阅览

  

**Heartfelt** **诚挚的，由衷的** **·** He hugged her awkwardly as he said goodbye,
but his good wishes were heartfelt.  
** Endeavors  ** ** 努力，奋斗；可作名词和动词使用  ** ** ·  ** Engineers are endeavoring to
locate the source of the problem.  **·** In spite of our best endeavors, it
has proven impossible to contact her.

  

  

任何语言的学习都要进行大量的阅读来打基础，英语学习也不例外。课本材料缺乏时效性，可利用累积的知识点不多，实时的英语报刊是非常好的学习资料。读外刊要有选择、讲效率，本科以上及职场人士推荐阅读《经济学人The
Economist》。经济学人以恢宏精致的内容和辛辣老到的文笔著称，用最凝练的语言讲述政治，商业和社会新闻。
