**Introduction 导入**

Good morning/afternoon (everyone) (ladies and gentlemen).  

(女士们、先生们)早上/下午(大家)好。

  

It's a pleasure to welcome (the President) here.

很高兴欢迎(总裁)。

  

I  '  m… (the Director of…)

我是……(主管……)

  

By the end of the talk/presentation/session, you  '  ll know how to… /…you
will have learned about… /

在演讲/演示/会议结束时，您会知道如何....../您会了解到......

  

  

**Introduce the topic 介绍主题**

I plan to say a few words about…  

我打算说几句关于……

  

I  '  m going to talk about…

我要讲的是……

  

The subject of my talk is…

我演讲的主题是……

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG56oToH5w5pLxw3R8I6MnHIN6XNm42eIuSlBGiaOY6f1NvYKoSxxPL2l0iaAEuqTMrnhe6ykeX3ysK9A/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)

  

Today we are here to give a presentation on...

今天我们要做一个关于...的演示

  

  

**Outline 大纲**

My talk will be in (three parts).  

我的演讲将分为(三部分)。  
In the first part…  在第一部分……  
Then in the second part…  然后在第二部分……  
Finally, I  '  ll go on to talk about…  最后，我将继续谈论……  
  

**Duration 持续时间**

During the next ten minutes. I shall...  
在接下来十分钟，我会……  
My presentation will last for about ten minutes...  我的演示大概有十分钟……  
I won  '  t take up more than ten minutes of your time...  我的演示不会超过十分钟……  
I know that time is short, so I intend to keep this brief.  我知道时间不多了，所以我会简短说明。  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG56oToH5w5pLxw3R8I6MnHINzibpZfvezvA1IRiaaGfh504Scmrta3TyXQZDVkfuMMzicy779V8bbdl0w/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)  
I have a lot to cram in to the next ten minutes, so I  '  d better make a
start.  在接下来十分钟我要说的很多，所以我现在进入正题.  
  

**Questions 提问**

Please interrupt if you have any questions.  
如果您有任何问题，请打断我。  
After my talk, there will be time for a discussion and any questions.
我的演讲结束后，将会有时间进行讨论和提问。
