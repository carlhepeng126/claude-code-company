每一位企业高管都是企业出海征途中的关键舵手，因此，高管对企业出海的认知和态度，将对企业在海外的发展产生重要影响。  
今天，小耐姐要特别分享一位从A1级别起步、基础薄弱、词汇量有限的汽车行业高管学员，如何通过短短三个月的密集学习，有效提升听力口语能力，克服表达障碍，使个人沟通技能实现了质的飞跃的同时，为企业走向国际市场、打开全球视野、促进跨国合作的真实故事。  
** 以下是该学员对英耐教学的真实评价：  **  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG55QtREEUUhwZUp6GfgsfDuTmqgR5KibSJ6LWQRyw8eWsMRpuMQLZJwKRDKpgq5XcvPUhqqhzECAZQQ/640?wx_fmt=png)

  
Through the past three months of APP learning and the careful guidance of
teacher Victor, l have gained a lot, as follows:  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_jpg/2iaIDwqRtG56FUicRV2vKe8a4dUxzpgbCvC3gFsIjpGg5icePPYPtb8QOCvgUaycx9TpP9NBfdZyH09K3czwiczQMQ/640?wx_fmt=jpeg&from=appmsg)  
1\.  ** The listening level has been significantly improved, mainly due to the
APP.  ** For daily language, telephone communication, character introduction,
year-round description, and simple questions and answers, through repeated
listening, first understand the general meaning, and then carefully understand
each sentence to achieve full understanding.  
2\.  ** The level of oral English has been slightly improved.  ** On the one
hand, continuous practice and correction through the APP and then focus on the
points that are easy to make mistakes. On the other hand, in the one-on-one
communication with Mr. Victor,  ** you can take the initiative to try to
express under the guidance of the teacher  ** .  
Although the current oral ability is still very weak, I believe that through
continuous learning and practice, it will definitely improve.  
3.Through the pre-class preview of recent classes, I feel the importance of
preview, especially for students like us who have a poor foundation and little
vocabulary. On the one hand, it can provide the efficiency of the class. At
the same time, it is relatively easier and easier to understand in the
learning process. I hope l can keep it.  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_jpg/2iaIDwqRtG56FUicRV2vKe8a4dUxzpgbCvOLcMPETWiasqnW5IRkIUT6v36YOn3hJPtIVwhhcWbjW2nukBV3PmnVg/640?wx_fmt=jpeg&from=appmsg)

  
In addition to the harvest, of course, there are also many shortcomings and
regrets:  
1.l am not very motivated to take the initiative to learn. Every time I
preview in a hurry before class, and I don't take time to review after class.  
2.There is no regular learning. If you have time, you can learn it. There is
not enough pressure to learn. There are many reasons and interfaces. This is
unreasonable.  
3.For the later study, I hope  ** that I can improve my enthusiasm for active
learning. APP learning is not less than 2 times a week, and not less than 2
hours at a time.  ** At the same time, the homework assigned by the teacher
should be completed and submitted on time.  
I hope that my spoken English will be significantly improved at the end of the
year.  
Finally. I would like to thank  ** Mr. Victor for his careful teaching in
every class. Mr.Victor is very professional and patient.  ** He often takes
time to tutor after class. Although I did not participate in every time the
teachers came to Wuhu, I could feel the enthusiasm and dedication of the
teachers.  
Thank you for your hard work!  

特别感谢这位高管的真诚分享，以及一直以来默默在学员背后付出的教练老师们。  **
