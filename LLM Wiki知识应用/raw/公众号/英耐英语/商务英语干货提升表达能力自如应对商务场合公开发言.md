** 成长经历  **

** Her experience  **

  

** No one is born speaking fluently. I have been looking for opportunities to
practice myself since primary school.  ** In elementary school, I participated
in many speech contests, hosted the school's weekly flag-raising ceremony, and
hosted various parties in high school. In college, I joined the debate team
and began to participate in activities that practiced more "self-expression."  

  

** 每个人都不是天生就能流利表达的，我从小学开始就有在找寻机会锻炼自己。  **
小学我参加了很多演讲比赛，主持学校每周的升旗仪式，再到高中主持各种晚会，大学加入校辩论队，开始参与更加锻炼“自我表达”的活动。

  

** Each of these small steps laid the foundation for every speech that
followed.  ** So now I don't get stage fright when speaking to hundreds of
people.

  

** 这些微小的每一步都为以后的每次发言奠定了基础。  ** 所以现在哪怕是几百人的公开演讲也不会怯场啦。

  

** 练习方法  **

** How to practice  **

  

Many people dare not speak in front of the public, worrying about their speech
being not fluent and full of logical errors. To change these problems, it is
not enough just shouting confident slogans,  **long-term practice is needed
behind it.** I made great improvements during debate team training. Here are
some tips for practicing:

  

很多人不敢在公众面前讲话，都是担心自己的发言不流畅，逻辑混乱，而要改变这些问题绝对不是空喊自信起来的口号就能做到的，  **背后需要的是长期的练习。**
我自己成长最快的阶段也是在辩论队训练的那几年，下面我就分享一下这些练习小方法：

**  
**

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG56F6YmbnF0WGWCBqNotDrpVgU8bxMhK0zEl5FqcIFK5RXibQnsKYW4op42TyNyQYTql8H2G8SDaWBg/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)

  

** 1\. 每天读稿20分钟  **

Collect speeches or press releases and read aloud for 20 minutes every day.
**This is basic language training.**

  

搜集一些演讲稿或者新闻稿，每天靠墙站，大声朗读20分钟，  **这是最基础的语言训练。**

  

** 2\. 复述法（针对说话不连贯）  **

Select any video of a speech or debate (preferably a draft) and just listen to
it once! Write down the main points and arguments in a notebook as you listen,
and then  **try to repeat the whole passage.** It may be a little difficult at
first, but it is  **the fastest way to improve** over time, and to consciously
form the habit of logical expression.

  

随便找一些演讲或者辩论视频（立论稿最好）只听一遍！在听的过程中用小本子记下主要观点和论证思路，  **听完以后尝试把整篇文章复述出来。**
可能刚开始会有点难，但是长期坚持这绝对是  **提升最快的方法** ，也能够有意识地形成逻辑表达的习惯。

**  
**

**3\. 对镜头练习**

** Find a mirror and pretend it to be an audience, and begin to state your
point of view.  ** In the process, you can practice your eye contact and
gestures and build confidence. **** **In fact, I think you can use a camera to
record video for yourself, which is more useful than a mirror.** **** You can
also look back to find your own shortcomings in expression.

  

** 找到一面镜子，把它假象成观众，开始阐述你的观点，  ** 在这个过程中，你可以锻炼自己的眼神和动作，培养自信。
**其实我觉得大可用摄像头来代替，给自己录视频，这个比镜子更有用** ，  也可以回看找到自己表达的不足。

  

**4\. 打磨内容**

** Many reports of public speaking can be prepared in advance  ** ,  at this
time, it is particularly important to constantly polish their own speeches,
and the special part of the public speech is  **to have a clear structure** ,
so that people understand your speech logic, so the speech should also be
prepared from this point of view.

  

** 很多汇报型的公开发言都是可以提前准备的  ** ，这时候不断打磨自己的发言稿就尤其重要，并且公开演讲稿的特殊之处就是必须要有  **清晰的结构**
，让听的人了解你的发言逻辑，所以演讲稿也要从这个角度去准备。

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG56F6YmbnF0WGWCBqNotDrpVuXic1iaiccuLaFC3vZvFYRNIIWlnZ4hRQR2b7JWSiazl38BPLAkAQCUf9g/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)

  

** 5\. 模拟临场表达  **

In addition to prepared public speaking, there are many occasions in life
where you have to speak off the cuff, which requires  **you to have this kind
of training on a daily basis.** The best thing to do is to find some "debate
questions," or "civil service interview questions," or Hypothetical question
about your own job scenario and start describing them unprepared.

  

除了能提前准备的公开发言，生活中我们还会遇到很多  **需要你临场发言的场合** **，这就需要你在日常中有类似的训练。**
最好的就是去找一些“辩题”，或者“公务员面试题”，或者你针对自己的工作场景假象一些问题，不准备的情况下开始描述。

  

不管什么技巧，说到底都是锦上添花，真正让你说得有分量、有深度的，还得靠肚子里的墨水儿。尤其是在职场商务交流中，大家都见多识广，所以你更得有东西可说，有思想可表达，这才叫言之有物。  
所以啊，平时多听多看英语新闻，多积累地道的表达方式，这才是提升表达能力的硬道理。同时，也别忘了把积累到的内容多做实践。
**无论是工作汇报、会议发言还是日常交流，都要把学到的技巧用起来。**
只要肯下功夫，日积月累下，你一定能成为一个在商务场合出口成章、言之有物的英语表达高手！
