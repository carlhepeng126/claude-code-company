假如你的一生被一项一项统计出来，会怎样？  
按照平均年龄78岁来计算：  _ 睡觉的时间：28年  _ _ 工作的时间：10.5年  _ _ 追剧刷社交媒体的时间：9年  _ _ 做家务：6年  _
_ 吃吃喝喝：4年  _ _ 坐车上下班的时间：2.5年  _ ……  
结论： **留给自己的时间也只有短短9年！**  
所以，花几分钟看完这个视频，然后放下手机，去做一些有价值的事情吧！  
  
** 演讲文本及翻译  **  
When asked “what's the biggest mistake we make in life?”  当被问及：人生最大的错误是什么？  
The Buddha replied,“The biggest mistake is you think you have time.”
佛祖回答：最大的错误就是你认为你有时间。  
Time is free but it's priceless. You can't own it, but you can use it.
时间是免费的但也是无价的。你不能拥有它，但你能够使用它  。  
You can't keep it, but you can spend it. And once it's lost you can never get
it back.  你不能储存它，但你能花费。它一旦它消失了，你就再也拿不回来  。  
The average person lives for 78 years. We spend 28.3 years of our life
sleeping. That's almost a third of our life but 30% of us struggle to sleep
well.  人的一生平均有78年，我们花28.3年在睡觉上。差不多花了我们三分之一的时间，但同时有30%的人在为睡个好觉而斗争。  
We spend 10.5 years of our life working but over 50% of us want to leave our
current jobs. Time is more valuable than money.
我们花10.5年去工作，但超过50%的人想离开现在的岗位。时间比金钱更有价值。  
You can get more money, but you can never get more time. We spend 9 years on
TV and social media. We spend 6 years doing chores. We spend 4 years eating
and drinking. We spend 3.5 years in education.
你可以赚更多的钱，但却不能赚到更多的时间。我们在电视和社交媒体上花费了9年的时间；花6年的时间在家务上；4年的时间花在吃吃喝喝上；累计消费3.5年在教育上。  
We spend 2.5 years grooming. We spend 2.5 years shopping. We spend 1.5 years
in child care. We spend 1.3 years commuting.
2.5年花费在打扮上；2.5年花费在购物上；1.5年的时间花费在呵护孩子；1.3年的时间花费在通勤上。  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG54k9t8LHOKzr9mk5a9MpTeR7KyQxxwO7AdRPhtDSgXtAl2kgOibXjZvDd6K6llNSDRKgf10E2m6hPQ/640?wx_fmt=png)  
That leaves us with 9 years. How will we spend that time?
剩下留给我们的只有9年的时间。我们将如何消费这些时间呢？  
Steve Jobs said, “your time is limited, so don't waste it living someone
else's life.”  乔布斯说：生命有限，别将它浪费在重复他人的生活上。  
So there's good news and there's bad news. That bad news is time flies, the
good news is you're the pilot.  所以这有好消息也有坏消息。坏消息是时光飞逝，好消息是你是领航者。  
Imagine you wake up every day with $86400 in your bank account, and at the end
of the night, it's all gone whether you spend it or not. And then the next day
you get another $86400.
想象一下你每天醒来银行账户中有86400美元，一天结束后不管你是否消费它们，它们都会消失，第二天你的账户又会拥有86400美元。  
What would we do with it?  我们将会用它做什么呢？  
Everyday 86400 seconds are deposited into your life account. At the end of the
day, once they're all used up, you get a new 86400 seconds.
每天86400秒都会存入你的生命账户，一天结束后，第二天你将拥有新的86400秒。  
We would never waste it if it was money, so why do we waste it when it comes
to time?  如果是钱我们绝不会浪费，那为什么我们要浪费时间呢？  
Those seconds are so much more powerful than dollars because you can always
make more dollars, you can't always make more time.
时间比金钱的力量更大，因为你能赚更多的钱却赚不了更多的时间  。  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG54k9t8LHOKzr9mk5a9MpTeRGdeg25KAgQlSOnuXWgfI0wn6gSTchLs3sfYfzDOtrJjKv3AgGamib6w/640?wx_fmt=png)  
To understand the value of 1 year, ask a student who failed a grade. To
understand the value of 1 month, ask a mother who lost their child in the
final month. To understand the value of 1 week, ask the editor of an online
magazine. To understand the value of 1 hour, ask the couple who is in a long-
distance relationship. To understand the value of 1 minute, ask the person who
just missed a bus, train or plane. To understand the value of 1 second, ask
the person who just missed an accident. To understand the value of a
millisecond, ask the person who just came 2nd at the Olympics.
想知道一年的价值，去问问考试失败的学生。想知道一个月的价值，去问问在分娩前一个月失去孩子的母亲。想知道一周的价值，去问问在线杂志的编辑们。想知道一小时的价值，去问问异地恋的情侣们。想知道一分钟的价值，去问问刚刚错过巴士、火车或飞机的人想知道一秒钟的价值，去问问刚刚幸免遇难的人。想知道一毫秒的价值，去问问与奥运冠军失之交臂的运动员
。  
We think that's people wasting our time but it's really us giving them the
permission to do that.  我们认为是别人在浪费我们的时间，其实是我们准许他们这样做。  
And in reality, there are 2 people living inside us. Don't let someone be a
priority when all you are to them is an option. Some of us lose the people
most important to us because we don't value their time. Some of us don't
recognize how important someone is to us until they're gone.
现实是，我们体内有两个人。当你对别人而言只是选项时，别将他们排在前面。我们失去了很多重要的人，因为我们没有珍惜他们。有时直到失去了才懂得他们有多重要。  
Inside all of us there are 2 voices:  我们的内心有两种声音：  
One voice that wants to uplift us.  一种声音鼓励我们上进  。  
One voice that wants us to expand.  鼓励我们丰富自己  。  
One voice that wants us to grow.  鼓励我们不断成长  。  
And there's another voice.  还存在着另一种声音  。  
The voice that holds us back.  这种声音令我们退步  。  
The voice that makes us lazy.  让我们变得懒惰  。  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG54k9t8LHOKzr9mk5a9MpTeReqIbUmmoEgSm6Bc7IQ04KOWPoeKVBtiaxKZtUa7yIp6xZFr7ic6nTkyQ/640?wx_fmt=png)

  
The voice that makes us complacent.  让我们骄傲自满  。  
The voice that restricts us from our potential.  限制了我们的潜能  。  
Every day from the moment we wake up until the moment we go to sleep, inside
of us there's this battle between the 2 voices.  每天从醒来到入睡，这两种声音一直在斗争  。  
And guess which one wins?  猜猜谁赢了？  
The one that we listen to the most.  我们听得最多的那个声音赢了  。  
The one that we feed.  我们滋养的那个声音  。  
The one that we amplify.  我们加强的那个声音  。  
It is our choice of how we use our time.  我们如何使用时间，是我们的选择  。  
Life and time are the best two teach  ers.  生活和时间是最好的两位老师  。  
Life teaches us to make good use of time.  生活教会我们更好地使用时间  。  
And time teaches us the value of life.  时间教会我们生活的价值  。  
And as William Shakespeare said,  就如莎士比亚说的一样，  
“Time is very slow for those who wait, very fast for those who are scared,
very long for those who are sad, and very short for those who celebrate, but
for those who love, time is eternal.”
对等待的人来说，时间很慢;对害怕它的人来说，时间很快;对悲观的人来说，时间很漫长;对充满欢愉的人来说，时间很短暂;但对于爱它的人来说，时间即是永恒。  
人这一生最大的错误是什么？  **认为自己有大把的时间** 。

李宗盛曾抱着吉他哽咽地唱着：

_ 当你发现时间是贼了，它早已偷光你的选择。  _

  

转眼，2020只剩一半，请大家在剩余的半年：

_ 那些说不出口的爱，这次好好说，  _

_ 那些没有做的事，立刻就去做，  _

_ 别说来不及，别等待！  _
