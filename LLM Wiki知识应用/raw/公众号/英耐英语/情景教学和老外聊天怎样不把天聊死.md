学习英语那么久，一开口还是会犯难。没等话匣子打开，就想“三板斧”（Hi, thank you, goodbye）啦。或许，这并不是你想要的……

  
喜欢看美剧、英剧，羡慕剧中人物风趣幽默的互动方式，但如果真要面对一个外籍精英男士或金发干练美女，却又不知如何深聊下去……

  
身在知名国际企业，身边不乏各国同事，茶水间遇见，眼波流转却担心无话可聊；工作之余，也不敢和外国同事单独相处，怕把天“聊死”……

  

作为“过来人”，你的种种苦恼，小耐姐都懂！精心收集  **15个开场白** ，整理出实践中最常用的  **“小耐讲堂”三系列**
，共享给爱学习的你，不要错过哦！

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG55LWxwxQqof64Oe1NP9HPOBtuIf80QPdfACyrIh81qGR6WmdibZhpqdicHICN7bFpv8emOUX3oWZFTg/640?wx_fmt=png)

  

**Favorite“最喜欢”系列**

**  
** ** 1\. What's your favorite season?  ** Here's a question that works well
with the "it's always easy to talk about the weather" concept. Follow up by
asking about the current weather conditions, and you have a conversation.
你最喜欢的季节是哪一个？这个问题验证了那句“如果不知道说什么，问天气总是没错的。” 在这之后再问现在的天气状况，对话就更自然、不无聊啦。  
** 2\. What's your favorite food/cuisine?  ** Everyone eats, making this an
icebreaker that works anywhere, anytime.  你最喜欢的食物是什么？民以食为天，这个问题百试不灵。  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG55LWxwxQqof64Oe1NP9HPOBeRAXbtMbjh7vI7Skb9LYZbDgg28pa22KCmHuv6tKCSUbRT7rPcpVnw/640?wx_fmt=png)  
**3\. What's your favorite drink? / What’s your poison?** A wonderfully basic
question, asking about best-loved beverages works at any gathering.
你最喜欢的饮料是什么？绝妙的常规问题。任何聚会上问这个问题总不会错。  
**4\. Where's your favorite place to hang out in town?** Here is another fun
way to start a conversation about your community without being threatening.
And it opens discussion to favorite restaurants, bars, and shops.
镇上最喜欢的去处？这又是一个有趣而不私人的开场问题。接着就可以讨论最喜欢的餐厅，酒吧和商店。  
** 5\. Do you have a favorite local restaurant?  ** This is a great
conversation to get people talking about their favorite local hangouts, which
puts everyone at ease.  你有最喜欢的餐厅吗？这个问题能让人们谈论起日常休闲的地方，使大家放松。

  

  

**Deeper Talks“深聊”系列**

**  
**

** 1.What's your dream job?  **  
Even at a corporate gathering, inquiring about someone's dream job is a fun
way to get a conversation started.  你理想的职业是什么？即使是在公司聚会上，问理想职业也是开始对话的一种有效方式。  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG55LWxwxQqof64Oe1NP9HPOB63EfJ1kGmTibJsDib94DZjIAO33r6rvzm5F7XWoQDaWwLB3nvj5s41aQ/640?wx_fmt=png)  
**** **** **2\. Tell me about something that made you laugh this week.**  
Everyone loves a good laugh! Asking about something that brought on a chuckle
makes anyone smile.  告诉我这星期让你开心的事情吧。人人都爱笑！讨论点有趣的事能让人开怀一笑。  
** 3\. If you could live/go anywhere in the world, where would it be?  **  
Not everyone gets to travel tons, making this a fun question for anyone.
如果你可以住在世界上任何一个地方，你会住在哪？不是每个人都有机会到处旅行，这个问题能让大家都开心起来。  
**4\. What countries have you traveled to? / Where have you been in Europe?**
Even if you haven't traveled the world, asking if others have encourages them
to share memories.  你去过哪些国家？即使你没有出去旅游过，问问别人可以让他们分享回忆。  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG55LWxwxQqof64Oe1NP9HPOBpLfBTOUcHXurIcuK4zgunTHmibWXC5z1Fek1nLu2JDsW7b1ibMNsGcOA/640?wx_fmt=png)  
** 5\. What's the craziest thing you've ever done?  ** For those who are ready
to make a bold first impression, this is a question that gets attention and
can lead to fun conversation.  你做过的最疯狂的事是什么？如果你想让人印象深刻的话，这是一个能够吸引注意而且制造乐趣的话题。

  

  

**Life“生活”系列**

**  
**

** 1.Where did you grow up?  ** This is a great icebreaker that gets someone
talking about the past, which often puts others at ease.
你是在哪长大的？这是非常好的一句开场白，可以让对方谈论起过去，这样对话双方会比较轻松。  
** 2\. Do you have any pets? Are you a dog person or a cat person?  ** People
love their pets, and inquiring about them encourages further conversation.
And, even if the person you're chatting with doesn't have any, it's still a
good start.  你养宠物吗？人们都喜欢宠物，问起他们的宠物能让对话进行下去。即使对方没有宠物，这也不失为一个好的开场白。  
** 3\. What's the first thing you do after work?  ** Do you kick off your
shoes? Hit the gym? Asking how people relax makes them feel relaxed.
这个问题显得有点太具体和Personal,是想发展亲密关系会聊的问题，general一点的，我会问这些：  What do you do for fun?
How do you relax yourself after a long day?
下班后做的第一件事是什么？是踢掉鞋子？去健身？问人们如何放松能让他们感到愉快。  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG55LWxwxQqof64Oe1NP9HPOBAhTQIxLXktUsKm3cszh8WdribFpdiamqFSER1udwEmx99qKQla2aGDNQ/640?wx_fmt=png)  
** 4\. Do you have anything fun planned for the weekend?  **
这个问题显得有压力，人家未必有大计划，问题不能太限定，建议这么问：  What are you up to this weekend?  What's
your plan for the weekend?  Make chatting easy by asking about future plans —
without setting off a stalker vibe. Keep it light and share your plans too.
这个周末有什么计划？关于未来打算的问题让对话变得轻松，但不要刨根问底，给人居心叵测的感觉。尽量保证话题轻松，同时也分享你的计划。  
**5\. Do you like to cook?** Asking about favorite dishes and culinary
adventures sparks delicious dishing.  你喜欢烹饪吗？问问对方最喜欢的菜肴，烹饪方面的经历总能引起有趣的话题。

  

三个系列里面最喜欢哪个话题呢？  **快找身边的同事练习起来吧，活学活用才是王道！**
