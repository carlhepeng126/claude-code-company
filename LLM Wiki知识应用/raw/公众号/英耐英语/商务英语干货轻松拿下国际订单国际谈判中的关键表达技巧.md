**高频词汇**

  

  

**  
** **alternative** n. 备选  eg: I am afraid that we cannot offer you the raise
you requested, but let's discuss some other alternatives.
我方恐怕无法满足你方提高价格的要求，但我们可以讨论一些其他方案。  
** arbitration  ** n. 仲裁  eg: We'd better settle this between us, because a
formal arbitration will cost both of us money.  我们最好能解决这一问题，因为正式的仲裁对双方来说均是开销。  
** bottom-line  ** n. 底线  eg: I'll accept a raise of 2 dollars per ton, but
that's my bottom line.  我方接受每吨提价2美元，但这是底线。  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG56eBvsxwFtqxHicrdaAZkbnnicBaAhCYIX7m8F7ZW0H9aCQU33VkvwBYBejDvk1sQ8jouicNKIibTzPxg/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)  
**consensus** n. 共识  eg: It would be great if we could come to a consensus as
soon as possible.  如果我们能尽快达成共识，那就太好了。  
** deadlock  ** n. 僵局  eg: Since the discussion has come to a deadlock, we
propose continuing the negotiations next week.  鉴于讨论陷入僵局，我方提议下周继续谈判。  
**concession** n. 妥协，让步，减价  eg: I think we can offer all of these concessions,
but not all at once.  我方认为能够提供以上所有让步，但并非一次性满足。  
**high-ball/ low-ball** v. 报高价/报低价  eg: I'm planning to high-ball my
expectations when I open the discussion.  我计划在谈论开始时报出高于我方期望的价格。  
** log-rolling  ** n. 各取所需  eg: After a bit of log-rolling we came to an
agreement that meets the satisfaction of both of us.  经过协商，我们达成了一个令双方满意的协议。  
  

  

  

**表示同意/理解的观点**

  

  

  
I agree with you on that point.  在这一点上我同意你的看法。  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG56eBvsxwFtqxHicrdaAZkbnnSuNJvRFj5SiaBMOyHaQ8IhhmibMsYD5VPRSNfhStaQIhQ4KUX1dC5TjA/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)  
That's a relatively fair suggestion.  这是一个相对合理的建议。  
So what you're saying is that you…  所以你说的意思是你……  
In other words, you feel that…  换句话说，你觉得……  
You have a strong point there.  你在这方面很有道理。  
I think we can both agree that…  我认为我们双方都能同意……  
  

  

  

**就对方观点提出异议**

  

  

  
I'm prepared to compromise, but…  我愿意做出让步，但是……  
I beg to differ.  我不同意你的看法。  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG56eBvsxwFtqxHicrdaAZkbnnwFpqzArQrkC72Y25VnulZeQ0LX9aAm5FJmmWpkkXhlStWKiboJH6C1g/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)  
I don't think that way.  我不这么认为。  
I'm afraid I had something different in mind.  恐怕我的想法有所不同。  
From my perspective…  在我看来……  
I'm afraid that doesn't work for me.  恐怕这对我来说不合适。
