**职场差旅口语提问篇**

May I reconfirm my flight?  我可以确认我的班机吗？  
Where can I make a reservation?  我到哪里可以预订？  
Do I have to make a reconfirmation?  我还要再确认吗？  
Is there any discount for the USA Railpass?  火车通行证有折扣吗？  
How long will I have to wait?  我要等多久呢？  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG576HrQwppgQuXMOKibyibACYHN3Vqve4MCCWYZPO0lSyK3O0ia5kNNImzwS2CGbmVdueBK0yibPHneyicg/640?wx_fmt=png)  
Could you check it to my final destination？  你能把它托运到我的目的地吗？  
Which would you prefer, a smoking seat or a non-smoking seat?
你喜欢哪种，吸烟座还是禁烟座呢？  
Here is a copy of itinerary we have worked out for you and your friends. Would
you please have a look at it?  这是我们为你和你的朋友拟定的活动日程安排。请过目一下，好吗？  
Is there any way of ensuring we'll have enough time for our talks?
我们是否能保证有充足的时间来谈判？  
Could you take some time to study what we've discussed during the day?
你们可以用点时间来研究讨论一下白天谈判的情况吗？  

  

**职场差旅口语讨论篇**

I've come to make sure that your stay in Shanghai is a pleasant one.
我特地为你们安排使你们在上海的逗留愉快。  
Shall we talk about the schedule, if it's convenient for you right now.
如果你们感到方便的话，我想现在讨论一下日程安排的问题。  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG576HrQwppgQuXMOKibyibACYHfCibeZX3oUp81aVknORY7sPoFxFuI9IicibpNCc2ydEOibXMM4mT6Prs8Q/640?wx_fmt=png)  
I think we can draw up a tentative plan now.  我认为现在可以先草拟一具临时方案。  
If he wants to make any changes, minor alternations can be made then.
如果他有什么意见的话，我们还可以对计划稍加修改。  
That'll put us both in the picture.  这样双方都能了解全面的情况。  
Then we'd have some ideas of what you'll be needing.  那么我们就会心中有点儿数，知道你们需要什么了。  
Better having something we can get our hands on rather than just spending all
our time talking.  有些实际材料拿到手总比坐着闲聊强。  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG576HrQwppgQuXMOKibyibACYHvVXX9nD4HFzPPUkeNZg6ZcaP6vsSjauNh4viaialNMVlxP8LBx3ic9YmQ/640?wx_fmt=png)  
It'll be easier for us to get down to facts then.  这样就容易进行实质性的谈判了。  
We've got to report back to the head office.  我们还要回去向总部汇报情况呢。  
If you have any questions on the details, feel free to ask.
如果对某些细节有意见的话，请提出来。  
I can see you have put a lot of time on it.  我相信你在制定这个计划上一定花了不少精力吧。  
Thanks for your cooperation.  谢谢你们的合作。
