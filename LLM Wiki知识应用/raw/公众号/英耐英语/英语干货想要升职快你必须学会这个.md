**简报呈现三原则** ****

**Expressing Yourself in a Logical Way** ****

** < ** ** 内容 CONTENT  ** ** > **

Well planned; clear, logical structure

Clear explicit links between parts

Full of relevant information

Contains an introduction and conclusion

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG56RATCOB7zKYqGYL3dxBApu9Dknj1ouJ5KO9Ce4KCFCowoT6TThg4Cex7OuftD1XZppk90FY3eTBQ/640?wx_fmt=png)

**  
**

** < ** ** 表达 DELIVERY  ** ** > **

Clear & fluent speech at suitable volume

Use of pauses for emphasis

Natural spoken language

  

** < ** ** 肢体语言 BODY LANGUAGE  ** ** > **

Use of body to increase meaning

No nervous or distracting gestures

Lots of eye contact with audience

Confident, positive, relaxed attitude

  

  

**表达的逻辑性**

**Expressing Yourself in a Logical Way**

** < ** ** 顺序很重要  ** ** > **

** Sequence Markers：  ** These signal the time or sequence relationship
between two or more stages  ** Beginning  ** ** First  ** /first of
all/initially/to start with/the first step/at the first stage  ** Second  **
/secondly/the second step/at the second stage  ** Third  ** /thirdly/the third
step/at the third stage  ** Then  ** /after that  ** Next  **
/subsequently/the next step/at the next stage  ** Finally  ** /the final
step/at the final stage  ** End  **

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG56RATCOB7zKYqGYL3dxBApumsvYVD05eznCtadvgyNp7iakticYUNM7kD1uQeStXoFWO0nibNFOAggsQ/640?wx_fmt=png)

**  
**

** < ** ** 举个栗子  ** ** > **

** a.  ** **First** there was the market research plan,  **then** the market
survey itself and  **finally** pricing and forecasts.  ** b.  ** ** Initially
** , we looked into the option of X, however, we  **then** also considered Y,
but we  **finally** opted for Z.  

** < ** ** 划重点 Points to Note  ** ** > **

** 1\.  ** ** ** Prepare your report beforehand and present it in an organized
way. If the reporting meetings you attend are regular, try to use the same
basic structure for each of your reports so that participants know what to
expect.  ** 2\.  ** ** ** Sign post the stages of your report with clear
sequence markers so that your listeners can follow your report easily.  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG56RATCOB7zKYqGYL3dxBApuJ2TjJ6guibf4V25S9LDvAvOPJFbzNJJtsvfDJDg1Z1kSOCRNPksv4yQ/640?wx_fmt=png)

  

** 3\.  ** Reports should be concise and on the point. However,be careful to
be complete – do not miss any relevant information that is needed to deliver
the listeners.  
** 4\.  ** Be prepared to clarify your information when clarification is
requested.  ** 5\.  ** ** ** Be ready for the interruptions from the
chairperson or other persons'present.  
  

**常用练习句式**

**Free-speaking Practice – Useful Language**

** < 开门见山 Introduction > **

My presentation will take around about 10 minutes. If you have any questions,
I’ll be happy to answer them at the end of my talk.  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG56RATCOB7zKYqGYL3dxBApucCHjo4PrIpqXol41ibw797hX6iciapJFHstJU7dt5qvMkeyBiaFGiczcDTA/640?wx_fmt=png)  

** < ** ** 敲黑板 Main section  ** ** > **

** First of all  ** , I’d like to look at …  Any questions so far?  Secondly,
…  I must emphasize that …  The question is …  
**I’d like to** turn your attention to … You will see that …  As you can see
from the graph …  The figures show that …  
**If I can** just side-track for a moment, …  As I mentioned earlier, …  I’ll
come back to that in a moment.  Now let’s move on to the question of …  
**This brings me** to my last point, which is …  As you know, …  In general, …
On the other hand, …  Finally, …  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG56RATCOB7zKYqGYL3dxBApuHY7KDqzST6bZy2vXETZA5T8AeGdtqYSU8icNzw6dMNiaF71yIMLd6HbQ/640?wx_fmt=png)

** **  

** < ** ** 总结一下 Conclusion  ** ** > **

**In conclusion** , let me briefly go through the main points again. First I
talked about …,  **then** I described …, and finally I ….  **Alright** ,
that’s all I want to present today. Let me finish with a huge thanks to
everyone. Thank you so much for your attention.  **And now** , if you have any
questions, I’ll be happy to try and answer them.
