** 一、凡事多问几个“为什么”——why 思维  ** ** Everything to ask "why"  **  
Why do you ask more questions? As you can get twice the result with half the
effort and you will grow much faster than others. For example:  
凡事多问几个为什么？工作往往能够事半功倍，你的成长速度会比别人快很多。例如：  
❌Get a job assignment, I do as commanded.  接到工作任务，领导怎么说，我就怎么做。  
·  Why did the boss assign me this job?  ·  Why do you want to do this?  ·
How to define Good?  ·  What should We do to make it Better?  ·  What result
does the leader hope to achieve?  
· 领导为什么安排我做这个工作?  · 为什么要这么做?  · 要做到什么程度才算好？  · 应该怎么做才能更好?  · 领导希望达到一个什么样的效果?  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG57lbP5655icQqgiacvcW81vRjia901udJ1UG1WqT9CBVSseOzVf1t7cibFjLrvytxCFveoU3wGJy1ibtdA/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)  
In the workplace, if you can't take the initiative to think and change
yourself, you will be changed by others. That's a key reason why, years after
graduation, the gap between many people and their peers is widening.  
职场中，你不能主动思考，不主动改变自己，你就会被别人改变。这就是为什么很多人在毕业多年之后，跟同龄人差距越来越大的一个关键原因。  
  
** 二、学习“说话艺术”  ** ** Learn the Art of Speaking  **  
The art of speaking and communication "learned quietly" from leaders: Say yes,
then no, then ask questions (to make it easier for the other person to follow
your advice).  
从领导身上“悄悄学会”的说话艺术和沟通方法:  先肯定+再否定+后反问（让对方更容易听从你的意见）。  
For example, in a meeting, you need to discuss a plan, and a colleague brings
up an idea, but it is obviously not feasible:  
[First affirmation] : "This idea is original"  [Again negative] : "But this
time the focus of the solution is to solve xx, if another method may be more
appropriate"  [back question] : "xx, do you think so?"  
例如在开会时，你们要讨论一个方案，同事提了一个想法，但很明显不可行：  
【先肯定】：“这个想法很有新意”  【再否定】：“但这次的方案侧重点是解决xx,如果用另外一个方法可能会更合适”  【后反问】：“xx，你觉得是吗？”  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG57lbP5655icQqgiacvcW81vRjjSvPfTibaMAUiaggoqP1e3KqicvQfT0R3uUaRtfjqdpb7q3qLM260YCCA/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)  
Without embarrassing the other person, he made his point with reason.
既没有让对方难堪，也有理有据地表达了自己的观点。  
  
** 三、培养对事情的“判断力”  ** ** Develop "judgment" about things  **  
The most fundamental benefit of having an accurate sense of things is:  
· Long-term thinking Training - Develop your own methodology by considering
the depth and dimension of a matter  
· Sufficient knowledge = A large amount of input is obtained through
reading/studying/communicating with different people/participating in
activities  
· Large amount of information = Look for platforms that can provide you with
information (companies, Internet, pay for knowledge, etc.) and absorb the best
information and hone your judgment  
对于事情有准确的判断力，最根本的是得益于:  
· 长期的思维训练:对事情的思考深度和维度,形成自己的方法论  
· 充足的知识储备：大量的知识输入，通过阅读/学习/与不同的人交流/参加活动等获取  
· 强大的信息量：寻找能给你提供信息的平台(公司/互联网/知识付费等)，吸收其最精华的信息，打磨自己的判断力  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG57lbP5655icQqgiacvcW81vRjtDJBSzlHOicNalRbI9c49icyhNicn31oKgr59zxfd24HzhACiaSMHEW46g/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)  
"Observe those who are really competitive in the workplace, are the master who
have a strong "judgment" about things, can quickly seize the opportunity in
the complex workplace,, always can make the most correct choice at the
critical moment.  
“观察职场中那些真正厉害的人，都是对事情有极强“判断力”的高手，能在复杂的职场中，快速抓住机会，总能在关键时刻做出最正确的选择。  
这就是选择>努力！  
  
** 四、提升“自我认知”  ** ** Improve your "self-awareness"  **  
· What are your strengths?  1\. What parts of your job did you find most
enjoyable/rewarding?  2\. Ask your leaders, colleagues, relatives and friends,
what are your characteristics? (Summarize in three adjectives)  3\. What
interests you in your work and life?  4\. Use assessment tools (to aid
judgment)  
· 你必须要明白自己的优势是什么？  1.你所从事的工作中，哪些部分你做的最得心应手/最有成就感？
2.问问你的领导同事、亲朋好友，你的特点是什么？（用三个形容词概括）  3.工作和生活中，你感兴趣的事情是什么？  4.借助测评工具(辅助判断)  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG57lbP5655icQqgiacvcW81vRjYN0TyIytEFsPooyqUPZlVPu1yic6rbff2HRCpxuzj4UfTNfHmJnffiaw/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)  
· What do you Want to Be?  1\. Think back to when you were a child. What did
you look forward to most?  2\. What are the common characteristics of the
person or thing you like?  3\. What do you care about most? What is important
to you?  4\. How do you want to spend your life?  5\. Do you work for the
money, or are you more focused on your personal worth?  
· 要清楚自己想成为什么样的人？  1.回想你小时候，你最期待什么？  2.你喜欢的人或事，有哪些共性特点？  3.你最在意的是什么？什么对你来说很重要？
4.你希望如何度过这一生?  5.你是为了钱而工作，还是更注重发挥自身价值?  
A person's real growth, is the continuous evolution of self-cognition!
一个人真正的成长，就是自我认知能力的不断进化！
