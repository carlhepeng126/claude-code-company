汪曾祺老先生说过：“人，要有点儿业余爱好。”尤其是在办公室里，有相同or相似爱好的同事总是更容易打成一片。很多职场人士总是说工作压力大，任务重，精神透支。如果能在工作之余，有自己喜欢的业余爱好，作为一种调剂，既缓解了压力也得到了乐趣。今天分享给大家的话题是：
**如何用英语谈论我们的业余爱好，以及一些常见的业余活动如何地道表达。**  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_gif/2iaIDwqRtG541dAHEC24Eq4ZSmXXcya1On5jObgwODtM0hyOeIGBGnWu80Aq7wxYibur3qqBwnZ1Gl9KND6Hu5NA/640?wx_fmt=gif)

  

**Ways to ask**

  
**** 我们在谈论业余活动的时候，最常见的问题是：  ** What do you do in your free time?  **
你空闲时间一般做什么？  或者:  ** What are your hobbies?  ** 你的兴趣爱好是什么？  
例：  A: What do you do in your free time?  B:  **In my free time I love / like
to** listen to musics.  
A: What are your hobbies?  B:  **My hobbies are** reading and watching TV.  
  

**Following up**

  
**** 在对方回答之后，可以看情况再问一两个问题深入了解。看一个例子：  
A  :  What do you do in your free time?  B: In my free time I love to listen
to music.  A:  ** What type of music do you like?  ** B: I like pop music.
我喜欢流行音乐。  A:  ** Do you have a favourite singer or group?  ** 你最喜欢哪个歌手/组合？  B
:  My favourite singer / group is Taylor Swift.

**Free Time Activities**

  

**Go to the cinema** \- to see Hollywood blockbuster movies, Bollywood movies
(from India), art films, animated films. You can also say go to the movies.
Some film categories are: Comedy, Drama, Horror, Thriller, Action, Science
Fiction (Sci-Fi), Fantasy, Documentary, Musical.  
**Watch TV** \- Different types of television programs are: The News, Soap
Operas, Criminal Investigation Dramas, Medical Dramas, Reality TV, Situation
Comedies (Sit-Coms), Talk Shows, Documentaries, Cartoons, Game Shows, Sports
programs, Movies, Political programs, Religious programs.  
**Spend time with family** \- You can do many things with your family.
Usually, the fact that you are together is more important than the activity.  
**Go out with friends** \- You can also do many things with your friends, like
go out to a bar, go dancing at a club, have dinner at a restaurant, play a
sport, sit down and talk, go out for a coffee, have a barbecue, or any other
activity that you all enjoy. Or sometimes when you don't do anything specific,
you can say hang out with friends.  
**Surf the internet** \- On the internet, you can research a topic you are
interested in using a search engine, visit your favourite websites, watch
music videos, create your own video and upload it for other people to see,
maintain contact with your friends using a social networking site, write your
thoughts in a blog, learn what is happening in the world by reading news
websites, etc.  
**Play video games** \- You can play games on your computer or on a game
consoles, like PlayStation, X-Box, Wii, PSP, Gameboy, etc. You can play on
your own or with your friends or family.  
**Play a musical instrument** \- Learn to play the piano, guitar, violin,
cello, flute, piano accordion, mouth organ, panpipes, clarinet, saxophone,
trumpet, etc. You can play on your own or with a group, such as a band or an
orchestra.  
**Listen to music** \- Turn up the volume and listen to your favourite type of
music, such as pop, rock, hip hop, rhythm & blues, blues, jazz, classical,
soul, heavy metal.  
**Read** \- Many people love to read both fiction,non-fiction books and
magazines. If you like fiction, you can read novels, short stories, crime
fiction, romance, etc. If you like non-fiction, you can read biographies,
autobiographies, or books on history, science, philosophy, religion, or any
other topic you are interested in.  
**Go to the park** \- You can go to the park alone, with family or with
friends. You can take a picnic rug,a picnic basket and have a picnic. You can
read, sleep, kick a football around, climb trees or play on the children's
playground.  
**Go to cultural locations and events** \- There are many types of cultural
locations and events. You can go to the museum, an art gallery or the zoo to
see animals from around the world. You can go to concerts, plays, musicals,
dance recitals and opera performances.  
**Go shopping** \- Many people like to go shopping. They often travel to
shopping malls to buy clothes, furniture and gardening items.  
**Cook** \- Many people like to cook different types of food. You can make
meals for breakfast, lunch and dinner. You can bake cakes, cookies, and
pastries in the oven. Some people boast that they have a special recipe - ask
them to cook it for you!  
**Study something** \- There are many things you could study. You might find
it is interesting! You could study a language, cooking, painting or making
furniture. You could even study the hieroglyphs of the ancient Egyptians, if
you want to!  
**Gardening** \- You can plant flowers, vegetables or herbs,maintain your
garden by watering it, pulling the weeds and feeding it with fertilizer.  
**Exercise and play a sport** \- To stay fit and healthy, you can do exercise
alone, such as swimming or working out at a gym, you can play a team sport,
such as football or basketball.

业余爱好作为职场的第二技能，也是个人能力的展现，会为你的职场生涯锦上添花。工作之外，拥有共同兴趣的业余爱好，让你与领导、同事间也会有更多的话题。培养业余爱好，为自己充电，让生活变得有趣，同时也让自己更具竞争力!
