感恩节将至，当北美合作伙伴纷纷开启假期模式，除了群发一句"Happy Thanksgiving"，我们还能做点什么，让客户真切感受到我们的心意？

  

对于出海企业而言，感恩节不只是一个西方节日，更是我们主动联络、深化信任的绝佳契机。  一句恰到好处的感谢，不仅温暖人心，更能为下一阶段的合作铺路。

  

今天，小耐姐为你准备了一系列真诚不刻意、得体不套路的感恩表达，从简短祝福到完整邮件模板，助你在这个感恩节，说出客户想听的那句“谢谢”。

  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG55QtREEUUhwZUp6GfgsfDuTmqgR5KibSJ6LWQRyw8eWsMRpuMQLZJwKRDKpgq5XcvPUhqqhzECAZQQ/640?wx_fmt=png)

  

** 简洁走心版 · 适合社媒/短信  **

I'm thankful to partner someone like you — not just on Thanksgiving, but every
day.

能拥有您这样的客户，不仅在感恩节，每一天我都心怀感激。

  

I'm reflecting on my blessings this year, and our collaboration is certainly
one of the most cherished.

今年我细数自己所获的福气，与您的合作便是其中之一。

  

I truly value you as both a trusted client and a respected friend.

感谢您既是客户，也是朋友。

  

![](https://mmecoa.qpic.cn/sz_mmecoa_png/2iaIDwqRtG54U1LtV8kzgMppEa9JLx5jTEnxzqlrkZzEUapoTQObHmAvQapDeFu8lkoAFTicgwQUBCZ9bRMH4mpQ/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)

  

Thank you for trusting us and being part of our journey.

感谢您的信任，陪伴我们一路前行。

  

Wishing you a restful break and a joyful Thanksgiving.

愿您度过一个轻松愉悦的感恩节假期。

  

  

** 真诚致谢版 · 适合邮件正文  **

如果你希望表达得更完整，不妨选用这一段：

  

This Thanksgiving, our team wants to express our sincere gratitude. Your
support has been instrumental to our growth this year, and we truly value the
trust you’ve placed in us. We look forward to continuing our partnership in
the year ahead. Wishing you and your family a warm and happy holiday.

  

值此感恩节，我们团队想向您表达诚挚的谢意。感谢您一直以来的支持，陪伴我们成长，我们非常珍视这份信任。期待在新的一年里继续携手前行。祝您与家人节日温馨快乐。

Subject: Thank You This Thanksgiving

  

Dear [Client's Name],

  

As Thanksgiving approaches, I wanted to take a moment to express how grateful
we are for your partnership and support this past year.

  

Working with you has been both a privilege and a pleasure, and we truly
appreciate the trust you’ve placed in our team. It’s clients like you who make
what we do meaningful.

  

We wish you a relaxing and joyful Thanksgiving surrounded by loved ones. We're
eager to achieve even greater things together in the year ahead!

Warmly,

  

[Your Name]

[Your Position]

一句适时、具体的感谢，不仅能强化你在客户心中的专业与温度，更区别于千篇一律的群发祝福，为来年的合作奠定信任基础。

  

这个感恩节，不妨花几分钟，选出最贴合心意的一句，发送给那些最重要的客户吧～
