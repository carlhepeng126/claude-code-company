**谈判小技巧 STRATEGIES**

** 1.积极开场 START POSITIVELY  ** ** eg.  ** We'll come out from this meeting as
winners.  这次会谈的结果将会产生双赢局面。  
** 2.表达感谢 EXPRESS GRATITUDE  ** ** eg.  ** It's an honor to speak with all of
you.  很荣幸有这个机会和大家交流。  
** 3.有效提问 USE EFFECTIVE QUESTIONING  ** ** eg.  ** What is your acceptable
price range?  你们可接受的价格范围是什么呢？  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG56YUJ8otTpAZ7qYZxibvMib2otOSAZjWN7PegOAyibqLJ61m180VjjANMa1sxChZrZ2B3icj7AGE2VN5A/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)  
** 4.缓和气氛 EASE TENSION  ** ** eg.  ** If I seemed sharp a few moments ago, be
assured that it was only due to my determination to make this work.
如果我刚才的言辞有些尖锐，请放心，这只是因为我决心完成这项工作。  
** 5.态度谦和 SHOW HUMILITY  ** ** eg.  ** We'd like you to consider our request
once again.  我希望你们能再考虑一下我们的建议。  
** 6.避免误会 AVOID MISUNDERSTANDINGS  ** ** eg.  ** I'm afraid you misunderstood
me.What I was trying to say was……  恐怕你误解我了。我想说的是……

  

掌握了上面的实用小技巧，小编也为大家贴心整理了产品、市场、价格相关的常用话术，让你应对谈判场合更加游刃有余！一起来看看吧！

  

**产品相关 PRODUCTS**

1.How do you feel like the quality of our products?  你觉得我们产品的质量怎么样？  
2.Our products are of great quality.  我们的产品质量上乘。  
3.All products have to pass strict inspection before they go out.
所有产品出厂前必须要经过严格检查。  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG56YUJ8otTpAZ7qYZxibvMib2oicGOHC07B6jaDhmVMnE4tF6GUln03dcvriczt9DhXPqiaW7bc5nic2dLHg/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)  
4.First of all, I will outline the characteristics of our product.
首先我将简略说明我们商品的特性。  
5.This is our most recently developed product.  这是我们最近开发的产品。  
6.I'm sure you'll be pleased with this product.  我敢保证你会喜欢这种产品的。  
7.If you are interested in our products, you can order a small quantity first.
如果你对我们的产品感兴趣，可以先少量订一批。  
8.We've already had a big demand for this product.  这种产品我们已有很大的需求量。  

**市场相关 MARKET**

1.This product is doing very well in foreign countries.  这种产品在国外很畅销。  
2.Our product is competitive in the international market.  我们的产品在国际市场上具有竞争力。  

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG56YUJ8otTpAZ7qYZxibvMib2owzEvsfFAo2SaN8DiaLCX3cGYHeI8Px71ibtx2LWHgQMrbM8DSGicH0HMg/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)

  
3.We are always improving our technology to cater to the world market.
我们一直在提高我们技术，以满足世界市场的要求。  
4.This new product is to the taste of European market.  这种新产品欧洲很受欢迎。  
5.We are sure our products will go down well in your market, too.
我们确定我们的产品在你们的市场也会畅销。  
6.The market prices are changing frequently.  市场价格随时都在变化。  
7.We have on hand many enquiries from other countries.  我们手头上有来自其他国家的很多询盘。  
8.All these articles are our best selling lines.  这些产品都是我们的畅销货。  

**价格相关 PRICE**

1.Could you please advise your budget?  能否告知您的预算。  
2.To a certain extent, our price depends on how large your order is.
在某种程度上，我们的价格就得看你们的订单有多大。  
3.I wonder if our specifications meet your requirements.  不知道您认为我们的规格是否符合你的要求？  
4.I'm sure the prices we submitted are competitive.  我敢肯定我们的价格是非常有竞争力的。  
![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/sz_mmbiz_png/2iaIDwqRtG56YUJ8otTpAZ7qYZxibvMib2odxqEYHiaD2CAWM2k5Osonhqnz2DY2LH6QCkKGoT1jARZF7d06s9EZVw/640?wx_fmt=png&from=appmsg)  
5.If we cut down the price, we can hardly keep reasonable profit on the order.
如果我们减价,这个订单我们就几乎没任何利润了。  
6.Thank you for your inquiry. Would you tell us what quantity you require so
that we can work out the offer?  谢谢你询价。为了便于我方提出报价，能否请你谈谈你方需求数量？  
7.Could you tell me which kind of payment terms you'll choose?
能否告知你们将采用哪种付款方式？  
8.As long as the quality is good. It is all right if the price is a bit
higher.  因为我们的高质量，我们的价格自然就会高一些。
