**导语**

  

近年来，科技发展与新冠疫情极大地加速了传统办公模式的转型与改革。  远程办公与在线工作在考验员工的自我管理能力的同时，  也对领导者提出了严峻的考验。  

  

未来职场需要怎样的领导力？

未来优秀的领导者又应当具备怎样的特征与能力？  
  
本文总结未来职场的七大领导力趋势，

并给出将相关技能投入具体实践的若干建议。  
  
今日分享于此，以馈读者。

  

**

****趋势** ** ****1：  
** ** ****基于自我意识的领导力** **

**

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/QzzpDQVZdo701IuhrR1WYuZBBJyCyejicjVUVicgiaM505dgZbNkRgfoFhEf6YEktK5NqSFd5beObdxD3DyZsYichg/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

  

**如何培养？**

领导者需要培养识别自己的情绪、信仰、行为、目的、激情和价值观的能力。

  

Leaders need to develop the ability to recognise their emotions, beliefs,
behaviours, purpose, passion, and values.

**有何作用？**

从自我意识出发的领导力能够创造一种包容和接受的文化：作为一个领导，你可以公开表现自己的感受和当前面临的困难，这种行为能为员工创造一个安全的工作空间，员工的个性与健康得到保证，员工也就有了更高的满意度。这直接关系到更高的留任率。89%的员工倾向于认为，一家重视员工健康状况的公司更值得他们为之工作。

  

Leadership from a place of self-awareness create a culture of inclusion and
acceptance: being open about feelings and challenges as a leaders create a
safe space for employees to do the same, and employees whose individuality and
wellbeing are supported have higher jo satisfaction. This directly relates to
higher retention rates: 89% of employees supporting wellbeing initiatives are
more likely to recommend the company as a good place to work.

**

****趋势2** ** ****：  
** **

** **基于健康的领导力** **

**

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/QzzpDQVZdo701IuhrR1WYuZBBJyCyejicMucGXzqvdO0Lkt8CaiaeLNC2Ao9WfpiamkPthQ0iaonVlDNnyxPnp4dXA/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

  

**工作对员工健康的影响**

对70%的人来说，他们的经理对他们心理健康造成的影响比治疗师或医生造成的影响更大，而与他们的伴侣的影响力相当。43%的员工表示工作让他们疲惫不堪，78%的人称压力对他们的工作表现产生了负面影响。

  

For 70% of people, their manager has more impact on their mental health than
therapist or doctor, and equal to their partner. 43% of employees report
exhaustion and 78% say stress negatively impacts their work performance.

**领导力如何改善员工健康？**

对于经理/领导而言，关注员工心理健康是至关重要的，同时也是商业利益所在--当员工拥有积极的心理健康时，63%的员工会将专心工作，80%的员工感到精力充沛。

  

It’s vital for managers/leaders to pay attention to mental health and also
business benefits — when employees have positive mental health, 63% are
committed to their work and 80% feel energised.

**趋势3：**  

**基于培训及成就他人的领导力**

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/QzzpDQVZdo701IuhrR1WYuZBBJyCyejicJsKhC3TtHoqU8r5sFPxQNkg81KQ5kLY0K3MBXhwoPYjRa7Hiak3Yib2A/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

  

**优秀的领导成就他人**

伟大的领导者提升自我，但顶尖的领导者同时也会成就他人。领导力的作用是能使他人的潜力得到充分发挥（商业培训也是如此）。

  

Great leaders develop themselves, but the best develop others too. Leadership
as enabling the full potential in others (also, business coaching).

**趋势4：**

**克服商业不确定性的领导力**

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/QzzpDQVZdo701IuhrR1WYuZBBJyCyejictlnzqtQRbj2ibrCibf7MVlnibZdARa9mRRmMWSicicwSicl1uxU6Y9pFLRfw/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

  

**关键词的变化**

今年的关键词：保留职位和削减成本，取代了2022年的关键词：安静离职（译注：指按时下班，绝不下班的工作模式）和效率偏执。

  

This year’s alliterative term: Role retention and cost cutting to replace
2022’s quiet quitting and productivity paranoia.

**领导力的不变**

领导者仍需为员工创造积极的工作环境，使其茁壮成长。加倍重视领导力和管理培训（业务辅导），关注员工的稳定、信心和忠诚。

  

Leaders will need to still create positive workplaces for their employees to
thrive. Double down on leadership and management training (business coaching),
focus on stability, confidence, and loyalty.

**趋势5：**  

****均匀分配领导力** **——在线办公、线下办公及混合式办公** **

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/QzzpDQVZdo701IuhrR1WYuZBBJyCyejicON8WZU5eyLXib8rwX5fJOHdVUvQibsEUoQAELdSFCIX6Ud3K8RJCdDhg/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

  

**接受现状**

2023年，灵活办公、远程办公和混合式办公将继续成为常态--灵活办公是95%的员工的新偏好。

  

Flexible, remote and hybrid work will continue to be the norm in 2023 - with
flexibility the new preference for 95% of employees.

**改进流程**

领导者需要建立正确的内部流程和管理工具，以实现向混合型工作的转换，并创造一种平等但异步的职场文化，使每个员工都能不分时区、地点地了解到公司的动态。远程和混合式办公的员工也需要被平等地纳入公司的管理体系，以避免他们被其他员工孤立，并需要注意避免出现
"职场小山头 "的排他性心态。

  

Leaders need to have the right internal processes and tools for the switch to
hybrid and creating an equal, asynchronous culture so that everyone is up to
speed regardless of timezone, or location. Remote and hybrid employees also
need to be included equally to avoid isolation and care needs to be taken to
avoid an “office gang” mentality of exclusion.

**趋势6：**  

**拥抱多样性的领导力**

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/QzzpDQVZdo701IuhrR1WYuZBBJyCyejicUvib1aNRjvibQqN6RJpicZH0jibcRzp5gwtXQ2HVY6WxwURDuBkhxUqibsw/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

  

**应对挑战**

多样性、老龄化，以及员工的稀缺性--即所谓的"人口荒漠化"。

  

Diversity, ageing, also worker scarcity — the “demographic drought.”

**趋势7：**  

**基于学习与倾听的领导力**

![](https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_jpg/QzzpDQVZdo701IuhrR1WYuZBBJyCyejicBePpkoia57hrzFnVKyItf2LvoHQw8GlBEJLOWy7Jic7LxGk3cxP5Nia7A/640?wx_fmt=jpeg)

  

**

在现场的领导力

**

领导者需要先亲自踏上学习之旅，然后才能向他们的员工发出（共同学习的）邀请，并将学习融入到工作流程中。

  

Leaders need to start by going on a learning journey too, and then extend this
invitation to their employees. Put learning into the flow of work.

**倾听的重要性**

能够有效倾听员工的领导者能与他们的团队建立更好的关系，并与他们进行更积极和富有成效的互动。研究显示，移情倾听的能力和有效的领导力之间存在关联。

  

Leaders with effective listening skills develop better rapport with their
teams and have more positive and fruitful interactions with them. Research
established a correlation between empathic listening skills and effective
leadership.
