## 桶1：企业外语培训（⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ · 核心桶）

### 可直接用作课程模块的内容
| # | 片段 | 应用场景 |
|---|------|---------|
| 1 | 开场短语7种：`Ok, shall we get started?` / `Let's get down to business` / `Shall we kick off?` | 会议英语入门课——开场模块 |
| 2 | 欢迎+介绍：`I'd like to introduce Miguel and Samantha` / `I'm not sure everyone knows...` | 跨文化会议中的破冰与介绍 |
| 3 | 议程陈述模板：`As you can see from the agenda, there are 4 items. Firstly...secondly...` | 会议结构化表达核心模块 |
| 4 | 征求意见：`What do you all think?` / `Does anyone have an opinion?` / `What are everyone's thoughts?` | 会议中的参与技巧 |
| 5 | 强弱观点表达对比：`I'm convinced that`(强) vs `I think that`(弱) | 语气控制——涉外沟通中的重要技能 |
| 6 | 深入追问：`Could you say a little more about that?` / `Would you elaborate?` | 会议中的深度提问技巧 |
| 7 | 确认理解：`In other words, what you're saying is...` / `If I'm hearing you right...` | 跨文化会议中的确认技巧 |
| 8 | 回到正题：`I think we're digressing. Can we get back on topic?` | 会议控制——主持人必备技能 |
| 9 | 推迟讨论：`Let's deal with this point in the next meeting` / `Shall we skip this item?` | 时间管理技能 |
| 10 | 插话技巧：`Excuse me, can I just interrupt quickly?` / `Can I piggyback on what Shelly was saying?` | 商务沟通中的"礼貌打断" |
| 11 | 截止插话：`If I could just finish what I was saying` / `May I just finish my point?` | 保护发言权 |
| 12 | 确认任务：`John, you're doing the report on...` / `Dave, you will get the data...` | 会议后行动闭环 |
| 13 | 闭会：`I think we've covered everything` / `Let's call it a day` / `I'll confirm the date...` | 会议收尾 |
