Engineering and Construction Contract Hong Kong Edition  
2023版香港NEC工程承建合约  
This contract should be used for the appointment of a contractor for engineering and construction work, including any level of design responsibility  
本合约适用于委聘承建商进行工程与施工工作，包括任何程度的设计责任  
An NEC document July 2023  
NEC文件 2023年7月

中英文共计7.2万余字，word 文档。  
部分章节示例如下：  
Core Clauses  
核心条款  
1\. GENERAL  
第1条 一般规定  
Actions 10  
10\. 当事方的行为  
10.1 The Parties, the Project Manager and the Supervisor shall act as stated in this contract.  
10.1 合约双方、项目经理及监理工程师应按本合约的规定行事。  
10.2 The Parties, the Project Manager and the Supervisor act in a spirit of mutual trust and co- operation.  
10.2 合约双方、项目经理及监理工程师应以相互信任与合作的精神行事。  
Identified and defined terms 11  
11\. 标识术语与定义术语  
11.1 In these conditions of contract, terms identified in the Contract Data are in italics and defined terms have capital initials.  
11.1 在本合约条件中，合约数据中标识的术语以斜体表示，定义术语以首字母大写表示。  
11.2 (1) The Accepted Programme is the programme identified in the Contract Data or is the latest programme accepted by the Project Manager. The latest programme accepted by the Project Manager supersedes previous Accepted Programmes.  
11.2 (1) 已接纳的进度计划是指合约数据中标识的进度计划，或项目经理最新接纳的进度计划。项目经理最新接纳的进度计划取代之前所有的已接纳进度计划。  
(2) Code on Access to Information is the code promulgated by the government of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region which provides a formal framework for access to information held by government departments and can be accessed at http://www.access. gov.hk.  
(2) 《公开资料守则》是指由香港特别行政区政府颁布的守则，该守则为查阅政府部门持有的资料提供了正式的框架，并可于 http://www.access.gov.hk 查阅。  
(3) Completion is when the Contractor has  
• done all the work which the Scope states is to be done by the Completion Date and  
• corrected notified Defects which would have prevented the Client from using the works  
or others from doing their work.  
If the work which the Contractor is to do by the Completion Date is not stated in the Scope, Completion is when the Contractor has done all the work necessary for the Client to use the works and for others to do their work.  
(3) 竣工是指承建商已：  
• 完成工程范围中规定须在竣工日期前完成的所有工作，以及  
• 修正在已通知的缺陷中那些会妨碍客户使用工程或妨碍他人进行其工作的缺陷。  
如果工程范围未规定承建商须在竣工日期前完成的工作，则竣工是指承建商已完成为使客户能够使用工程以及使他人能够进行其工作所必需的全部工作。  
(4) The Completion Date is the completion date unless later changed in accordance with the contract.  
(4) 竣工日期即为合约所载的竣工日期，除非其后按照合约规定予以变更。  
Contractor’s proposals 16  
16\. 承建商的建议  
16.1 The Contractor may propose to the Project Manager that the Scope provided by the Client is changed in order to reduce the amount the Client pays to the Contractor for Providing the Works. The Project Manager consults with the Client and the Contractor about the change.  
16.1 为减少业主就提供工程而向承建商支付的款额，承建商可向项目经理建议更改由业主提供的《范围》。项目经理须就该更改与业主及承建商进行磋商。  
16.2 Within four weeks of the Contractor making the proposal the Project Manager  
•accepts the Contractors proposal and issues an instruction changing the Scope,  
•informs the Contractor that the Client is considering the proposal and instructs the  
Contractor to submit a quotation for a proposed instruction to change the Scope or  
•informs the Contractor that the proposal is not accepted.  
The Project Manager may give any reason for not accepting the proposal.  
16.2 在承建商提出建议后四周内，项目经理须：  
• 接纳承建商的建议，并发出指示更改《范围》；  
• 通知承建商业主正在考虑该建议，并指示承建商就拟更改《范围》的指示提交报价；或  
• 通知承建商该建议不获接纳。  
项目经理可给出任何理由说明不接纳该建议。  
16.3 The Contractor may submit a proposal for adding an area to the Working Areas to the  
Project Manager for acceptance. A reason for not accepting is that the proposed area is  
•not necessary for Providing the Works or  
•used for work not in the contract.  
16.3 承建商可向项目经理提交建议，要求在《工作区域》中增加某一区域，以供项目经理接纳。不接纳的理由包括该建议区域：  
• 对提供工程并非必要；或  
• 用于合约范围以外的工作。  
Requirements for instructions 17  
17\. 对指示的要求  
17.1 The Project Manager or the Contractor notifies the other as soon as either becomes aware of an ambiguity or inconsistency in or between the documents which are part of the contract. The Project Manager states how the ambiguity or inconsistency should be resolved.  
17.1 项目经理或承建商一旦发现构成合约的文件之内或之间存有含糊或不一致之处，须立即通知对方。项目经理须说明该含糊或不一致之处应如何解决。  
17.2 The Project Manager or the Contractor notifies the other as soon as either becomes aware that the Scope includes an illegal or impossible requirement. If the Scope does include an illegal or impossible requirement, the Project Manager gives an instruction to change the Scope appropriately.  
17.2 项目经理或承建商一旦发现《范围》包含非法或不可能做到的要求，须立即通知对方。如果《范围》确实包含非法或不可能做到的要求，项目经理须发出指示，对《范围》作出适当更改。  
2\. THE CONTRACTOR’S MAIN RESPONSIBILITIES  
第2条 承建商的主要责任  
Providing the Works 20  
提供工程 20  
20.1 The Contractor Provides the Works in accordance with the Scope.  
20.1 承建商须按照《范围》提供工程。  
The Contractor’s design 21  
21\. 承建商的设计  
21.1 The Contractor designs the parts of the works which the Scope states the Contractor is to design.  
21.1 承建商须按照《范围》的规定，设计其中指明由承建商负责设计的工程部分。  
21.2 The Contractor submits the particulars of its design as the Scope requires to the Project Manager for acceptance. A reason for not accepting the Contractor’s design is that it does not comply with either the Scope or the applicable law.  
The Contractor does not proceed with the relevant work until the Project Manager has accepted its design.  
21.2 承建商须按《范围》的规定，将其设计的详细资料提交项目经理以待接纳。不接纳承建商设计的理由是该设计不符合《范围》或适用法律的规定。  
在项目经理接纳其设计之前，承建商不得进行相关工作。  
21.3 The Contractor may submit its design for acceptance in parts if the design of each part can be assessed fully.  
21.3 如果设计各部分均可被充分评估，承建商可将其设计分部分提交以待接纳。  
Using the Contractor’s design Design of Equipment 22  
22\. 使用承建商的设计 设备设计  
Subcontracting 26  
26．分包  
26.1 If the Contractor subcontracts work, it is responsible for Providing the Works as if it had not subcontracted. The contract applies as if a Tier Subcontractor’s employees and equipment were the Contractor’s.  
26.1 如果承建商将工程分包，其仍须负责提供本工程，如同未分包一样。本合约适用时，视层级分包商的雇员及设备为承建商的雇员及设备。  
26.2 The Contractor submits the name of each proposed Subcontractor to the Project Manager for acceptance. A reason for not accepting the Subcontractor is that the appointment will not allow the Contractor to Provide the Works. The Contractor does not appoint a proposed Subcontractor until the Project Manager has  
•accepted the Subcontractor and, to the extent these conditions of contract require,  
•accepted the subcontract documents.  
26.2 承建商将每位建议分包商的名称提交项目经理以待接纳。不接纳分包商的一个理由是，该任命将导致承建商无法提供本工程。承建商不得任命建议分包商，直至项目经理已：  
•接纳该分包商，并且（在本合约条件要求的范围内）  
•接纳该分包合约文件。  
26.3 The Contractor submits the proposed subcontract documents, except any pricing information, for each subcontract to the Project Manager for acceptance unless the Project Manager has agreed that no submission is required. A reason for not accepting the subcontract documents is that  
•their use will not allow the Contractor to Provide the Works or  
•they do not include a statement that the parties to the subcontract act in a spirit of mutual trust and co-operation.  
26.3 承建商将每份分包合约的建议分包合约文件（不包括任何定价信息）提交项目经理以待接纳，除非项目经理已同意无需提交。不接纳分包合约文件的理由是：  
•使用该等文件将导致承建商无法提供本工程，或  
•该等文件未包含一份声明，说明分包合约各方本着互信与合作的精神行事。  
Acceleration 36  
36 加速施工  
36.1 The Contractor and the Project Manager may propose to the other an acceleration to achieve Completion before the Completion Date. If the Project Manager and Contractor are prepared to consider the proposed change, the Project Manager instructs the Contractor to provide a quotation. The instruction states changes to the Key Dates to be included in the quotation. The Contractor provides a quotation within three weeks of the instruction to do so. The Project Manager replies to the quotation within three weeks. The reply is  
•a notification that the quotation is accepted or  
•a notification that the quotation is not accepted and that the Completion Date and Key Dates are not changed.  
36.1 承建商与项目经理可相互提议加速施工，以期在竣工日期前达成竣工。如果项目经理与承建商均准备考虑所提议的变更，项目经理须指示承建商提供报价。该指示须说明拟纳入报价的关键日期的变更。承建商须在收到指示后三周内提供报价。项目经理须在三周内对报价作出答复。答复须为：  
• 通知接纳该报价；或  
• 通知不接纳该报价，并说明竣工日期及关键日期维持不变。  
36.2 A quotation for an acceleration comprises proposed changes to the Prices and a revised programme showing the earlier Completion Date and the changed Key Dates. The Contractor submits details of the assessment with each quotation.  
36.2 加速施工报价须包括对价格提出的变更，以及一份显示提前的竣工日期及变更后的关键日期的经修订工程计划。承建商须随每份报价提交评估的详细资料。  
4\. QUALITY MANAGEMENT  
4\. 质量管理  
Quality management system 40  
40 质量管理制度  
40.1 The Contractor operates a quality management system which complies with the requirements stated in the Scope.  
40.1 承建商须运行符合工作范围所载要求的质量管理制度。  
40.2 Within the period stated in the Contract Data, the Contractor provides the Project Manager with a quality policy statement and a quality plan for acceptance. A reason for not accepting a quality policy statement or quality plan is that it does not allow the Contractor to Provide the Works.  
If any changes are made to the quality plan, the Contractor provides the Project Manager with the changed quality plan for acceptance.  
40.2 承建商须在合约资料所述的期限内，向项目经理提交质量方针声明书及质量计划以供接纳。不接纳质量方针声明书或质量计划的理由是：该声明书或计划不足以让承建商提供工程。  
如果对质量计划作出任何更改，承建商须向项目经理提交经更改的质量计划以供接纳。  
40.3 The Project Manager may instruct the Contractor to correct a failure to comply with the quality plan. This instruction is not a compensation event.  
40.3 项目经理可指示承建商纠正不符合质量计划的情况。该指示不构成补偿事件。  
Tests and inspections 41  
41 测试及检验  
41.1 This clause only applies to tests and inspections required by the Scope or the applicable law.  
41.1 本条款仅适用于工作范围或适用法律所要求的测试及检验。  
41.2 The Contractor and the Client provide materials, facilities and samples for tests and inspections as stated in the Scope.  
41.2 承建商与客户须按工作范围的规定，提供测试及检验所需的材料、设施及样本。  
41.3 The Contractor and the Supervisor informs the other of each of their tests and inspections before the test or inspection starts and afterwards informs the other of the results. The Contractor informs the Supervisor in time for a test or inspection to be arranged and done before doing work which would obstruct the test or inspection. The Supervisor may watch any test done by the Contractor.  
41.3 承建商与监理方须在各自的测试或检验开始前通知对方，并在其后将结果告知对方。承建商须及时通知监理方，以便在展开可能妨碍测试或检验的工作之前安排并完成该测试或检验。监理方可旁观承建商进行的任何测试。  
5\. PAYMENT  
第5条 付款  
Assessing the amount due 50  
50 评估应付金额  
50.1 The Project Manager assesses the amount due at each assessment date. The first assessment date is decided by the Project Manager to suit the procedures of the Parties and is not later than the assessment interval after the starting date. Later assessment dates occur at the end of each assessment interval until  
•the Supervisor issues the Defects Certificate or  
•the Project Manager issues a termination certificate.  
50.1 项目经理在每个估价日评估到期款项。首个估价日由项目经理根据双方的程序决定，且不得迟于开工日期后的一个估价间隔。后续估价日在每个估价间隔结束时发生，直至出现以下任一情况：  
•监理颁发缺陷证书，或  
•项目经理颁发终止证书。  
50.2 The Contractor submits an application for payment to the Project Manager two weeks before each assessment date setting out the amount the Contractor considers will be due at the assessment date. The Contractor’s application for payment includes details of how the amount has been assessed and is in the form stated in the Scope.  
In assessing the amount due, the Project Manager considers an application for payment submitted by the Contractor before the assessment date.  
50.2 承建商须在每个估价日两周前向项目经理提交付款申请，列明承建商认为在该估价日将到期支付的金额。承建商的付款申请须包含该金额的评估详情，并采用工程范围规定的格式。  
在评估到期款项时，项目经理须考虑承建商在估价日前提交的付款申请。  
Payment 51  
51款项支付  
51.1 The Project Manager certifies a payment within two weeks of each assessment date. The Project Manager’s certificate includes details of how the amount due has been assessed. The first payment is the amount due. other payments are the change in the amount due since the previous assessment. A payment is made by the Contractor to the Client if the change reduces the amount due. other payments are made by the Client to the Contractor. Payments are in the currency of the contract unless otherwise stated in the contract and are subject to the Client’s rights of set off in law or equity.  
51.1 项目经理在每个估价日期后两周内核证一笔付款。项目经理的证书须载明应付款额的估价详情。首笔付款即为应付款额。后续付款为自上次估价以来应付款额的变动额。如果该变动导致应付款额减少，则由承建商向客户付款；其他情况下，由客户向承建商付款。除非合约另有规定，付款均以合约货币进行，并受客户在法律或衡平法上享有的抵销权约束。  
51.2 Each certified payment is made within three weeks of the assessment date or, if a different period is stated in the Contract Data, within the period stated. If a certified payment is late, or if a payment is late because the Project Manager has not issued a certificate which should be issued, interest is paid on the late payment. Interest is assessed from the date by which the late payment should have been made until the date when the late payment is made, and is included in the first assessment after the late payment is made.  
51.2 每笔经核证的付款应在估价日期后三周内支付；如果合约资料中规定了不同的期限，则在该期限内支付。如果经核证的付款逾期，或因项目经理未签发应签发的证书而导致付款逾期，则须就逾期付款支付利息。利息自该笔付款应付之日起计算至实际支付之日止，并计入该笔逾期付款支付后的首次估价中。  
Contractor’s liabilities 81  
81 承建商的法律责任  
81.1 The following are Contractor’s liabilities unless they are stated as being Client’s liabilities.  
81.1 以下各项为承建商的法律责任，但被列为业主法律责任的除外。  
•Claims and proceedings from others and compensation and costs payable to others which arise from or in connection with the Contractor Providing the Works.  
•因承建商提供工程而引起或与之相关的他人索赔及法律程序，以及须向他人支付的补偿及费用。  
•Loss of or damage to the works, Plant and Materials and Equipment.  
•工程、装置、物料及设备的遗失或损毁。  
•Loss of or damage to property owned or occupied by the Client other than the works, which arises from or in connection with the Contractor Providing the Works.  
•因承建商提供工程而引起或与之相关的、业主拥有或占用的工程以外财产的遗失或损毁。
