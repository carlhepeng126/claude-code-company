近日，西北分公司安哥拉洛比托炼油厂一期工程项目部现场工程英语专题培训正式启动。

安哥拉以葡萄牙语为官方语言，英语是国际工程通用沟通语言。本次培训面向中方管理人员与安哥拉本地员工同步开展，旨在提升跨文化沟通与工程履约效率，为项目安全优质建设筑牢沟通基础。

培训坚持实战实用、因地制宜。前期开展英语水平测评，量身定制教学方案，以工程函件、技术资料、现场会话为核心内容，采用骨干授课、情景模拟、实景演练等方式，推动学用结合、即学即用。

项目部把英语培训常态化，以人才建设保障高质量履约，  为服务共建“一带一路”高质量发展，落实企业海外人才队伍建设部署  贡献力量。

  

Recently, a specialized on-site language training program was officially
launched for Chinese and Angolan employees at CNCEC-CC2’s Angola Lobito
Refinery Project (Phase I), aiming to strengthen cross-cultural communication
and improve project delivery efficiency. The training emphasizes practical
application, covering technical briefings, site coordination and business
communication. In addition to lectures, scenario simulations and on-site
drills help trainees to immediately apply what they have learned in their
daily work. CNCEC-CC2 actively fulfills its social responsibilities by
promoting localized employment, and fostering local talent through skills and
language training.
