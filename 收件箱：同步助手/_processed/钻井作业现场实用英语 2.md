**第3 部分：请将下列钻井作业指令翻译成汉语。**

Spud 开钻

Drill 26 inch hole down to 120 feet 钻26英寸井眼到120英尺

Circulate and clean hole 循环直到井眼干净

Perform wiper trip and spot Hi-Vis pill on bottom, drop Totco survey and pull out of hole.

短起钻，往井底打入高粘度处理剂

Conduct safety meeting 开安全会

R/U 20 inch conductor pipe and run the conductor to bottom.

组装20英寸套管，下套管到井底。

Set conductor on slip.

把套管座到卡瓦上。

**第4 部分：请给甲方作业经理（或钻井监督）写一封信（或电子邮件），表达以下内容。**

（1），眼下井下情况比较复杂，井漏经常发生。

（2），泥浆性能很不稳定，粘度太低。

（3），建议在泥浆中加入堵漏剂，适当提高钻井机械钻速。

To drilling operation manager or drilling supervisor:

We notice that hole has become more difficult to drill, well circulation lost can be come across very often. In our viewpoints, we believe that the major factor is coming from the mud properties. The mud properties are not stable, mud viscosity in particular, which is too low.

If possible, we suggest that plugging materials should be put into the mud, and at the same time, increasing the drilling rate to a proper level may be a good choice.

This is our consideration, which is just for your reference.

Hope to get the reply from you.

Rig manager
