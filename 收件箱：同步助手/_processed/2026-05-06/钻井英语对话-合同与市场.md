**1.上次中国与外国签订了什么合同?**  
What agreements were signed between China and other countries lasttime?  
根据一个新的五年合同,中国将供给泰国约为其年进口量10%的石油。  
China will supply about 10% of Thailand's annual crude oil importsunder terms of a new 5-year agreement .

石油现货市场价格升高,供应减缩。  
The oil spot market prices are high, and supplies are tightening.  
**3.什么是矿区使用税?**  
What are the Royalties?  
矿区使用税,就是用传统的方法,简单地以井口石油产量为准，以桶为单位收税。  
Royalties are a simple barrelage tax traditionally imposed on the oil'swellhead value .

west China.

**8.一口井能生产多长时间?**  
How long will a well produce?  
一口井能生产多少年没有一个确切的数字,每口井都不一样。  
There is no exact number of years for a well to produce . It varies con-siderably from well to well .  
**9.美国有多少天然气?**  
How much natural gas is there in the U.S.A?  
一九七六年一月一日美国探明的天然气储量为228万亿立方英尺。  
The proved reserves of natural gas in the United States on January 1,1976,were estimated to be 228 trillion cubic feet .  
**10.有多少外国石油公司打算在中国大陆架上进行地球物理勘探工作?**  
How many foreign oil companies are preparing to do geophysical survey on the continental shelf of China?  
八家石油公司(美国六家,英、法各一家)准备用12条以上的船只在中国未经勘探的广大沿海水域进行地球物理勘探。Eight oil company operators-six from the U. S.,one British andone French-are preparing to conduct geophysical group shoots withmore than a dozen vessels in the vast unexplored waters off the coastof China.
